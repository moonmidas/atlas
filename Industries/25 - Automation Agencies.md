# 25 - Automation Agencies

**Category:** Operations & Automation  
**Project Range:** $8K-$60K+  
**Timeline:** 2-8 weeks per engagement  
**Business Model:** Project fees + retainers

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Automation Agencies = Implementation and workflow optimization businesses. You identify manual processes that bleed money, build automated solutions, and maintain them. Recurring revenue from maintenance and optimization.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $3K-$12K (tools, initial clients) |
| Project Fees | $8K-$40K |
| Retainers | $2K-$8K/month |
| Gross Margin | 60-75% |
| Projects/Year | 8-20 |
| Annual Revenue | $150K-$500K+ |

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

**Hook:** "We find the processes costing you $50K+ per year and automate them in 30 days."

**Discovery Questions:**
- "What task takes up most of your team's Monday morning?"
- "Where do errors cost you the most money?"
- "What would break if you 3x'd next quarter?"

**Pricing:**
- Audit: $2K-$5K (credited toward project)
- Implementation: $8K-$40K
- Maintenance: $1.5K-$6K/month

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Clients wanting custom software (not automation)
- ❌ No budget for tools (expecting free solutions)
- ❌ No decision-maker access
- ❌ Unrealistic timelines
- ❌ Expectations of 100% automation (human-in-loop needed)
