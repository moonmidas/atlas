# Email Templates

**Category:** Digital Products  
**Sub-Category:** Communication and Conversion Assets  
**Entry Cost:** $300-$1,500  
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks to first sale  
**Revenue Model:** One-time purchase, niche packs, subscription updates

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Email templates productize proven copy structures for sales, onboarding, follow-up, retention, and newsletter workflows. Buyers are paying for speed, confidence, and higher response or conversion rates from messages they already need to send.

This category performs best when the templates are tied to a specific outcome and buyer context, not when they are sold as generic swipe files. Packaging matters: use-case sequencing, personalization instructions, and objection handling increase perceived value significantly.

**Market Size:** $50M-$150M across sales, marketing, and customer success workflows  
**Target:** Founders, agencies, sales teams, creators, operators  
**Examples:** Cold outreach packs, onboarding sequences, launch emails, win-back flows

---

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Stop rewriting the same email. Start sending sequences that already know what they need to do."

### Authority Builders
1. Free subject line banks by niche
2. Teardowns of underperforming email sequences
3. Lifecycle email maps
4. Before-and-after reply rate case studies

### Positioning Angles
- **Conversion systems:** Sell outcomes, not swipe files.
- **Niche-specific language:** Packaged by audience and use case.
- **Fast implementation:** Plug into existing sends quickly.

### Differentiators
- Sequence logic and timing guidance included
- Personalization prompts, not just canned copy
- Platform-friendly formatting and implementation notes

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Buyers expecting templates to replace product-market fit or a broken offer
- ❌ Teams wanting fully custom copywriting at template prices
- ❌ Customers with no list, no targeting, and no sending plan
- ❌ Buyers who only want to spam colder audiences harder
- ❌ Companies needing deep deliverability consulting inside the product price

**Best Prospects:**
- Small teams sending recurring campaigns
- Agencies standardizing client communication
- Sellers and founders running their own outreach

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"I can write emails myself."**
> "You can, but the question is whether you want to keep rewriting proven structures from scratch every week. The value is speed and consistency."

**"Templates will sound generic."**
> "Only if they are sold as fixed scripts. Good templates provide structure, positioning, and personalization cues so the final email still sounds like you."

**"Our audience is different."**
> "That is why segmentation and niche packs matter. The framework is reusable even when details change."

**"This is too much for copy templates."**
> "If one sequence improves replies, activation, or conversions even modestly, the economics usually justify the purchase very quickly."

**"We already have AI for writing."**
> "AI is strongest when it starts from a good framework. These templates provide the structure and intent that generic prompts usually miss."
