# 01 - Micro SaaS

**Category:** Software  
**Project Range:** $12K-$120K+  
**Timeline:** 2-6 months to MVP, 12-24 months to $10K MRR  
**Business Model:** Subscription ($29-$299/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Micro SaaS = Small software products that solve one specific, expensive workflow for a narrow audience. The goal is $10K-$50K MRR with 1-3 founders, minimal overhead, and high margins (80-90%).

### Why It Works
- One stubborn workflow × 500 customers × $50/month = $25K MRR
- Low acquisition costs through organic/industry channels
- High retention when workflow is mission-critical
- Sellable asset at 3-7x ARR

## 🎯 Validation Framework

### Week 1-2: Problem Validation
- Interview 20 potential customers
- Validate they spend $X hours/month on problem
- Confirm they pay $Y for current workaround
- Build waitlist (target: 100 emails)

### Week 3-4: Solution Validation
- Landing page with video/demo
- Pricing page with tiers
- Collect pre-orders or $1 deposits
- Target: 10+ commitments

### Month 2: MVP Development
- Build with 3 design partners (free)
- Weekly feedback loops
- Focus on core workflow only
- Ignore nice-to-haves

### Month 3: Launch
- Release to waitlist
- Goal: 10 paying customers
- Price: $29-$99/month
- Gather testimonials

### Month 4-6: Iterate to PMF
- Weekly customer calls
- Churn analysis
- Feature prioritization
- Raise prices as value proven

## 🚨 Red Flags (Universal)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Markets with <1,000 potential customers
- ❌ Buyers who can't describe current workaround
- ❌ Industries with 18+ month sales cycles
- ❌ Opportunities requiring $100K+ to validate
- ❌ No domain expertise or access
- ❌ Workflow happens <1x per month
- ❌ Commoditized solutions (10+ competitors)

---

## ✅ Success Patterns

**The Formula:**
1. Pick workflow you understand deeply
2. Target 1,000 customers willing to pay $50/month
3. Build in 2-3 months, launch to waitlist
4. Get to $10K MRR before hiring
5. Run lean until $30K MRR

**Pricing Strategy:**
- **Freemium:** No (attracts wrong customers)
- **Free Trial:** 14-30 days
- **Annual Prepay:** 2 months free (improves cash flow)
- **Enterprise:** Custom only after $50K MRR
