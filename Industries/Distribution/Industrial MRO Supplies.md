# Industrial MRO Distribution

**Category:** B2B Distribution  
**Sub-Category:** Industrial/Maintenance  
**Entry Cost:** $500K-$1.5M  
**Timeline:** 6-12 months to launch/acquire  
**Revenue Model:** Wholesale margin (20-35%)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Maintenance, repair, and operations (MRO) supplies for manufacturing plants. Fragmented market, relationship-driven, recurring revenue.

**Market Size:** $140B MRO market  
**Target:** Manufacturers, plants, facilities  
**Examples:** Grainger, McMaster-Carr, local distributors

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Maintenance manager or purchasing agent
**Secondary:** Plant managers, engineers
**Decision Maker:** Purchasing (with technical input)

**Budget:** $100K-$2M+ annually on MRO  
**Pain Urgency:** High (downtime is expensive)

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Stockout Costs ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Critical part unavailable, line down
**Current Solution:** Emergency orders, premium freight
**Cost of Pain:** $10K-$100K/hour downtime
**Your Solution:** Inventory management + local stock
**Pricing:** VMI (Vendor Managed Inventory)

---

### 2. Inventory Management
**Pain:** Too much inventory, or wrong inventory
**Current Solution:** Buyer manages (overwhelmed)
**Cost of Pain:** Working capital, stockouts
**Your Solution:** Vendor managed inventory (VMI)
**Pricing:** Included in service

---

### 3. Long Lead Times
**Pain:** 2-4 week delivery on critical items
**Current Solution:** Expedited freight ($$$), inventory
**Cost of Pain:** Working capital, delays
**Your Solution:** Same-day/next-day delivery
**Pricing:** 10-20% premium

---

### 4. Catalog Complexity
**Pain:** Thousands of SKUs to manage
**Current Solution:** Multiple suppliers, scattered info
**Cost of Pain:** Time, errors
**Your Solution:** Consolidated catalog + search
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Price Shopping
**Pain:** Don't know if prices are competitive
**Current Solution:** Quotes from multiple vendors
**Cost of Pain:** Time, fragmented purchasing
**Your Solution:** Competitive pricing guarantee
**Pricing:** Market rate

---

### 6. Technical Support
**Pain:** Need help selecting right part
**Current Solution:** Engineer time (expensive), guesswork
**Cost of Pain:** Wrong parts, returns, downtime
**Your Solution:** Technical sales support
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 7. Emergency Service
**Pain:** After-hours emergencies
**Current Solution:** On-call vendor (limited), stock
**Cost of Pain:** Downtime, expedited freight
**Your Solution:** 24/7 emergency service
**Pricing:** Premium service tier

---

### 8. Purchase Order Complexity
**Pain:** Multiple POs, approvals, paperwork
**Current Solution:** Manual process
**Cost of Pain:** Admin time, delays
**Your Solution:** Punchout catalog, consolidated billing
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 9. Compliance Documentation
**Pain:** Need certs, MSDS, traceability
**Current Solution:** Request from suppliers (slow)
**Cost of Pain:** Audit failures, delays
**Your Solution:** Digital documentation
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 10. Obsolete Parts
**Pain:** Equipment old, parts obsolete
**Current Solution:** Reverse engineering, stocking
**Cost of Pain:** Downtime, expensive solutions
**Your Solution:** Obsolete part sourcing
**Pricing:** Premium + sourcing fee

---

### 11. Cost Allocation
**Pain:** Hard to track MRO spend by department
**Current Solution:** Manual allocation
**Cost of Pain:** Budget overruns, no visibility
**Your Solution:** Spend tracking by cost center
**Pricing:** Reporting included

---

### 12. Safety Stock Planning
**Pain:** Don't know how much to stock
**Current Solution:** Rules of thumb
**Cost of Pain:** Overstock, stockouts
**Your Solution:** Data-driven safety stock
**Pricing:** VMI service

---

### 13. Product Substitutions
**Pain:** Preferred brand unavailable
**Current Solution:** Research alternatives (time)
**Cost of Pain:** Delays, compatibility risk
**Your Solution:** Approved substitutions
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 14. Kitting Services
**Pain:** Ordering multiple parts for projects
**Current Solution:** Multiple POs, receiving
**Cost of Pain:** Time, coordination
**Your Solution:** Pre-kitting
**Pricing:** +5-10% for kitting

---

### 15. E-commerce Expectation
**Pain:** Want Amazon-like experience
**Current Solution:** Phone/fax orders (archaic)
**Cost of Pain:** Frustration, inefficiency
**Your Solution:** Modern e-commerce platform
**Pricing:** Included

- ❌ High customer concentration
- ❌ No technical capability

- Manufacturing plants
- Local/regional focus
- Technical expertise
- Inventory services
