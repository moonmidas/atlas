# No-Code Startup Bootcamp

**Category:** Cohort Courses  
**Sub-Category:** Entrepreneurship/Technology  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 6-12 weeks  
**Revenue Model:** $1,500-$3,500 per student

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Intensive bootcamp teaching non-technical founders to build MVPs using no-code tools. Launch business in 30-60 days without developers.

**Market Size:** 10M+ aspiring entrepreneurs  
**Target:** Non-technical founders, corporate escapees  
**Examples:** No-Code MBA, Build in Public, Zeroqode Labs

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Aspiring founder with idea but no tech skills
**Secondary:** Corporate employees wanting side business
**Demographics:** 25-45 years old, $75K+ income

**Budget:** $1,500-$3,500  
**Pain Urgency:** High (want to start NOW)

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ No idea (not ready)
- ❌ Expecting $1M business (unrealistic)
- ❌ Not willing to put in 10+ hours/week
- ❌ Complex technical requirements

**Best Prospects:**
- Clear idea or problem to solve
- 10+ hours/week to dedicate
- Comfortable with tools/learning
- Willing to launch imperfect MVP
