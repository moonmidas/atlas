# 24 - Licensing Agencies

**Category:** Asset-Light  
**Project Range:** $8K-$55K+  
**Timeline:** 3-12 months to first deal  
**Business Model:** Commission (10-30% of licensing deals)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Licensing Agencies = Broker and structure revenue-sharing partnerships between creators, brands, and channels. You don't own IP—you connect IP owners with commercialization opportunities.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $8K-$25K (legal, CRM, initial outreach) |
| Commission | 10-30% of deal value |
| Deal Size | $10K-$1M+ |
| Deals per Year | 5-20 |
| Gross Margin | 85-95% |
| Annual Revenue | $100K-$500K+ |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Network:**
- Deep relationships with creators
- Access to decision-makers at brands
- Industry reputation

**Value-add:**
- Deal structuring expertise
- Contract negotiation
- Ongoing relationship management

**Focus:**
- Niche specialization (one vertical)
- Quality over quantity
- Long-term relationships

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ One-hit wonder (single deal dependency)
- ❌ Commoditized service (pure matching)
- ❌ Legal/contract disputes
- ❌ Cash flow issues (deals take months to close)
- ❌ No exclusivity (creators go direct)
