# 22 - Franchises

**Category:** Asset-Light  
**Project Range:** $25K-$120K+  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months to first franchisee  
**Business Model:** Franchise fees + ongoing royalties

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Franchises = Replicable operating systems distributed through operators with local market skin in the game. You create the playbook, franchisees put up capital and operate.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $100K-$500K (pilot, legal, support systems) |
| Franchise Fee | $25K-$50K per location |
| Royalty | 4-8% of gross revenue |
| Marketing Fee | 1-3% of gross revenue |
| Break-even | 10-30 franchisees |
| Annual Revenue | $500K-$5M+ at scale |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Proven Model:**
- 3+ company-owned locations
- Unit economics proven
- Systems documented

**Franchisee Support:**
- Training (initial + ongoing)
- Marketing support
- Technology platform
- Operations guidance

**Legal/Compliance:**
- FDD (Franchise Disclosure Document)
- Trademark protection
- Territory definitions

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Unproven concept (<2 years, <3 locations)
- ❌ Poor unit economics (franchisees don't make money)
- ❌ Legal issues (lawsuits from franchisees)
- ❌ High turnover (franchisees leaving)
- ❌ Saturated markets
