# 16 - B2B Distribution

**Category:** Commerce  
**Project Range:** $20K-$110K+  
**Timeline:** 6-18 months to scale  
**Business Model:** Buy wholesale, sell retail + value-add services

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

B2B Distribution = Old-school sales and sourcing categories with pricing opacity and service gaps. You aggregate supply, add technology/services, and capture margin.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $100K-$500K (inventory, sales team) |
| Revenue | $1M-$10M+ |
| Gross Margin | 20-35% |
| Net Margin | 5-15% |
| Inventory Turns | 6-12x per year |
| Payback Period | 12-24 months |

- Mobile ordering

- Custom solutions

**Logistics:**
- Same-day delivery
- Will-call
- Vendor managed inventory

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Commoditized products (pure price play)
- ❌ Amazon competition (can they fulfill?)
- ❌ High customer concentration (>30%)
- ❌ Declining end markets
- ❌ Poor inventory management
