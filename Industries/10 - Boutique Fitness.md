# 10 - Boutique Fitness

**Category:** Local  
**Project Range:** $6K-$35K+  
**Timeline:** 3-12 months to launch  
**Business Model:** Membership subscriptions ($100-$300/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Boutique Fitness = Community-driven membership models with pricing and retention upside. Unlike big box gyms ($30/month), boutiques charge $150-$300/month for specialized experiences. High margins when done right.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $100K-$500K (equipment, buildout) |
| Monthly Revenue Target | $25K-$75K |
| Members Needed | 150-400 |
| Price Point | $150-$300/month |
| Gross Margin | 60-75% |
| Break-even | 6-12 months |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Location:**
- High-income demographics
- Visibility/foot traffic
- Parking availability

**Programming:**
- Consistent class schedule
- Variety of offerings
- Community events

**Retention:**
- Onboarding sequences
- Milestone recognition
- Referral incentives

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Oversaturated market (3+ competitors within 2 miles)
- ❌ Low-income demographics
- ❌ Poor parking/visibility
- ❌ High instructor turnover
- ❌ Groupon dependency
