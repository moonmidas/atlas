# Design/Creative Newsletter

**Category:** Newsletter Media  
**Sub-Category:** Design/Creative  
**Entry Cost:** $2K-$6K  
**Timeline:** 6-12 months  
**Revenue Model:** Free + sponsors + templates

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Design trends, tools, and portfolio advice for designers. Monetize via design tool sponsors and template sales.

**Market Size:** 5M+ professional designers  
**Target:** Graphic designers, UX designers, creatives  
**Examples:** Dense Discovery, Sidebar, Fonts In Use

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Designer (freelance or in-house)  
**Secondary:** Design students, creative directors  
**Budget:** Free; tool sponsors pay $2K-$8K
