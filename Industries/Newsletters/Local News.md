# Local News Newsletter

**Category:** Newsletter Media  
**Sub-Category:** Local/Community  
**Entry Cost:** $1K-$5K  
**Timeline:** 3-9 months  
**Revenue Model:** Sponsorships + memberships ($5-$15/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

City-specific news (parking, development, schools, business) for engaged local readers. Local business sponsorships.

**Market Size:** 300M+ Americans in local markets  
**Target:** Engaged locals, local business owners  
**Examples:** 6AM City, The Charlotte Ledger, Axios Local

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Local resident (engaged citizen)  
**Secondary:** Local business owners  
**Budget:** Free-$15/month; businesses $500-$5K/year

---
