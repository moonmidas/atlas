## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Stop scrolling Twitter for 4 hours a day. Get the alpha in 10 minutes."

### Authority Builders
1. Trade performance tracker (show your calls were right)
2. Airdrop success rate (how many readers qualified)
3. Protocol deep dives (technical but accessible)
4. Portfolio tracking (show your own trades/returns)

### Positioning Angles
- **For degens:** "The alpha you're missing"
- **For normies:** "Crypto explained like you're 5"
- **For pros:** "On-chain analysis you can't get elsewhere"

### Trust Signals
- Transparent portfolio (show you're in the trenches)
- Track record of calls (admit when wrong)
- No paid shills (disclose all relationships)

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Unregistered securities advice (SEC risk)
- ❌ Guaranteed returns (scam territory)
- ❌ Anonymous (no accountability)
- ❌ Shilling low-cap coins (pump and dump vibes)
- ❌ No bear market track record

**Best Positioning:**
- Educational focus over financial advice
- Transparent about conflicts
- Risk management emphasis
- Long-term perspective
