# 20 - Expert Memberships

**Category:** Education  
**Project Range:** $6K-$45K+  
**Timeline:** 1-3 months to launch  
**Business Model:** Monthly/annual subscriptions ($50-$500/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Expert Memberships = Ongoing access models where clients pay for answers, accountability, and advantage. Not courses—ongoing relationship and continuous value.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $3K-$12K (platform, initial members) |
| Monthly Price | $50-$500 |
| Annual Price | $500-$5,000 |
| Members | 50-500 |
| Retention | 75-90% annually |
| Gross Margin | 85-95% |
| Annual Revenue | $50K-$500K |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Access:**
- Regular office hours
- Quick response times
- Direct communication channels

**Value:**
- Exclusive insights
- Early access
- Member-only resources

**Community:**
- Peer connections
- Accountability
- Recognition

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Expert too busy to engage
- ❌ No clear value proposition
- ❌ High churn (>25% monthly)
- ❌ Member complaints about access
- ❌ No community engagement
