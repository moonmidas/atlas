# 17 - Marketplaces

**Category:** Commerce  
**Project Range:** $20K-$100K+  
**Timeline:** 12-36 months to liquidity  
**Business Model:** Transaction fees + premium features

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Marketplaces = Supply-demand businesses where liquidity and trust are the core product. Winner-take-most dynamics, network effects, and high defensibility once scaled.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $50K-$200K (platform, initial liquidity) |
| Take Rate | 10-30% of GMV |
| GMV to Revenue | 10-20% |
| Break-even | Often 24-48 months |
| Network Effects | Strong once critical mass achieved |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Liquidity:**
- Solve chicken-and-egg (start with supply or demand)
- Concentrate geographically or vertically
- Manual matching before automation

**Trust:**
- Reviews and ratings
- Identity verification
- Insurance/guarantees

**Value-add:**
- Don't just connect—facilitate transaction
- Payment processing
- Dispute resolution

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Low transaction frequency (<1x per year)
- ❌ Low transaction value (<$100)
- ❌ High disintermediation risk
- ❌ Strong incumbent with liquidity
- ❌ Network effects not present
