## 🎯 The Opportunity

Fractional FP&A services provide strategic financial planning and analysis to companies that have outgrown basic accounting but aren't ready for a full-time FP&A hire. These businesses typically have $3M-$30M in revenue and are facing budgeting challenges, forecasting difficulties, or analytical gaps. They need financial models, budget frameworks, variance analysis, and strategic insights—but can't justify $100K-$160K for a full-time hire.

A Fractional FP&A professional works 1-3 days per week, building the analytical infrastructure that helps leadership make better financial decisions.

**Market Size:** 500,000+ companies needing FP&A support  
**Target:** CEOs, CFOs, and finance leaders of growth-stage companies  
**Examples:** FP&A consultancies, CFO services firms, individual practitioners

## 💬 Objection Handling

**Do not we just need better accounting?**
> Accounting tells you where you have been—historical financial reporting. FP&A helps you navigate where you are going—planning, forecasting, and analysis. Both are necessary. Many of my clients have strong accounting and add FP&A for forward-looking insight. You need both a rearview mirror and a windshield.

**How is this different from having a CFO?**
> A full-time CFO provides strategic financial leadership across all areas. FP&A is a specific function focused on planning and analysis. Many companies start with fractional FP&A and add CFO services as they grow. I can also work alongside your existing CFO to provide specialized FP&A support.

**What if we already have a financial model?**
> Many companies have financial models but they are outdated, broken, or not used for decision-making. I can audit your existing model, fix issues, and ensure it becomes a living tool for planning. Or if your model is solid, I can focus on other FP&A needs like budgeting, forecasting, or KPI development.

**How do we measure FP&A success?**
> Success metrics include: forecast accuracy improvement, budget variance reduction, time spent on planning, decision quality, and executive confidence in financial decisions. We establish baselines in month 1 and track improvements. Most clients see significant improvements in planning efficiency within 90 days.

**Can you handle our industry-specific needs?**
> FP&A fundamentals translate across industries, though metrics and drivers vary. I have experience with SaaS, professional services, e-commerce, and manufacturing. Let us discuss your specific industry to ensure I can address your unique planning and analysis needs.
