# 06 - Fractional Ops

**Category:** Service  
**Project Range:** $10K-$55K+  
**Timeline:** 3-6 month engagements  
**Business Model:** Monthly retainer ($5K-$15K/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Fractional Ops = Embedded operators for businesses between chaos and full-time executive hires. You provide C-level or VP-level operations leadership on a part-time basis. The leverage comes from templates, playbooks, and repeatable systems you deploy across clients.

### Why It Works
- Companies $2M-$20M need ops leadership but can't afford $200K+ full-time
- Full-time hires take 6+ months to ramp up
- Fractional = best practices from 10+ companies applied to yours
- High leverage: 4-6 clients = $300K-$600K annual income

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Get [C-level] expertise without the [C-level] price tag. I help [client type] [outcome] in [timeframe]."

### Validation Path
1. **Month 1:** Define niche, build case studies from past experience
2. **Month 2:** Launch with content: "5 signs you need a fractional [role]"
3. **Month 3:** Land first 2 clients at lower rate ($5K/month)
4. **Month 4-6:** Scale to 4-6 clients, raise rates
5. **Month 6-12:** Optimize for best-fit clients, raise to $10K+/month

### Client Selection Criteria
- **Revenue:** $2M-$30M (sweet spot)
- **Stage:** Post-product-market fit, pre-corporate structure
- **Pain:** Acknowledged operational gap
- **Budget:** Can afford $8K-$15K/month
- **Culture:** Values expertise over headcount

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Pre-revenue or <$1M (can't afford you)
- ❌ Founders who want you to "just fix it" (no engagement)
- ❌ Clients with 3+ fractional execs (too much change)
- ❌ Companies in decline (you can't save them)
- ❌ Clients who negotiate hard on price (value mismatch)

---

## 💡 Success Patterns

**The Formula:**
1. 10+ years experience in target function
2. Pick one role (COO, CFO, etc.)—don't be generalist
3. Build playbook library (reusable across clients)
4. Target 4-6 clients simultaneously
5. Stay 12-18 months, then transition or repeat

**Rate Progression:**
- Month 1-3: $5K-$7K/month (building track record)
- Month 4-12: $8K-$12K/month
- Year 2+: $12K-$20K/month (premium positioning)
