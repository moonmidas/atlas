# 15 - Niche E-Commerce

**Category:** Commerce  
**Project Range:** $8K-$65K+  
**Timeline:** 3-9 months to profitability  
**Business Model:** Product sales + subscription

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Niche E-Commerce = Focused brands with tighter storytelling, merchandising, and repeat purchase loops. Not dropshipping—real brands with differentiated products, content, and community.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $15K-$50K (inventory, ads, build) |
| Monthly Revenue | $20K-$200K+ |
| Gross Margin | 50-70% |
| Marketing % | 20-35% of revenue |
| Payback Period | 6-18 months |
| LTV:CAC | 3:1 minimum |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Product:**
- Differentiated (not commodity)
- Solves specific problem
- Quality justifies price

**Marketing:**
- Content-driven acquisition
- Community building
- Retention focus

**Operations:**
- Inventory management
- 3PL for fulfillment
- Customer service excellence

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Saturated market (50+ competitors)
- ❌ Low margins (<40% gross)
- ❌ High CAC (>50% of LTV)
- ❌ Commoditized product
- ❌ No repeat purchase potential
