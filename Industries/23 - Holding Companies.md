# 23 - Holding Companies

**Category:** Asset-Light  
**Project Range:** $30K-$120K+  
**Timeline:** 18-48 months to platform  
**Business Model:** Buy + improve + compound

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Holding Companies = Portfolio models that buy, improve, and compound durable cash-flow businesses. Private equity for the masses, with operational excellence as the edge.

## 🎯 Value Creation Playbook

**Operational:**
- Centralize back office (accounting, HR, IT)
- Implement best practices across portfolio
- Technology enablement
- Pricing optimization

**Strategic:**
- Cross-selling between portfolio companies
- Geographic expansion
- Service line additions
- Professional management

**Financial:**
- Multiple arbitrage (buy low, sell high)
- Leverage at holding company level
- Working capital optimization

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Owner-dependent businesses
- ❌ Declining industries
- ❌ Poor financial record-keeping
- ❌ Key person risk
- ❌ Customer concentration
