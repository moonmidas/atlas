# 09 - Home Services Rollups

**Category:** Local  
**Project Range:** $20K-$95K+  
**Timeline:** 6-18 month acquisitions  
**Business Model:** Acquire + improve + hold/sell

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Home Services Rollups = Buy boring local businesses (HVAC, plumbing, electrical), apply operational excellence, and build regional platforms. Low competition, high fragmentation, recession-resistant.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $200K-$1M (first acquisition + working capital) |
| Target EBITDA Multiple | 2-4x |
| Platform Value | 6-10x EBITDA |
| Revenue per Location | $1M-$5M |
| EBITDA Margin | 15-25% |

## 🎯 Value Creation Playbook

**Operational Improvements:**
1. Shared services (accounting, HR, marketing)
2. Pricing optimization (20-30% increases common)
3. Technology (scheduling, CRM, field service)
4. Maintenance agreements (recurring revenue)
5. Cross-selling between services

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Owner-dependent businesses (no systems)
- ❌ Customer concentration >20%
- ❌ Declining revenue trends
- ❌ Poor reputation/reviews
- ❌ Key employees leaving post-close
