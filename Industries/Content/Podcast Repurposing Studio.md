# Podcast Repurposing Studio

**Category:** Content Studios  
**Sub-Category:** Content Repurposing  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 1-2 months to launch  
**Revenue Model:** Monthly retainer based on output volume

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Podcast Repurposing Studios transform long-form podcast episodes into dozens of pieces of short-form content optimized for different platforms. The studio extracts clips, creates audiograms, writes blog posts, generates social content, and produces email newsletters—all from a single podcast episode.

Most podcasters create amazing content that reaches only a fraction of its potential audience. A 60-minute podcast could become 20+ short clips, 5 blog posts, 50 social graphics, and an email newsletter—but most creators lack the time or systems to execute this. A specialized repurposing studio can extract maximum value from existing content while capturing recurring revenue from busy podcasters.

**Market Size:** $4B podcast market with 80% of content going underutilized  
**Target:** Existing podcasters, busy executives with shows, B2B companies podcasting  
**Examples:** Repurpose.io (software), Castmagic (AI), Content Allies (service)

**Avoid:**

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"AI tools can do this now"**
> "AI is great for initial processing, but it misses context, brand voice, and strategic moments. Our hybrid approach uses AI for efficiency and humans for quality judgment. Clients who tried pure AI came back because the engagement difference was 3-4x. Want to see a comparison?"

**"That's expensive for 'just repurposing'"**
> "Most podcasters spend 15-20 hours per episode on DIY repurposing or $6K+ on multiple contractors. We deliver consistent quality with a dedicated repurposer who knows your show. What's your current total repurposing cost including time?"

**"We don't have time to review all that content"**
> "We include an approval workflow where you review key pieces and we handle the rest based on your brand guidelines. Most clients spend 30 minutes per episode vs 15+ hours doing it themselves. How much time do you currently spend?"

**"How do we know this drives results?"**
> "We track podcast download attribution from repurposed content, engagement rates by format, and audience growth across platforms. Most clients see 40-60% increase in podcast discovery within 90 days. What's your current cross-platform reach?"

**"We tried repurposing before and it didn't work"**
> "Most DIY repurposing fails due to inconsistent execution and poor platform optimization. We bring systems, expertise, and guaranteed output. The difference between amateur and professional repurposing is night and day. What challenges did you face?"

**"Can we start with just clips?"**
> "Absolutely—we offer modular services starting at $1.5K for clip extraction and audiograms. Many clients start there and expand once they see the value. What format is your highest priority?"
