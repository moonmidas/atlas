# Course Production Studio

**Category:** Content Studios  
**Sub-Category:** Educational Content  
**Entry Cost:** $20K-$50K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months per course  
**Revenue Model:** Per-course project fees + revenue share options

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Course Production Studios provide end-to-end online course creation services for experts, coaches, and companies who want to monetize their knowledge. The studio handles curriculum design, filming, editing, platform setup, workbook creation, and launch strategy—delivering a professional course without the expert needing to become a videographer or instructional designer.

The online course market exceeds $350B globally with 15%+ annual growth. Experts everywhere have valuable knowledge but lack the production capabilities to transform it into marketable courses. Meanwhile, consumers demand increasingly high production quality. A specialized studio that delivers Netflix-quality educational content can capture premium project fees while helping clients build significant revenue streams.

**Market Size:** $350B+ global e-learning market  
**Target:** Coaches, consultants, industry experts, corporate training teams  
**Examples:** MasterClass (production quality), Teachable (platform), CXL (B2B courses)

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Course Creation Overwhelm ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Experts have the knowledge but the technical production process—filming, editing, platform setup—is paralyzing
**Current Solution:** Attempting DIY with webcam, getting stuck, course never launches
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$500K+ in missed course revenue, competitor market capture, personal brand stagnation
**Your Solution:** Turnkey course production from outline to launch, expert only needs to show up and teach
**Pricing:** $25K for standard 6-module course, $50K+ for premium production

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How long have you been planning to create this course?"
- "What's prevented you from launching so far?"
- "What would it cost you in lost revenue to delay another 6 months?"

---

### 2. Curriculum Design and Structure
**Pain:** Knowledge is scattered and unstructured—don't know how to organize into a logical learning journey
**Current Solution:** Brain dump of topics, no learning objectives or progression strategy
**Cost of Pain:** Confusing student experience, high refund rates, poor reviews
**Your Solution:** Instructional design expertise, learning outcome mapping, module structure optimization
**Pricing:** $3K-$5K curriculum design package

**Discovery Questions:**
- "Do you have your course outlined with learning objectives?"
- "How would you organize your knowledge into modules?"
- "Have you validated what students actually need to learn?"

---

### 3. Professional Filming Quality
**Pain:** DIY videos look amateur compared to MasterClass and other high-end courses, hurting perceived value
**Current Solution:** Webcam recording, poor lighting, echoey audio, shaky camera
**Cost of Pain:** Low conversion rates, high refunds, brand credibility damage, can't charge premium prices
**Your Solution:** Multi-camera filming, professional lighting, broadcast-quality audio, teleprompter support
**Pricing:** Included in project fee

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How are you planning to film your course?"
- "How does your production quality compare to competitors?"
- "What production quality do your target students expect?"

---

### 4. Editing and Post-Production Bottleneck
**Pain:** Raw footage sits for months because editing is technical and time-consuming
**Current Solution:** Hiring cheap editors with inconsistent quality, or never finishing
**Cost of Pain:** Delayed launch, wasted filming investment, lost market timing
**Your Solution:** Professional editing with graphics, B-roll, animations, and quick turnaround
**Pricing:** Included in project fee

**Discovery Questions:**
- "Do you have experience with video editing?"
- "How long would editing take you or your team?"
- "What happens if launch gets delayed by 3 months?"

---

### 5. Platform Selection and Setup
**Pain:** Dozens of course platforms exist—Kajabi, Teachable, Thinkific, Udemy—each with different tradeoffs
**Current Solution:** Analysis paralysis, wrong platform choice, manual workarounds
**Cost of Pain:** Technical debt, limited functionality, student experience issues, migration costs
**Your Solution:** Platform recommendation based on needs, full setup and configuration, integration
**Pricing:** $2K-$3K platform setup

**Discovery Questions:**

- "Do you need membership features, community, or just course hosting?"

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"That's expensive compared to doing it myself"**
> "DIY course creation typically takes 6-12 months and still looks amateur. Most experts never finish. We deliver in 60-90 days with professional quality that commands 2-3x higher prices. What's the cost of waiting 6 months to launch?"

**"What if the course doesn't sell?"**
> "We include launch strategy and can offer revenue share pricing where we win when you win. However, course success depends on your expertise and audience. We recommend validating demand before production. Do you have proof people want this course?"

**"Can I just use a course platform's templates?"**
> "Platform templates are fine for basic courses, but they look like everyone else's. Our production quality differentiates you in a crowded market and justifies premium pricing. MasterClass didn't become successful with webcam videos. What's your competitive advantage?"

**"I need to launch faster than 90 days"**
> "Rushed courses typically fail. We can expedite to 60 days with rush fees, but quality suffers. Better to launch right than launch fast. When do you actually need revenue from this course?"

**"What about ongoing updates?"**
> "We offer update packages and can train your team on basic updates. Many clients return for major revisions annually. How often does your industry content change?"

**"Do you guarantee course success?"**
> "We guarantee professional production quality and timely delivery. Course sales depend on your marketing, audience, and market fit. We set you up for success but can't control the market. What's your marketing plan post-launch?"
