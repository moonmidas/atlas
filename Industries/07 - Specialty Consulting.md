# 07 - Specialty Consulting

**Category:** Service  
**Project Range:** $15K-$80K+  
**Timeline:** 4-12 week engagements  
**Business Model:** Project fees + value-based pricing

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Specialty Consulting = Narrow expertise with explicit ROI and fast implementation. Unlike general consulting, you own one specific problem and solve it repeatedly. The leverage comes from frameworks, diagnostic tools, and outcome-based pricing.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $3K-$10K (certifications, tools) |
| Project Fees | $20K-$75K |
| Gross Margin | 70-85% |
| Projects/Year | 6-12 |
| Annual Target | $250K-$600K |

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

**Hook:** "I help [specific client] solve [specific problem] in [timeframe], delivering [specific ROI]."

**Validation:** 
1. Define 3-5 case studies from past work
2. Create diagnostic tool (free assessment)
3. Publish frameworks on LinkedIn
4. Land 3 clients through referrals
5. Build reputation in niche

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Clients who want "general business advice"
- ❌ No decision-making authority
- ❌ Can't articulate the problem quantitatively
- ❌ Expectations of overnight transformation
- ❌ Price shopping against generalist consultants
