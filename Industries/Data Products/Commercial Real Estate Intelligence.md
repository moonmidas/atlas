# Commercial Real Estate Intelligence

**Category:** Data Products  
**Sub-Category:** Real Estate/PropTech  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months to MVP  
**Revenue Model:** Subscription ($500-$2,000/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Aggregated data on rent comps, lease terms, tenant movements, and development pipeline for commercial real estate. Brokers and investors work in information darkness—expensive tools like CoStar ($5K+/month) dominate, leaving room for niche players.

**Market Size:** $16B CRE data market  
**Target:** Commercial brokers, investors, landlords  
**Examples:** Reonomy, CompStak, VTS

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Commercial real estate broker or analyst  
**Secondary:** Investors, landlords, developers  
**Decision Maker:** Managing director or investment committee

**Budget:** $500-$2,000/month per seat  
**Pain Urgency:** High (information = deal flow)

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Rent Comp Accuracy ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Public lease data is stale, actual comps hard to get
**Current Solution:** CoStar ($5K-$20K/year), networking, guesswork
**Cost of Pain:** Mispriced deals, lost opportunities
**Your Solution:** Real-time lease comp database
**Pricing:** $800-$1,500/month

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"CoStar costs $15K/year and tells you what happened last quarter. We tell you what's happening now—for $1,000/month."

### Authority Builders
1. Quarterly market reports (free)
2. Rent forecast accuracy tracker
3. Deal flow case studies
4. Broker interview series

### Positioning Angles
- Speed: "Real-time vs. quarterly"
- Niche: "Focus on [industrial/medical office/retail]"
- Cost: "80% cheaper than CoStar"

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Secondary markets with no transaction volume
- ❌ Buyers who only use free resources
- ❌ Markets dominated by one player
- ❌ No data sources available

**Best Prospects:**
- Secondary/tertiary markets CoStar ignores
- Niche property types (medical office, self-storage)
- Growing markets (Austin, Nashville, etc.)

---

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"We already have CoStar"**
