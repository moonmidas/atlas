# Mental Health Practice Tools

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Healthcare/Mental Health  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$35K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $89-$199/month per provider

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Telehealth, session notes, and client management for therapists, psychologists, and counselors.

**Market Size:** 200K+ mental health providers  
**Target:** Solo to small group practices

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Session Documentation ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Notes take hours, behind on paperwork
**Current Solution:** EHRs (clunky), paper
**Cost of Pain:** Burnout, compliance risk
**Your Solution:** Streamlined note templates
**Pricing:** $89-$149/month

---

### 2. Telehealth Integration
**Pain:** HIPAA-compliant video
**Current Solution:** Zoom (not HIPAA), Doxy.me (expensive)
**Cost of Pain:** Compliance risk, cost
**Your Solution:** Built-in telehealth
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 3. Scheduling Flexibility
**Pain:** Complex scheduling (variable sessions)
**Current Solution:** Generic schedulers (don't fit)
**Cost of Pain:** Phone tag, gaps
**Your Solution:** Therapy-specific scheduling
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 4. Client Portal
**Pain:** Secure messaging, forms
**Current Solution:** Email (not secure), paper
**Cost of Pain:** HIPAA concerns
**Your Solution:** Secure client portal
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Insurance Billing
**Pain:** Mental health billing complexity
**Current Solution:** Billers ($$$), DIY
**Cost of Pain:** Claim denials, delays
**Your Solution:** Automated billing
**Pricing:** $100-$200/month premium

---

### 6. Treatment Planning
**Pain:** Creating measurable plans
**Current Solution:** Generic templates
**Cost of Pain:** Audit failures
**Your Solution:** Goal tracking, outcomes
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month

---

### 7. No-Show Management
**Pain:** Therapy no-shows (no insurance cover)
**Current Solution:** Phone reminders
**Cost of Pain:** Lost income
**Your Solution:** Automated reminders + waitlist
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 8. Outcome Measurement
**Pain:** Tracking client progress
**Current Solution:** Memory, subjective
**Cost of Pain:** No data, no improvement
**Your Solution:** Outcome measures (PHQ-9, etc.)
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 9. Group Therapy
**Pain:** Managing group sessions
**Current Solution:** Multiple individual slots
**Cost of Pain:** Administrative mess
**Your Solution:** Group management
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 10. Supervision Tracking
**Pain:** Tracking hours for licensure
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets
**Cost of Pain:** Lapsed licenses
**Your Solution:** Supervision logs
**Pricing:** $20-$40/month add-on

---

### 11. Crisis Management
**Pain:** Client crisis protocols
**Current Solution:** Hope it doesn't happen
**Cost of Pain:** Liability
**Your Solution:** Crisis workflows
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 12. Intake Automation
**Pain:** Lengthy intake process
**Current Solution:** First session consumed by intake
**Cost of Pain:** Lost therapy time
**Your Solution:** Pre-session digital intake
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month

---

### 13. Prescription Management
**Pain:** Coordinating with prescribers
**Current Solution:** Fax, phone tag
**Cost of Pain:** Medication errors
**Your Solution:** Care coordination
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month premium

---

### 14. Self-Pay Clients
**Pain:** Sliding scale, packages
**Current Solution:** Manual tracking
**Cost of Pain:** Billing confusion
**Your Solution:** Flexible payment options
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 15. Practice Analytics
**Pain:** Don't know practice health
**Current Solution:** Gut feeling
**Cost of Pain:** No growth strategy
**Your Solution:** Practice dashboards
**Pricing:** Included
