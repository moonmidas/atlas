# Gym Membership Management

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Fitness/Gyms  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$299/month per gym

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Check-in, billing, and member management for boutique fitness studios and independent gyms.

**Market Size:** 100K+ fitness studios  
**Target:** Boutique studios, CrossFit boxes, yoga studios

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Check-in/Attendance ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Tracking who's in class
**Current Solution:** Paper sign-in (messy)
**Cost of Pain:** Overcrowding, no shows
**Your Solution:** Digital check-in
**Pricing:** $99-$199/month

---

### 2. Class Booking
**Pain:** Waitlists, full classes
**Current Solution:** Mindbody (expensive, 5-15% fees)
**Cost of Pain:** High costs, limited customization
**Your Solution:** Branded booking app
**Pricing:** $149-$299/month premium

---

### 3. Membership Billing
**Pain:** Failed cards, expired memberships
**Current Solution:** Manual tracking
**Cost of Pain:** Revenue leakage
**Your Solution:** Automated billing + dunning
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 4. Retention Tracking
**Pain:** Members ghost before canceling
**Current Solution:** Notice when they quit
**Cost of Pain:** 30%+ annual churn
**Your Solution:** Attendance alerts
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 5. Personal Training
**Pain:** PT session tracking
**Current Solution:** Punch cards, memory
**Cost of Pain:** Session disputes
**Your Solution:** PT package tracking
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month add-on

---

### 6. Retail/POS
**Pain:** Selling merch, drinks
**Current Solution:** Separate POS
**Cost of Pain:** Double system
**Your Solution:** Integrated POS
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 7. Member Communication
**Pain:** Class cancellations, updates
**Current Solution:** Instagram, email (missed)
**Cost of Pain:** Empty classes, frustrated members
**Your Solution:** Push notifications
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 8. Instructor Management
**Pain:** Paying instructors (per class, commission)
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets
**Cost of Pain:** Calculation errors
**Your Solution:** Automated instructor pay
**Pricing:** $60-$120/month premium

---

### 9. Lead Management
**Pain:** Trial members don't convert
**Current Solution:** No follow-up system
**Cost of Pain:** Low conversion (20-30%)
**Your Solution:** Trial nurture sequences
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month add-on

---

### 10. Capacity Management
**Pain:** Class size limits
**Current Solution:** First-come first-serve
**Cost of Pain:** Crowding or empty spots
**Your Solution:** Smart capacity + waitlist
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 11. Challenges/Events
**Pain:** Running fitness challenges
**Current Solution:** Manual tracking
**Cost of Pain:** Low participation
**Your Solution:** Challenge platform
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 12. Nutrition Tracking
**Pain:** Members want holistic approach
**Current Solution:** MyFitnessPal (separate)
**Cost of Pain:** Fragmented experience
**Your Solution:** Nutrition integration
**Pricing:** $20-$40/month add-on

---

### 13. Family Memberships
**Pain:** Multi-person accounts
**Current Solution:** Separate memberships
**Cost of Pain:** Confusion, lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Family plans
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 14. Corporate Wellness
**Pain:** B2B contracts
**Current Solution:** Manual invoicing
**Cost of Pain:** Complex tracking
**Your Solution:** Corporate portal
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month premium

---

### 15. Multi-location
**Pain:** Managing multiple studios
**Current Solution:** Separate systems
**Cost of Pain:** No unified view
**Your Solution:** Multi-location dashboard
**Pricing:** $200-$400/month

---
