# Church Giving Platform

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Nonprofit/Religious  
**Entry Cost:** $20K-$50K  
**Timeline:** 4-6 months  
**Revenue Model:** $49-$149/month per church + 1-2% transaction fees

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Online giving, member management, and communication for small-to-medium churches.

**Market Size:** 300K+ churches in US  
**Target:** 100-1,000 member churches

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Cash/Check Only Giving ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Younger members don't carry cash
**Current Solution:** Passing plate (declining)
**Cost of Pain:** 20-30% donation decline
**Your Solution:** Online/mobile giving
**Pricing:** $49-$99/month + 1% fee

---

### 2. Recurring Giving Setup
**Pain:** Hard to set up automatic tithes
**Current Solution:** Bank transfers (complicated)
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent giving
**Your Solution:** Easy recurring gifts
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 3. Member Communication

**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on
