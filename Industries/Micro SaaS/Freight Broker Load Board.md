# Freight Broker Load Board

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Logistics/Transportation  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months to MVP  
**Revenue Model:** $199-$499/month per broker

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Niche-specific load board with automated matching for freight brokers. General boards (DAT, Truckstop) are expensive and generic—you can win by focusing on specific freight types (refrigerated, flatbed, specialized) or lanes.

**Market Size:** 17,000+ freight brokers in US  
**Target:** $2M-$20M revenue brokers, 5-50 employees

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Broker owner or operations manager
**Secondary:** Dispatchers, carrier sales reps
**Decision Maker:** Owner

**Budget:** $200-$500/month per user
**Pain Urgency:** Very High (every load uncovered = lost margin)

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Load Coverage Speed ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Finding carriers for loads takes 2-6 hours
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, email blasts, DAT/Truckstop posting
**Cost of Pain:** $50-$200 in labor per load, delayed shipments
**Your Solution:** Instant matching algorithm
**Pricing:** $299-$499/month

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How long does it take to cover a typical load?"
- "What percentage of loads go uncovered?"
- "How many phone calls does it take to cover one load?"

---

### 2. Carrier Reliability
**Pain:** Carriers cancel, no-show, or are late
**Current Solution:** Known carriers, trial and error
**Cost of Pain:** $500-$5K per failed load, customer complaints
**Your Solution:** Carrier rating system, reliability scores
**Pricing:** Included in premium tier

---

### 3. Rate Negotiation
**Pain:** Don't know market rates, overpay or lose carriers
**Current Solution:** DAT RateView ($150/month), gut feeling
**Cost of Pain:** 10-20% overpay on rates
**Your Solution:** Real-time rate benchmarking
**Pricing:** $199-$349/month

---

### 4. Carrier Onboarding
**Pain:** Each carrier needs setup: insurance, W-9, contracts
**Current Solution:** Manual paperwork, file cabinets
**Cost of Pain:** 2-4 hours per new carrier
**Your Solution:** Digital onboarding, document management
**Pricing:** $249-$399/month

---

### 5. Load Tracking
**Pain:** Don't know where shipments are, customer calls
**Current Solution:** Calling drivers, ELD integrations (expensive)
**Cost of Pain:** 5-10 calls/day per dispatcher
**Your Solution:** Automated tracking via driver app
**Pricing:** Included in standard

---

### 6. Double Brokering Fraud
**Pain:** Carriers re-broker loads without authorization
**Current Solution:** Trust, occasional checks
**Cost of Pain:** Liability exposure, customer issues
**Your Solution:** Carrier verification, load monitoring
**Pricing:** Included in premium

---

### 7. Payment Processing
**Pain:** Factoring companies, slow pay, cash flow issues
**Current Solution:** Factoring (3-5% fee), quick pay
**Cost of Pain:** 3-5% of revenue in factoring fees
**Your Solution:** Integrated quick pay (lower fees)
**Pricing:** Transaction fees (revenue share model)

---

### 8. Lane History
**Pain:** Don't know lane-specific rates, capacity
**Current Solution:** Memory, scattered notes
**Cost of Pain:** Overpaying, missed opportunities
**Your Solution:** Lane-specific rate history
**Pricing:** $199-$299/month

---

### 9. Customer Communication
**Pain:** Customers call for updates, tracking info
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, email updates
**Cost of Pain:** 2-3 hours/day on customer service
**Your Solution:** Customer portal with real-time tracking
**Pricing:** $249-$399/month

---

### 10. Document Management
**Pain:** BOLs, PODs, rate confirmations scattered
**Current Solution:** Email, file cabinets, scanning
**Cost of Pain:** Hours searching for docs, billing delays
**Your Solution:** Centralized document repository
**Pricing:** Included in standard

---

### 11. Carrier Capacity Forecasting
**Pain:** Don't know which lanes will have capacity issues
**Current Solution:** Reactive, firefighting
**Cost of Pain:** Emergency rates, failed loads
**Your Solution:** Predictive capacity alerts
**Pricing:** $299-$499/month (premium feature)

---

### 12. Compliance Monitoring
**Pain:** Tracking carrier insurance, authority expirations
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, manual checking
**Cost of Pain:** Using non-compliant carriers, liability
**Your Solution:** Automated compliance monitoring
**Pricing:** $149-$249/month

---

### 13. Load Reposting
**Pain:** Loads fall off, need to repost quickly
**Current Solution:** Manual reposting across multiple boards
**Cost of Pain:** Delayed shipments, rush rates
**Your Solution:** Auto-repost, backup carrier network
**Pricing:** Included in premium

---

### 14. Team Coordination
**Pain:** Multiple brokers working same lanes, no visibility
**Current Solution:** Whiteboards, shouted updates
**Cost of Pain:** Internal competition, missed loads
**Your Solution:** Team load board, activity feed
**Pricing:** $349-$499/month (team tier)

---

### 15. TMS Integration
**Pain:** Load board doesn't talk to accounting/customer systems
**Current Solution:** Double data entry
**Cost of Pain:** Errors, delayed invoicing
**Your Solution:** API integrations with major TMS
**Pricing:** $249-$399/month

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Cover loads in 15 minutes instead of 4 hours. DAT is expensive and full of noise—we're built for [specific freight type]."

### Authority Builders
1. Load coverage time benchmark report
2. Lane rate history database
3. Case study: "How [Broker] reduced coverage time by 70%"
4. Carrier reliability scorecard

### Positioning Angles
- Speed: "Cover loads 10x faster"
- Specialization: "Built for reefer/flatbed/specialized"
- Quality: "Pre-vetted, reliable carriers only"

### Differentiators
- Niche focus (specific equipment type)
- Automated matching (not just posting)
- Integrated quick pay
- Better carrier quality

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Brokers with <5 employees (can't afford)
- ❌ Brokers doing <$2M/year (too small)
- ❌ Asset-based carriers (different model)
- ❌ Brokers happy with current process (no pain)
- ❌ Highly price-sensitive (want free/cheap)

**Best Prospects:**
- Refrigerated freight specialists
- Flatbed/heavy haul brokers
- Cross-border Mexico/US freight
- Growing brokers (hiring dispatchers)
- Brokers frustrated with DAT costs

---

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"We already use DAT/Truckstop"**
> "Most brokers use multiple boards. DAT gives you volume, we give you quality matches for [specific freight type]. Can I show you the difference?"

**"Carriers won't adopt another app"**
> "Carriers love us because we send them matched loads they actually want—less phone tag, better rates. Our carrier adoption is 80% within 30 days."

**"Our TMS has a load board"**
> "TMS load boards are basic posting. We do [automated matching/carrier vetting/quick pay] that TMSs can't do. Most brokers use us alongside their TMS."

**"It's too expensive"**
> "If we save you 2 hours per load and you cover 50 loads/week, that's $3K-$5K in labor savings. At $400/month, you break even covering 4 loads. How many do you cover weekly?"
