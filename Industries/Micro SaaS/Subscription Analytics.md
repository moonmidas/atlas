# Subscription Analytics

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** SaaS/E-commerce  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$299/month per store

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Churn analysis, cohort reporting, and subscription metrics for Shopify Subscription apps and SaaS companies.

**Market Size:** 100K+ subscription businesses  
**Target:** $50K+ MRR subscription companies

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Churn Analysis ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Don't know why customers churn
**Current Solution:** Guesswork, exit surveys (low response)
**Cost of Pain:** 5-10% monthly churn bleeding revenue
**Your Solution:** Churn cohort analysis
**Pricing:** $99-$199/month

---

### 2. LTV Calculation
**Pain:** Don't know true customer LTV
**Current Solution:** Simple averages (wrong)
**Cost of Pain:** Wrong CAC targets
**Your Solution:** Accurate LTV by cohort
**Pricing:** Included
