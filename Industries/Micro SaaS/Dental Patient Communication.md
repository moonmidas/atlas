# Dental Patient Communication

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Healthcare/Dental  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $79-$199/month per practice

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Appointment reminders, recall campaigns, and two-way texting for dental practices. Reduces no-shows and increases recall revenue.

**Market Size:** 190K+ dental practices  
**Target:** General dentistry, specialists

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. No-Shows ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** 10-15% no-show rate
**Current Solution:** Phone calls (time-consuming)
**Cost of Pain:** $200-$500 per day lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Automated reminders + confirmations
**Pricing:** $79-$129/month

---

### 2. Recall Revenue Loss
**Pain:** Patients don't return for cleanings
**Current Solution:** Postcards (ignored)
**Cost of Pain:** $100K+ annual lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Automated recall campaigns
**Pricing:** $99-$199/month

---

### 3. Phone Tag
**Pain:** Playing phone tag with patients
**Current Solution:** Voicemail, callbacks
**Cost of Pain:** Front desk overwhelmed
**Your Solution:** Two-way texting
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 4. Online Scheduling
**Pain:** Phone-only booking
**Current Solution:** Patients call, wait on hold
**Cost of Pain:** Lost appointments after hours
**Your Solution:** 24/7 online booking
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 5. Review Requests
**Pain:** Hard to get Google reviews
**Current Solution:** Ask in person (awkward)
**Cost of Pain:** Low online presence
**Your Solution:** Automated review requests
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 6. Treatment Plan Follow-up
