# Law Firm Document Automation

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Legal Tech  
**Entry Cost:** $20K-$50K  
**Timeline:** 4-6 months  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$249/month per attorney

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Document automation for law firms—contracts, pleadings, discovery with conditional logic and e-signature. Reduces paralegal time dramatically.

**Market Size:** 1.3M attorneys in US  
**Target:** Small-medium firms (5-50 attorneys)

---
