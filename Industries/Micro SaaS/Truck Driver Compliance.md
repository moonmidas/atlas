# Truck Driver Compliance

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Transportation  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $25-$49/month per driver

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

ELD (Electronic Logging Device), Hours of Service tracking, and DOT compliance for owner-operators and small fleets.

**Market Size:** 3.5M truck drivers  
**Target:** Owner-operators, 5-50 truck fleets

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. HOS Violations ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Exceeding hours, DOT fines
**Current Solution:** Paper logs (outdated), basic ELDs
**Cost of Pain:** $1,000-$15,000 fines, shutdown
**Your Solution:** Smart HOS tracking + alerts
**Pricing:** $35-$49/month

---

### 2. ELD Complexity
**Pain:** Current ELDs are clunky
**Current Solution:** Big providers (expensive, poor UX)
**Cost of Pain:** Wasted time, errors
**Your Solution:** Simple, driver-friendly ELD
**Pricing:** $25-$39/month

---

### 3. DVIR Inspections
**Pain:** Daily Vehicle Inspection Reports
**Current Solution:** Paper forms, manual
**Cost of Pain:** Lost forms, audit failures
**Your Solution:** Digital DVIR with photos
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 4. IFTA Reporting
**Pain:** Fuel tax calculations by state
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, manual entry
**Cost of Pain:** Errors, audit risk
**Your Solution:** Automated IFTA reports
**Pricing:** $15-$25/month add-on

---

### 5. Document Management
**Pain:** CDL, medical certs, permits
**Current Solution:** File folders, expiration surprises
**Cost of Pain:** Expired credentials = downtime
**Your Solution:** Document tracking with alerts
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 6. Dispatch Communication
**Pain:** Load info, route changes
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, text chaos
**Cost of Pain:** Miscommunication, rejected loads
**Your Solution:** Driver-dispatch messaging
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 7. Route Optimization
**Pain:** Inefficient routing
**Current Solution:** Google Maps (not truck-friendly)
**Cost of Pain:** Lost time, fuel
**Your Solution:** Truck routing
**Pricing:** $10-$20/month add-on

---

### 8. Load Tracking
**Pain:** Brokers want location updates
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, check calls
**Cost of Pain:** Annoyance, distraction
**Your Solution:** Automated load tracking
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 9. Expense Tracking
**Pain:** Receipts, per diem, fuel
**Current Solution:** Shoebox, manual entry
**Cost of Pain:** Missed deductions
**Your Solution:** Simple expense capture
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 10. Settlement Reconciliation
**Pain:** Comparing pay to miles
**Current Solution:** Trust, spot checks
**Cost of Pain:** Underpaid loads
**Your Solution:** Pay verification
**Pricing:** $5-$15/month add-on

---

### 11. CSA Score Monitoring
**Pain:** DOT compliance score
**Current Solution:** Check quarterly (too late)
**Cost of Pain:** High score = insurance, customer issues
**Your Solution:** Real-time CSA monitoring
**Pricing:** $20-$30/month premium

---

### 12. Maintenance Scheduling
**Pain:** PM tracking across fleet
**Current Solution:** Mileage logs, memory
**Cost of Pain:** Breakdowns, violations
**Your Solution:** Maintenance alerts
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 13. Parking Finder
**Pain:** Finding truck parking
**Current Solution:** Hope for space
**Cost of Pain:** Hours lost, safety issues
**Your Solution:** Parking availability
**Pricing:** Free add-on

---

### 14. Weigh Station Bypass
**Pain:** Time at scales
**Current Solution:** Drive through, hope
**Cost of Pain:** Delays, inspections
**Your Solution:** Bypass integration
**Pricing:** Partner integration

---

### 15. Drug/Alcohol Testing
**Pain:** Random testing compliance
**Current Solution:** Manual tracking
**Cost of Pain:** Compliance gaps
**Your Solution:** Testing management
**Pricing:** Included
