# Real Estate Investor Tools

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Real Estate/Investment  
**Entry Cost:** $10K-$25K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $29-$79/month per investor

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Deal analysis, property tracking, and portfolio management for real estate investors. Analyzes rentals, flips, BRRRR deals.

**Market Size:** 10M+ real estate investors  
**Target:** Active investors (1-50 properties)

---
