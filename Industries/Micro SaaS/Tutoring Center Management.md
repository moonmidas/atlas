# Tutoring Center Management

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Education/Tutoring  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $79-$149/month per center

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Scheduling, tutor management, and billing for tutoring centers and test prep companies.

**Market Size:** 100K+ tutoring businesses  
**Target:** 5-50 tutor centers

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Tutor-Student Matching ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Finding right tutor for student
**Current Solution:** Manual matching, memory
**Cost of Pain:** Bad fits, churn
**Your Solution:** Smart matching algorithm
**Pricing:** $79-$129/month

---

### 2. Schedule Coordination
**Pain:** Tutor + student + room availability
**Current Solution:** Complex spreadsheets
**Cost of Pain:** Double-bookings, gaps
**Your Solution:** Automated scheduling
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 3. Session Tracking
**Pain:** Tutors don't log sessions
**Current Solution:** Paper logs (incomplete)
**Cost of Pain:** Lost billing
**Your Solution:** Digital check-in/out
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month add-on

---

### 4. Progress Reporting
**Pain:** Parents want updates
**Current Solution:** Manual reports (time-consuming)
**Cost of Pain:** Parent dissatisfaction
**Your Solution:** Automated progress reports
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month

---

### 5. Tutor Payroll
**Pain:** Different rates, session counts
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, disputes
**Cost of Pain:** Errors, tutor turnover
**Your Solution:** Automated payroll calc
**Pricing:** $60-$120/month premium

---

### 6. Room Management
**Pain:** Physical space constraints
**Current Solution:** Whiteboard, hope
**Cost of Pain:** Conflicts, inefficiency
**Your Solution:** Room scheduling
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 7. Package Tracking
**Pain:** Prepaid hours management
**Current Solution:** Punch cards, manual
**Cost of Pain:** Over/under delivery
**Your Solution:** Package tracking
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 8. Online Session Integration
**Pain:** Zoom links, scheduling
**Current Solution:** Separate systems
**Cost of Pain:** Confusion, no-shows
**Your Solution:** Built-in video platform
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 9. Parent Communication
**Pain:** Keeping parents informed
**Current Solution:** Email, phone (reactive)
**Cost of Pain:** Churn, complaints
**Your Solution:** Parent portal
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month

---

### 10. Assessment Tracking
**Pain:** Test scores, improvement
**Current Solution:** Files, memory
**Cost of Pain:** Can't show value
**Your Solution:** Score tracking + visualization
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 11. Tutor Performance
**Pain:** Which tutors are effective
**Current Solution:** Anecdotal
**Cost of Pain:** Keeping poor tutors
**Your Solution:** Performance analytics
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month premium

---

### 12. Enrollment Management
**Pain:** New student onboarding
**Current Solution:** Paper forms, data entry
**Cost of Pain:** Delayed start
**Your Solution:** Digital intake
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 13. Curriculum Management
**Pain:** Tracking what was covered
**Current Solution:** Tutor notes (inconsistent)
**Cost of Pain:** Gaps, repetition
**Your Solution:** Session notes + curriculum tracking
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month add-on

---

### 14. Marketing Attribution
**Pain:** Which marketing works
**Current Solution:** "How did you hear about us?"
**Cost of Pain:** Wasted marketing spend
**Your Solution:** Lead tracking
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 15. Recurring Billing
**Pain:** Monthly package billing
**Current Solution:** Invoicing manually
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow issues
**Your Solution:** Automated billing
**Pricing:** Included
