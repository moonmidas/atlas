# Roofing Insurance Claim Software

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Construction/Insurance  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$35K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$199/month per roofer

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Streamlined insurance claim processing for roofing contractors—documentation, supplementing, and tracking for storm damage claims.

**Market Size:** 50K+ roofing companies  
**Target:** Storm restoration roofers

---
