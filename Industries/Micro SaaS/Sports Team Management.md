# Sports Team Management

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Sports/Youth  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $19-$49/month per team or $99-$299/league

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Scheduling, communication, and payment collection for youth sports teams and adult leagues.

**Market Size:** 45M youth sports participants  
**Target:** Team managers, league organizers

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Practice/Game Scheduling ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Field availability, conflicts
**Current Solution:** Group texts, chaos
**Cost of Pain:** Double-bookings, cancellations
**Your Solution:** Calendar with availability
**Pricing:** $29-$49/month per team

---

### 2. RSVP/Attendance
**Pain:** Who's coming to practice/game
**Current Solution:** "Reply all" texts
**Cost of Pain:** Planning blind
**Your Solution:** Automated RSVP tracking
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 3. Payment Collection
**Pain:** Dues, uniform fees, tournament costs
**Current Solution:** Venmo chaos, checks
**Cost of Pain:** Chasing payments
**Your Solution:** Team payment portal
**Pricing:** $5-$10/transaction or $19-$39/month

---

### 4. Communication Overload
**Pain:** Constant group text spam
**Current Solution:** WhatsApp groups (messy)
**Cost of Pain:** Missed important info
**Your Solution:** Structured communication
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Snack/Drink Schedule
**Pain:** Who brings snacks when
**Current Solution:** Sign-up sheet (forgotten)
**Cost of Pain:** No snacks, confusion
**Your Solution:** Volunteer scheduling
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 6. Carpool Coordination
**Pain:** Getting kids to away games
**Current Solution:** Manual coordination
**Cost of Pain:** Transportation gaps
**Your Solution:** Carpool matching
**Pricing:** $10-$20/month add-on

---

### 7. Photo Sharing
**Pain:** Game photos scattered
**Current Solution:** Text threads (low quality)
**Cost of Pain:** Memories lost
**Your Solution:** Team photo albums
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 8. Stats/Score Tracking
**Pain:** Player stats, team records
**Current Solution:** Paper book, memory
**Cost of Pain:** No tracking
**Your Solution:** Stats platform
**Pricing:** $15-$30/month add-on

---

### 9. Roster Management
**Pain:** Contact info, medical forms
**Current Solution:** Paper forms (lost)
**Cost of Pain:** Emergency info missing
**Your Solution:** Digital roster
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 10. League Scheduling
**Pain:** Creating balanced schedules
**Current Solution:** Excel marathon
**Cost of Pain:** Hours of work, errors
**Your Solution:** Auto-scheduler
**Pricing:** $99-$299/league

---

### 11. Referee/Umpire Assigning
**Pain:** Finding officials
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, begging
**Cost of Pain:** Games without refs
**Your Solution:** Official marketplace
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 12. Fundraising
**Pain:** Team fundraising
**Current Solution:** GoFundMe, sales
**Cost of Pain:** Admin burden
**Your Solution:** Fundraising tools
**Pricing:** 5-10% of funds raised

---

### 13. Multi-team Management
**Pain:** Managing club with 10+ teams
**Current Solution:** Separate group chats
**Cost of Pain:** Chaos, no visibility
**Your Solution:** Club dashboard
**Pricing:** $199-$499/month

---

### 14. Playbook/Strategy
**Pain:** Sharing plays, formations
**Current Solution:** Paper (wet/lost)
**Cost of Pain:** Confusion on field
**Your Solution:** Digital playbook
**Pricing:** $20-$40/month add-on

---

### 15. Tournament Management
**Pain:** Multi-day tournaments
**Current Solution:** Paper brackets, chaos
**Cost of Pain:** Scheduling disasters
**Your Solution:** Tournament platform
**Pricing:** $50-$150/tournament

---
