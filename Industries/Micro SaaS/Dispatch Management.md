# Dispatch Management

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Transportation/Logistics  
**Entry Cost:** $20K-$50K  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$249/month per dispatcher

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Route optimization, load assignment, and fleet coordination for trucking companies and delivery fleets.

**Market Size:** 500K+ trucking companies  
**Target:** 10-100 truck fleets

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Manual Dispatching ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Whiteboards, phone calls, chaos
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, memory
**Cost of Pain:** Inefficiency, missed loads
**Your Solution:** Digital dispatch board
**Pricing:** $99-$149/month

---

### 2. Route Optimization
**Pain:** Inefficient routes, wasted miles
**Current Solution:** Google Maps, guessing
**Cost of Pain:** 10-20% extra fuel
**Your Solution:** AI route optimization
**Pricing:** $149-$249/month premium
