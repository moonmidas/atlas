# Test Prep Tracking

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Education/Test Prep  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$35K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $49-$99/month per student or $199-$499/course

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Study planning, practice test analytics, and progress tracking for SAT/ACT/LSAT/MCAT prep.

**Market Size:** 10M+ test takers annually  
**Target:** Test prep companies, tutors

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Study Plan Creation ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Overwhelmed by material
**Current Solution:** Generic schedules
**Cost of Pain:** Poor time management
**Your Solution:** Personalized study plans
**Pricing:** $49-$79/student/month

---

### 2. Progress Tracking
**Pain:** Don't know if improving
**Current Solution:** Practice tests (occasional)
**Cost of Pain:** Blind studying
**Your Solution:** Continuous progress tracking
**Pricing:** Included
