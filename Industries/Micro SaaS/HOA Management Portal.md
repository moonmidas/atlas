# HOA Management Portal

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Community/Real Estate  
**Entry Cost:** $20K-$50K  
**Timeline:** 4-6 months  
**Revenue Model:** $49-$149/month per HOA

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Communication, dues collection, and maintenance requests for Homeowner Associations.

**Market Size:** 350K+ HOAs in US  
**Target:** 50-500 unit HOAs

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Dues Collection ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Late payments, collections
**Current Solution:** Checks, manual follow-up
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow issues, legal costs
**Your Solution:** Online payment portal
**Pricing:** $79-$129/month

---

### 2. Owner Communication
**Pain:** Reaching all homeowners
**Current Solution:** Email lists, paper notices
**Cost of Pain:** Poor attendance, violations
**Your Solution:** Centralized communication
**Pricing:** $49-$99/month

---

### 3. Maintenance Requests
**Pain:** Common area issues
**Current Solution:** Email, phone (lost)

**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on
