# Accounting Workflow Tools

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Professional Services  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $49-$129/month per firm

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Client management, document collection, and workflow automation for CPA firms and bookkeeping practices.

**Market Size:** 50K+ CPA firms, 200K+ bookkeepers  
**Target:** 5-50 person accounting firms

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Client Document Collection ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Chasing clients for documents during tax season
**Current Solution:** Email chains, phone calls, physical drop-offs
**Cost of Pain:** 30-50% of staff time, delayed filings
**Your Solution:** Client portal with automated reminders
**Pricing:** $79-$129/month
