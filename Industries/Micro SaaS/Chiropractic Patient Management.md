# Chiropractic Patient Management

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Healthcare/Chiropractic  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $79-$149/month per practice

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Care plans, SOAP notes, and patient tracking for chiropractic practices.

**Market Size:** 70K+ chiropractors  
**Target:** Solo to small group practices

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Care Plan Tracking ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Patients drop off care plans
**Current Solution:** Memory, spreadsheet
**Cost of Pain:** Lost revenue (60% of revenue)
**Your Solution:** Care plan tracking + alerts
**Pricing:** $79-$129/month

---

### 2. SOAP Notes
**Pain:** Documentation time
**Current Solution:** Handwritten (illegible), typing
**Cost of Pain:** 5-10 min per patient
**Your Solution:** Quick SOAP templates
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 3. X-Ray Integration
**Pain:** Managing imaging
**Current Solution:** Separate systems, printing
**Cost of Pain:** Lost images, inefficiency
**Your Solution:** X-ray storage + markup
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 4. Appointment Scheduling
**Pain:** Frequent visits (3x/week typical)
**Current Solution:** Generic schedulers
**Cost of Pain:** Complex booking patterns
**Your Solution:** Chiropractic-specific scheduling
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Patient Education
**Pain:** Explaining same concepts repeatedly
**Current Solution:** Verbal explanation
**Cost of Pain:** Time, confusion
**Your Solution:** Video library, handouts
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 6. Progress Exams
**Pain:** Tracking functional improvement
**Current Solution:** Memory, paper files
**Cost of Pain:** Can't demonstrate value
**Your Solution:** Outcome tracking
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month

---

### 7. Maintenance Care
**Pain:** Converting to wellness care
**Current Solution:** Hope they stay
**Cost of Pain:** Churn after acute phase
**Your Solution:** Maintenance reminders
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 8. Personal Injury

**Current Solution:** Separate systems
**Cost of Pain:** Billing complexity
**Your Solution:** PI case tracking
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month premium

### 9. Family Plans

**Your Solution:** Family tracking
**Pricing:** Included

### 10. Report of Findings

### 11. Travel Card/Packages

**Pricing:** Included
