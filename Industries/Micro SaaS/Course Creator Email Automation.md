# Course Creator Email Automation

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Education/Creator  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $49-$129/month per creator

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Student engagement, completion campaigns, and automated sequences for online course creators.

**Market Size:** 500K+ course creators  
**Target:** $10K+ revenue course creators

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

**Pain:** 90%+ students don't finish
**Current Solution:** Hope they come back
**Cost of Pain:** Bad reviews, refunds, low LTV
**Your Solution:** Engagement sequences
**Pricing:** $49-$99/month
