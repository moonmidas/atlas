# E-commerce Inventory Alerts

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** E-commerce  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $29-$99/month per store

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Stock level monitoring, reorder alerts, and demand forecasting for Shopify/WooCommerce stores.

**Market Size:** 2M+ Shopify stores  
**Target:** $100K-$5M revenue e-commerce stores

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Stockouts ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Running out of popular items
**Current Solution:** Manual checking, guesswork
**Cost of Pain:** Lost sales, ad waste
**Your Solution:** Low stock alerts
**Pricing:** $29-$49/month

---

### 2. Overstock ⭐ #2 PAIN
**Pain:** Tying up cash in inventory
**Current Solution:** Reactive ordering
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow issues, clearance
**Your Solution:** Optimal stock recommendations
**Pricing:** $49-$99/month premium

---

### 3. Supplier Delays
