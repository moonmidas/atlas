# HVAC Maintenance Agreement Software

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Home Services  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$30K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $79-$149/month per contractor

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Software to manage recurring HVAC maintenance agreements—scheduling, billing, and customer retention for contractors.

**Market Size:** 100K+ HVAC contractors  
**Target:** $1M-$10M HVAC companies

---
