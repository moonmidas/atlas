# Contractor Estimating Software

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Construction/Home Services  
**Entry Cost:** $12K-$35K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months to MVP  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$249/month per estimator

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

AI-assisted takeoff and estimating software for general contractors, replacing spreadsheets and manual blueprint review.

**Market Size:** 3M+ contractors in US  
**Target:** $1M-$10M revenue contractors, 5-50 employees

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Chief Estimator or Owner
**Secondary:** Project managers, office managers
**Decision Maker:** Owner (often makes final call)

**Budget:** $100-$250/month (needs to save 10+ hours to justify)
**Pain Urgency:** High during busy season, medium otherwise

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Manual Takeoff Time ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** 20-40 hours per complex estimate
**Current Solution:** Excel, manual blueprint measuring, scale rulers
**Cost of Pain:** $2,000-$4,000 per estimate in labor
**Your Solution:** AI-assisted digitized takeoff
**Pricing:** $149-$249/month per estimator

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How many estimates do you lose because they take too long?"
- "How many hours does your estimator spend on takeoffs vs. actual estimating?"
- "What's your cost per estimate including labor?"

---

### 2. Material Price Volatility
**Pain:** Prices change weekly, estimates go stale
**Current Solution:** Manual updates, phone calls to suppliers

**Pricing:** Included in premium tier

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Stop paying estimators $75/hour to do $15/hour work."

### Authority Builders
1. ROI calculator: "How much is manual estimating costing you?"
2. Case study: "How [Contractor] cut estimate time by 60%"
3. Benchmark report: "Estimating efficiency by trade"
4. Webinar: "Win more bids with faster estimates"

### Positioning Angles
- Speed: "Cut estimate time from 40 hours to 8 hours"
- Accuracy: "Eliminate the $30K spreadsheet error"
- Win rate: "Submit 3x more bids with same team"

### Differentiators
- AI takeoff (not just digital)
- Real-time pricing
- Trade-specific templates

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Residential-only contractors (price sensitive, low volume)
- ❌ Subs with <3 employees (can't afford)
- ❌ Companies using ancient software (high switching cost)
- ❌ Estimators who "like their spreadsheets" (change resistant)
- ❌ Custom home builders (every project unique)

**Best Prospects:**
- Commercial GCs ($5M-$50M revenue)
- Trade contractors (electrical, HVAC, plumbing)
- Multi-family residential builders
- Companies growing fast (hiring estimators)
