# Wholesale B2B Portal

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** E-commerce/B2B  
**Entry Cost:** $20K-$50K  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months  
**Revenue Model:** $149-$399/month per store

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

B2B ordering portals with net terms, volume pricing, and account management for wholesale businesses.

**Market Size:** 500K+ wholesale businesses  
**Target:** $1M+ revenue wholesalers

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Manual Order Entry ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Phone/email orders, manual entry
**Current Solution:** Order desk, spreadsheets
**Cost of Pain:** 10-20 hours/week, errors
**Your Solution:** Self-service B2B portal
**Pricing:** $149-$249/month

---

### 2. Net Terms Management
**Pain:** Credit applications, terms tracking
**Current Solution:** Accounting system, manual
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow risk, collections
**Your Solution:** Automated net terms
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 3. Tiered Pricing
**Pain:** Volume discounts, customer-specific pricing
**Current Solution:** Price lists (outdated), memory
**Cost of Pain:** Margin erosion, disputes
**Your Solution:** Dynamic pricing engine
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 4. Quick Order
**Pain:** Reordering same SKUs
**Current Solution:** Search, add to cart (slow)
**Cost of Pain:** Customer frustration
**Your Solution:** Quick order forms, CSV upload
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Account Management
**Pain:** Managing B2B customer accounts
**Current Solution:** CRM (separate), email
**Cost of Pain:** No account visibility
**Your Solution:** Account dashboards
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 6. Order History
**Pain:** "What did I order last time?"
**Current Solution:** Dig through invoices
**Cost of Pain:** Time, errors
**Your Solution:** Order history + reorder
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 7. Inventory Visibility
**Pain:** B2B customers need stock info
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, delayed response
**Cost of Pain:** Sales lost to stock uncertainty
**Your Solution:** Real-time stock visibility
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 8. Multi-user Accounts
**Pain:** Multiple buyers per company
**Current Solution:** Shared login (no tracking)
**Cost of Pain:** No visibility
**Your Solution:** Multi-user with permissions
**Pricing:** $30-$60/month add-on

---

### 9. Quote Requests
**Pain:** Custom quotes for large orders
**Current Solution:** Email back-and-forth
**Cost of Pain:** Slow response, lost deals
**Your Solution:** Digital quote workflow
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month

---

### 10. Case/MOQ Management
**Pain:** Case packs, minimum orders
**Current Solution:** Manual calculation
**Cost of Pain:** Order errors
**Your Solution:** Case pack ordering
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 11. Sales Rep Integration
**Pain:** Reps need customer visibility
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, CRM gaps
**Cost of Pain:** Blind selling
**Your Solution:** Rep portals
**Pricing:** $60-$120/month premium

---

### 12. Backorder Management
**Pain:** Managing partial shipments
**Current Solution:** Manual coordination
**Cost of Pain:** Confusion, cancellations
**Your Solution:** Backorder tracking
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 13. Punchout Catalog
**Pain:** Large customers need punchout
**Current Solution:** Expensive custom dev
**Cost of Pain:** Lost enterprise deals
**Your Solution:** Punchout integration
**Pricing:** $100-$200/month premium

---

### 14. Shipping Integration
**Pain:** Freight quotes, LTL
**Current Solution:** Phone calls, estimates
**Cost of Pain:** Under/overcharging
**Your Solution:** Freight quoting
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 15. Payment Terms
**Pain:** Multiple payment methods
**Current Solution:** Invoices, checks, wires
**Cost of Pain:** Reconciliation nightmare
**Your Solution:** Unified B2B payments
**Pricing:** Transaction fees
