# Music Studio Management

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Education/Music  
**Entry Cost:** $10K-$25K  
**Timeline:** 2-4 months  
**Revenue Model:** $39-$89/month per studio

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Lesson scheduling, billing, and student progress tracking for private music teachers and studios.

**Market Size:** 200K+ private music teachers  
**Target:** Independent teachers, small studios

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Lesson Scheduling ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Back-and-forth to find times
**Current Solution:** Email, phone tag
**Cost of Pain:** Hours of coordination
**Your Solution:** Self-service booking
**Pricing:** $39-$69/month

---

### 2. Make-up Lessons
**Pain:** Managing cancellations, reschedules
**Current Solution:** Chaos, lost revenue
**Cost of Pain:** 10-20% no-shows
**Your Solution:** Automated make-up scheduling
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 3. Recital Management
**Pain:** Organizing student recitals
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, emails
**Cost of Pain:** Hours of admin
**Your Solution:** Recital planning tools
**Pricing:** $20-$40/month add-on

---

### 4. Practice Tracking

**Pricing:** Included
