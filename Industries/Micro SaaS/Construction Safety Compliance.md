# Construction Safety Compliance Software

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Construction/Compliance  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $89-$179/month per contractor

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

OSHA compliance, safety training tracking, and incident reporting for construction companies. Regulatory requirement with liability reduction.

**Market Size:** 700K+ construction companies  
**Target:** $5M+ revenue GCs and trades
