# Physical Therapy Home Exercise

**Category:** Micro SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Healthcare/PT  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-5 months  
**Revenue Model:** $99-$249/month per clinic

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Home exercise programs, remote monitoring, and patient engagement for physical therapy practices.

**Market Size:** 50K+ PT clinics  
**Target:** Outpatient PT practices

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Home Exercise Compliance ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Patients don't do exercises at home
**Current Solution:** Paper handouts (lost, ignored)
**Cost of Pain:** Poor outcomes, longer recovery
**Your Solution:** Video-based HEP with tracking
**Pricing:** $99-$179/month

---

### 2. Outcome Tracking
**Pain:** Measuring functional improvement
**Current Solution:** Subjective assessment
**Cost of Pain:** Can't prove value, denied reimbursement
**Your Solution:** Standardized outcome measures
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 3. Telehealth PT
**Pain:** Remote visits, form checking
**Current Solution:** Video calls (can't see well)
**Cost of Pain:** Limited remote capability
**Your Solution:** Async video assessment
**Pricing:** $50-$100/month add-on

---

### 4. Patient Messaging
**Pain:** "Is this pain normal?" calls
**Current Solution:** Phone tag
**Cost of Pain:** Staff time, patient anxiety
**Your Solution:** Secure messaging
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Plan of Care Compliance
**Pain:** Patients not attending prescribed visits
**Current Solution:** Phone reminders
**Cost of Pain:** Revenue loss, poor outcomes
**Your Solution:** Attendance tracking + engagement
**Pricing:** $40-$80/month add-on

### 6. Video Library

**Pricing:** Included
