# 19 - Cohort Courses

**Category:** Education  
**Project Range:** $5K-$30K+  
**Timeline:** 6-12 weeks to launch  
**Business Model:** Cohort-based enrollment ($500-$5,000 per student)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Cohort Courses = Structured transformation with accountability, live support, and premium positioning. Unlike self-paced courses, cohorts have completion rates of 70-90% (vs. 5-15%).

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $5K-$20K (curriculum, platform, first cohort) |
| Price per Student | $500-$5,000 |
| Cohort Size | 20-100 students |
| Revenue per Cohort | $10K-$100K |
| Gross Margin | 70-85% |
| Cohorts per Year | 4-12 |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Curriculum:**
- Clear transformation promise
- Structured milestones
- Accountability mechanisms

**Community:**
- Peer groups
- Live Q&A sessions
- Office hours

**Outcomes:**
- Case studies/testimonials
- Completion certificates
- Job placement (if applicable)

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ No clear outcome (vague promises)
- ❌ Creator can't teach (expert ≠ educator)
- ❌ No time commitment from students
- ❌ Low completion rates (<60%)
- ❌ No demand validation
