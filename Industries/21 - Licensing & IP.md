# 21 - Licensing & IP

**Category:** Asset-Light  
**Project Range:** $15K-$90K+  
**Timeline:** 6-18 months to first deal  
**Business Model:** Royalties (3-10% of sales) + flat fees

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Licensing & IP = Brands, characters, methods, and formats monetized without operating every transaction. Create once, license repeatedly. High leverage, low overhead.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $15K-$50K (IP development, legal) |
| Royalty Rate | 3-10% of licensee revenue |
| Flat Fee | $5K-$100K per license |
| Minimum Guarantees | $10K-$500K per deal |
| Gross Margin | 90%+ |
| Sales Cycle | 6-18 months |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**IP Protection:**
- Trademarks for brands
- Copyrights for content
- Patents for inventions
- Trade secrets for methods

**Licensee Support:**
- Training materials
- Quality standards
- Marketing support
- Ongoing updates

**Deal Structure:**
- Minimum guarantees
- Performance milestones
- Territory exclusivity
- Audit rights

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Weak IP protection
- ❌ No proven demand
- ❌ Licensee concentration (one deal = 80% revenue)
- ❌ Difficult to monitor/enforce
- ❌ Declining relevance
