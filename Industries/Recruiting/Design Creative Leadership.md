## The Opportunity

Design and creative leadership recruiting serves companies where design is a competitive differentiator. Need ranges from VP Design to Creative Directors for product, brand, and marketing creative.

**Market Size:** $2B annually  
**Target:** Tech companies, consumer brands, agencies  
**Examples:** Designer Fund, 8VC Design Network

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / CPO / VP Design / Creative Director  
**Budget:** $20K-$50K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** High - design gaps impact product experience

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Design as Strategic Function (TOP PAIN)
**Pain:** Design is underutilized as strategic asset
**Current Solution:** Design as last step, decoration only
**Cost of Pain:** Poor UX, competitive disadvantage
**Your Solution:** Strategic design leader network
**Pricing:** $35K-$50K for VP Design

### 2. Design Team Scaling
**Pain:** Cannot hire designers fast enough for product growth
**Current Solution:** Stretch designers, use contractors
**Cost of Pain:** Design bottlenecks, inconsistent quality
**Your Solution:** Design leadership specialist
**Pricing:** $28K-$45K for design leader

### 3. Product Design Leadership
**Pain:** Need design leaders who understand product strategy
**Current Solution:** Visual designers without product thinking
**Cost of Pain:** Pretty but unusable products
**Your Solution:** Product design specialist
**Pricing:** $30K-$48K for product design leader

**Pricing:** $26K-$44K for systems leader
