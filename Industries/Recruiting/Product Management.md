## The Opportunity

Product management recruiting is critical for tech companies where product execution determines success. Companies need product leaders who can translate vision into roadmap, work with engineering, and drive growth.

**Market Size:** $3.5B annually  
**Target:** Tech companies, SaaS businesses, consumer apps  
**Examples:** Product Gym, Hired, Aha!

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / CPO / VP Product  
**Budget:** $25K-$65K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** Critical - product leadership gaps stall execution

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Founder-Led Product Cannot Scale (TOP PAIN)
**Pain:** Founder has been the product visionary but cannot scale to full product organization
**Current Solution:** Hire junior PMs, hope they figure it out
**Cost of Pain:** Product drift, delayed launches, founder burnout
**Your Solution:** VP Product with scaling experience, transition planning
**Pricing:** $40K-$65K for VP Product

### 2. Technical vs Commercial Product Balance
**Pain:** Cannot find PMs who balance technical depth with commercial acumen
**Current Solution:** Hire technical PMs and hope they learn business, or vice versa
**Cost of Pain:** Engineering conflict, poor business outcomes
**Your Solution:** Balanced competency assessment
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K per placement

### 3. Product Culture Fit Challenges
**Pain:** Product leaders from big tech cannot adapt to startup constraints
**Current Solution:** Assume product skills transfer
**Cost of Pain:** Cultural clashes, unrealistic expectations
**Your Solution:** Stage-fit assessment, startup product network
**Pricing:** $30K-$50K per placement

### 4. Customer Discovery Capability
**Pain:** PMs lack customer research and discovery skills
**Current Solution:** Hope PMs talk to customers
**Cost of Pain:** Product-market fit erosion, wrong features
**Your Solution:** Customer-centric PM assessment
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per placement

### 5. Data-Driven Decision Making
**Pain:** PMs make decisions on intuition rather than data
**Current Solution:** Accept subjective prioritization
**Cost of Pain:** Wrong priorities, wasted engineering time
**Your Solution:** Analytics-savvy PM sourcing
**Pricing:** $28K-$48K per placement

### 6. Cross-Functional Leadership
**Pain:** PMs cannot effectively lead without authority across functions
**Current Solution:** Hire smart people, hope they influence
**Cost of Pain:** Team dysfunction, slow execution
**Your Solution:** Influence skills assessment
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K per placement

### 7. Agile/Scrum Expertise
**Pain:** Need PMs who can run effective agile processes
**Current Solution:** Hire certified scrum masters separately
**Cost of Pain:** Process overhead without outcomes
**Your Solution:** Agile-experienced PM network
**Pricing:** $22K-$40K per placement

### 8. Growth Product Management
**Pain:** Need product leaders focused on growth and monetization
**Current Solution:** Marketing handles growth, product handles features
**Cost of Pain:** Siloed growth efforts, missed opportunities
**Your Solution:** Growth PM specialization
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K for growth PM

### 9. Platform Product Expertise
**Pain:** Platform products require different skills than end-user products
**Current Solution:** Treat platform PMs the same as consumer PMs
**Cost of Pain:** Poor API design, developer experience issues
**Your Solution:** Platform PM specialist network
**Pricing:** $28K-$50K per placement

### 10. International Product Experience
**Pain:** Expanding internationally requires localization expertise
**Current Solution:** Retrofit US product for international
**Cost of Pain:** Poor international adoption
**Your Solution:** International product experience sourcing
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K per placement

### 11. Zero-to-One Product Experience
**Pain:** Launching new products requires different skills than optimizing existing
**Current Solution:** Hire optimizers, expect them to innovate
**Cost of Pain:** Failed new product launches
**Your Solution:** Zero-to-one PM assessment
**Pricing:** $35K-$60K for innovation PM

### 12. Stakeholder Management Skills
**Pain:** PMs struggle managing conflicting stakeholder demands
**Current Solution:** PMs learn on the job
**Cost of Pain:** Burnout, missed expectations
**Your Solution:** Stakeholder management evaluation
**Pricing:** $25K-$42K per placement

### 13. Roadmap Communication
**Pain:** PMs cannot effectively communicate roadmap and strategy
**Current Solution:** Assume communication skills
**Cost of Pain:** Misaligned teams, poor execution
**Your Solution:** Communication skills assessment
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per placement

### 14. Technical Depth Evaluation
**Pain:** Non-technical leaders cannot assess PM technical credibility
**Current Solution:** Hope they are technical enough
**Cost of Pain:** Engineering credibility issues
**Your Solution:** Technical depth assessment
**Pricing:** $28K-$48K per placement

### 15. Product Metrics Definition
**Pain:** PMs lack framework for defining and tracking product metrics
**Current Solution:** Each PM defines their own metrics
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent measurement, poor accountability
**Your Solution:** Metrics-savvy PM sourcing
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K per placement
