# Template: Private Equity VC Talent

**Category:** Recruiting Firms  
**Sub-Category:** Financial Services Executive Search  
**Entry Cost:** $15K-$40K  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months  
**Revenue Model:** Retainer (significant upfront); placement fee ($50K-$150K)

---

## The Opportunity

Private Equity and Venture Capital talent recruiting serves investment firms needing investment professionals, operating partners, and portfolio company executives. Highest fees in recruiting due to compensation levels and candidate scarcity.

**Market Size:** $1.5B annually  
**Target:** Private equity firms, VC funds, family offices  
**Examples:** Heidrick & Struggles, Korn Ferry

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Managing Partner / Chief Talent Officer / HR Director  
**Budget:** $50K-$150K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** Critical - talent determines fund returns

---
