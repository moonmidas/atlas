## The Opportunity

Legal and compliance recruiting serves companies needing in-house counsel, compliance officers, and legal operations leaders. Critical for regulated industries and scaling companies facing complex legal challenges.

**Market Size:** $3B annually  
**Target:** Tech companies, financial services, healthcare, regulated industries  
**Examples:** Major Lindsey, Lateral Link, Kinney Recruiting

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / General Counsel / Chief Compliance Officer  
**Budget:** $25K-$60K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** High - legal gaps create risk

**Cost of Pain:** High legal costs, slow response times
**Your Solution:** First legal hire specialist
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K for first legal counsel
