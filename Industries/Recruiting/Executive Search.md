# Executive Search (Retained)

**Category:** Recruiting Firms  
**Sub-Category:** Executive Recruitment  
**Entry Cost:** $10K-$25K  
**Timeline:** 60-120 day placements  
**Revenue Model:** 25-33% of first-year compensation

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Retained executive search for VP+ roles. High fees, long sales cycles, but $50K-$150K per placement.

**Market Size:** 100K+ executive searches annually  
**Target:** VC-backed startups, PE portfolio companies  
**Examples:** Korn Ferry, Heidrick & Struggles, boutique firms

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO or Board (for C-suite)  
**Secondary:** VP Talent, investors  
**Decision Maker:** CEO

**Budget:** $50K-$150K per search  
**Pain Urgency:** High (role is critical, can't fill)

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Confidentiality Requirements ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Can't advertise role publicly
**Current Solution:** Network outreach (limited), contingent recruiters (spray & pray)
**Cost of Pain:** Limited candidate pool, wrong fits
**Your Solution:** Confidential executive network
**Pricing:** Retainer + success fee

**Discovery Questions:**
- "Why is this role confidential?"
- "Who knows you're hiring?"
- "What happens if word gets out?"

---

### 2. Network Exhaustion
**Pain:** Founder's network tapped out
**Current Solution:** Job postings (wrong level), LinkedIn (limited)
**Cost of Pain:** Can't fill critical role
**Your Solution:** Executive network access
**Pricing:** Retained search

---

### 3. Speed Requirements
**Pain:** Need someone in 60-90 days
**Current Solution:** Internal recruiting (too slow), contingency (unreliable)
**Cost of Pain:** Role vacant, company stalled
**Your Solution:** Dedicated search team
**Pricing:** Premium for speed

---

### 4. Cultural Fit Assessment
**Pain:** Previous hires didn't fit culturally
**Current Solution:** Interviews (subjective), references
**Cost of Pain:** Failed hires ($500K+ cost)
**Your Solution:** Culture assessment framework
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 5. Compensation Benchmarking
**Pain:** Don't know market rate for role
**Current Solution:** Comp surveys (dated), guesswork
**Cost of Pain:** Lose candidates to comp, overpay
**Your Solution:** Real-time comp data
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 6. Assessment Depth
**Pain:** Surface-level interviews miss issues
**Current Solution:** Reference checks (friends)
**Cost of Pain:** Bad hires, firing drama
**Your Solution:** 360-degree assessment
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 7. Passive Candidates
**Pain:** Best candidates aren't looking
**Current Solution:** LinkedIn outreach (low response)
**Cost of Pain:** Settling for active candidates
**Your Solution:** Passive candidate outreach
**Pricing:** Core value

---

### 8. Candidate Experience
**Pain:** Slow process loses candidates
**Current Solution:** Internal process (chaotic)
**Cost of Pain:** Offers declined
**Your Solution:** Streamlined executive process
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 9. Reference Verification
**Pain:** References are coached/fake
**Current Solution:** Provided references only
**Cost of Pain:** Surprise performance issues
**Your Solution:** Back-channel references
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 10. Offer Negotiation
**Pain:** Candidates have leverage
**Current Solution:** Founder negotiation (emotional)
**Cost of Pain:** Overpayment, lost candidates
**Your Solution:** Negotiation representation
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 11. Onboarding Support
**Pain:** Executives fail in first 90 days
**Current Solution:** Sink or swim
**Cost of Pain:** Failed hire, restart search
**Your Solution:** Onboarding coaching
**Pricing:** Additional $10K-$20K

---

### 12. Diversity Requirements
**Pain:** Diverse slate requirements
**Current Solution:** Limited diverse networks
**Cost of Pain:** Board/investor pressure
**Your Solution:** Diverse candidate sourcing
**Pricing:** Included

---

### 13. Board Hiring
**Pain:** Board member recruitment
**Current Solution:** Investor networks (limited)
**Cost of Pain:** Wrong board fit
**Your Solution:** Board member network
**Pricing:** $50K-$100K

---

### 14. International Search
**Pain:** Hiring outside US
**Current Solution:** Limited network
**Cost of Pain:** Can't expand globally
**Your Solution:** Global executive network
**Pricing:** Premium + expenses

---

### 15. Replacement Guarantee
**Pain:** Fear of bad hire
**Current Solution:** Hope it works
**Cost of Pain:** Failed search fee
**Your Solution:** 12-month replacement guarantee
**Pricing:** Built into fee

---

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Big search firm results without the big firm bureaucracy. Dedicated partner attention."

### Authority Builders
1. Placement track record
2. Founder testimonials
3. Industry specialization
4. Thought leadership on hiring

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Companies shopping on price
- ❌ Roles <$200K total comp
- ❌ Unrealistic timelines
- ❌ Founders who want 10+ candidates

**Best Prospects:**
- Series B+ startups
- PE-backed companies
- Rapid growth (need executive infrastructure)
- Board-driven searches
