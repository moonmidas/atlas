## The Opportunity

Customer Success leadership recruiting serves subscription and recurring revenue businesses needing to reduce churn and expand accounts. Critical for SaaS companies where net revenue retention drives valuation.

**Market Size:** $2.5B annually  
**Target:** SaaS companies, subscription businesses  
**Examples:** Gainsight, Customer Success Network

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / CCO / VP Customer Success  
**Budget:** $20K-$50K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** Critical - CS gaps drive churn

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Post-Sales Engagement Gap (TOP PAIN)
**Pain:** Customers churn because of poor post-sale experience
**Current Solution:** Support handles everything post-sale
**Cost of Pain:** High churn, low expansion revenue
**Your Solution:** VP Customer Success specialist
**Pricing:** $35K-$50K for VP CS placement

### 2. Net Revenue Retention Focus
**Pain:** Need CS leaders who drive NRR, not just prevent churn
**Current Solution:** Reactive customer support only
**Cost of Pain:** Missed expansion opportunities
**Your Solution:** NRR-focused CS leader network
**Pricing:** $32K-$48K for CS leader

### 3. Customer Success Operations
**Pain:** CS team lacks process and systems
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets and ad-hoc processes
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent customer experience
**Your Solution:** CS Ops specialist
**Pricing:** $25K-$42K for CS Ops leader

### 4. Implementation and Onboarding
**Pain:** Poor onboarding leads to early churn
**Current Solution:** Professional services handles onboarding
**Cost of Pain:** Time-to-value delays, early churn
**Your Solution:** Implementation leader specialist
**Pricing:** $28K-$45K for implementation leader

### 5. Customer Health Scoring
**Pain:** No systematic way to predict churn risk
**Current Solution:** Reactive churn prevention
**Cost of Pain:** Surprise churn, reactive firefighting
**Your Solution:** Health score framework expert
**Pricing:** $26K-$44K for CS leader

### 6. Account Management Expansion
**Pain:** Need to grow existing accounts systematically
**Current Solution:** Sales handles expansion when convenient
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent expansion, missed quota
**Your Solution:** Account management specialist
**Pricing:** $28K-$46K for AM leader

### 7. Customer Education and Training
**Pain:** Customers do not adopt product fully
**Current Solution:** Documentation and webinars only
**Cost of Pain:** Low adoption, high support burden
**Your Solution:** Customer education specialist
**Pricing:** $24K-$42K for education leader

### 8. Support to Success Transition
**Pain:** Moving from support model to success model
**Current Solution:** Rename support to success
**Cost of Pain:** Same reactive behavior, no outcomes
**Your Solution:** Transformation CS leader
**Pricing:** $30K-$48K for CS transformation leader

### 9. Enterprise Customer Success
**Pain:** Enterprise accounts need dedicated success resources
**Current Solution:** Shared CSMs across all segments
**Cost of Pain:** Enterprise churn, expansion gaps
**Your Solution:** Enterprise CS specialist
**Pricing:** $30K-$50K for enterprise CS leader

### 10. CS Technology Stack
**Pain:** Need to implement CS platform (Gainsight, ChurnZero)
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets and CRM only
**Cost of Pain:** No visibility, reactive approach
**Your Solution:** CS tech-savvy leader
**Pricing:** $26K-$46K for tech-enabled CS leader

### 11. Customer Journey Mapping
**Pain:** No systematic view of customer journey
**Current Solution:** Intuitive understanding only
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent experience, gaps
**Your Solution:** Journey mapping expert
**Pricing:** $24K-$42K for CS leader

### 12. Voice of Customer Programs
**Pain:** Customer feedback is not systematic
**Current Solution:** Occasional surveys, anecdotal feedback
**Cost of Pain:** Missed insights, product misalignment
**Your Solution:** VoC program specialist
**Pricing:** $25K-$44K for insights leader

### 13. CS Team Scaling
**Pain:** Cannot hire CSMs fast enough for growth
**Current Solution:** Stretch existing team
**Cost of Pain:** Burnout, inconsistent coverage
**Your Solution:** Scalable CS hiring expertise
**Pricing:** $22K-$40K per placement

### 14. CS and Product Alignment
**Pain:** CS insights do not reach product team
**Current Solution:** Occasional escalations only
**Cost of Pain:** Product does not solve customer needs
**Your Solution:** Product-aligned CS leader
**Pricing:** $28K-$48K for CS leader

### 15. Retention Metrics and Reporting
**Pain:** Cannot accurately measure and report on retention
