## The Opportunity

HR and People leadership recruiting serves companies scaling their people operations, culture, and talent strategy. Critical for companies going through rapid growth or transformation.

**Market Size:** $4B annually  
**Target:** Growth-stage companies, companies scaling headcount  
**Examples:** HR Partners, HR Collaborative

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / CHRO / VP People  
**Budget:** $20K-$50K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** High - people gaps impact culture and retention
