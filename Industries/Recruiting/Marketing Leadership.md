## The Opportunity

Marketing leadership recruiting serves growth-stage companies needing to scale customer acquisition and brand awareness. The wrong marketing leader burns budget without results.

**Market Size:** $4B annually  
**Target:** B2B SaaS, consumer tech, e-commerce  
**Examples:** Marketing Architects, The Connective Good

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / CMO / VP Marketing  
**Budget:** $25K-$60K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** High - marketing gaps stall growth
