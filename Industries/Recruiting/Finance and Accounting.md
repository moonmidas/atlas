## The Opportunity

Finance and accounting recruiting serves companies needing financial leadership—from Controllers and CFOs to FP&A analysts and accounting managers. Growth-stage companies need financial operators who can scale with the business.

**Market Size:** $8B annually  
**Target:** Growth-stage companies, PE-backed firms, public companies  
**Examples:** Robert Half, Accountemps, Parker + Lynch

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO / CFO / Controller  
**Budget:** $18K-$45K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** High - financial leadership gaps impact fundraising and operations

---

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Controller to CFO Transition Gap (TOP PAIN)
**Pain:** Companies outgrow their Controller but cannot find CFO-level talent
**Current Solution:** Promote Controller beyond capability, delay CFO hire
**Cost of Pain:** Fundraising challenges, strategic planning gaps
**Your Solution:** CFO assessment framework, staged leadership placement
**Pricing:** $35K-$45K for CFO placement

### 2. Public Company Experience Shortage
**Pain:** Need public company financial experience for IPO preparation
**Current Solution:** Hire consultants, train existing team
**Cost of Pain:** Compliance issues, delayed IPO readiness
**Your Solution:** Public company CFO network, SOX compliance expertise
**Pricing:** $40K-$50K for public company CFO

### 3. FP&A Function Build-Out
**Pain:** Growth-stage companies lack financial planning capability
**Current Solution:** Reactive forecasting, spreadsheet-based planning
**Cost of Pain:** Poor resource allocation, missed targets
**Your Solution:** FP&A specialist sourcing, function design consulting
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K for FP&A leader

### 4. Accounting Manager Pipeline
**Pain:** Cannot find qualified accounting managers to handle growth
**Current Solution:** Promote senior accountants too early
**Cost of Pain:** Close errors, audit issues, team turnover
**Your Solution:** Accounting manager specialization, CPA network
**Pricing:** $18K-$30K per placement

### 5. Technical Accounting Expertise
**Pain:** Complex GAAP issues require specialized knowledge (revenue recognition, M&A accounting)
**Current Solution:** External consultants, expensive Big 4 support
**Cost of Pain:** Delayed closes, audit adjustments, compliance risk
**Your Solution:** Technical accounting specialist network
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per placement

### 6. Financial Systems Implementation
**Pain:** Need finance leaders who can implement ERP systems (NetSuite, SAP)
**Current Solution:** Hire for accounting only, hope they know systems
**Cost of Pain:** Failed implementations, costly rework
**Your Solution:** Systems-savvy finance leader sourcing
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per placement

### 7. Investor Relations Experience
**Pain:** Public companies need investor relations expertise
**Current Solution:** External IR firms, minimal internal capability
**Cost of Pain:** Poor investor communication, stock price volatility
**Your Solution:** IR specialist network, public markets experience
**Pricing:** $30K-$45K for IR leader

### 8. Treasury Management Gaps
**Pain:** Growth companies lack sophisticated treasury management
**Current Solution:** Bank relationships only, reactive cash management
**Cost of Pain:** Suboptimal cash returns, FX exposure
**Your Solution:** Treasury specialist sourcing
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per placement

### 9. Financial Operations Scaling
**Pain:** Finance operations (AP, AR, payroll) cannot keep pace with growth
**Current Solution:** Add headcount without process improvement
**Cost of Pain:** Close delays, vendor issues, employee frustration
**Your Solution:** Operations-focused finance leader placement
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K per placement

### 10. Audit Preparation Support
**Pain:** Annual audits consume excessive management time
**Current Solution:** Scramble at year-end, external consultant support
**Cost of Pain:** Audit delays, management distraction, extra fees
**Your Solution:** Audit-ready finance leader placement
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K per placement

### 11. International Financial Complexity
**Pain:** Global operations require multi-entity accounting expertise
**Current Solution:** Local accountants in each country, lack consolidation
**Cost of Pain:** Reporting delays, compliance issues
**Your Solution:** International finance specialist network
**Pricing:** $30K-$45K per placement

### 12. Financial Modeling Capability
**Pain:** Need advanced financial modeling for strategic decisions
**Current Solution:** Hire MBAs, hope they can model
**Cost of Pain:** Poor decision support, inaccurate projections
**Your Solution:** Financial modeling assessment, specialist sourcing
**Pricing:** $22K-$38K per placement

### 13. Fundraising Support Experience
**Pain:** CFOs need capital-raising experience for growth funding
**Current Solution:** Hire investment bankers for every round
**Cost of Pain:** High fees, limited internal capability
**Your Solution:** Fundraising-experienced CFO network
**Pricing:** $35K-$50K for fundraising CFO

### 14. Financial Culture Translation
**Pain:** Finance leaders from big companies cannot adapt to startup pace
**Current Solution:** Assume finance skills transfer
**Cost of Pain:** Cultural clashes, delayed decision-making
**Your Solution:** Stage-fit assessment, startup finance network
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per placement

### 15. Remote Finance Team Management
**Pain:** Managing distributed finance teams requires different skills
**Current Solution:** Assume in-person management skills transfer
**Cost of Pain:** Process inconsistencies, control issues
**Your Solution:** Remote finance management assessment
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K per placement
