**Category:** Recruiting Firms  
**Sub-Category:** Healthcare Staffing

## The Opportunity

Healthcare staffing is a $20B+ market with chronic shortages of physicians, nurse practitioners, and allied health professionals.

**Market Size:** $20B+ annually  
**Target:** Hospitals, health systems, private practices, telehealth companies  
**Examples:** AMN Healthcare, CompHealth, Merritt Hawkins

---

## Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Chief Medical Officer / HR Director / Practice Manager  
**Budget:** $15K-$40K per placement  
**Pain Urgency:** Critical - staffing shortages impact patient care

---

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Physician Shortages (TOP PAIN)
**Pain:** Critical shortage of physicians in most specialties
**Current Solution:** Locum tenens (expensive), extend existing staff hours
**Cost of Pain:** $1M+ per unfilled physician in lost revenue
**Your Solution:** National physician network, relocation assistance
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per physician placement

### 2. Nurse Practitioner Recruitment Difficulties
**Pain:** High demand for NPs, limited supply
**Current Solution:** Compete on salary alone
**Cost of Pain:** Extended patient wait times, physician burnout
**Your Solution:** NP-focused sourcing, practice opportunity marketing
**Pricing:** $15K-$25K per NP placement

### 3. Credentialing Takes Too Long
**Pain:** 3-6 month credentialing process delays starts
**Current Solution:** Manual credentialing, hope for speed
**Cost of Pain:** Lost candidates accept other offers
**Your Solution:** Credentialing process optimization
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K with credentialing support

### 4. Rural Location Challenges
**Pain:** Hard to attract talent to rural/underserved areas
**Current Solution:** Sign-on bonuses
**Cost of Pain:** Chronic understaffing in rural facilities
**Your Solution:** Rural opportunity packaging
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K for rural placements

### 5. Visa Requirements for International Physicians
**Pain:** J-1 waivers, H-1B complexity
**Current Solution:** Immigration attorneys
**Cost of Pain:** 12-18 month delays, legal fees
**Your Solution:** Immigration expertise
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K with visa support

### 6. Allied Health Professional Shortages
**Pain:** Shortages of PTs, OTs, SLPs, pharmacists
**Current Solution:** General staffing agencies
**Cost of Pain:** Service limitations, patient access issues
**Your Solution:** Allied health specialization
**Pricing:** $10K-$20K per allied health placement

### 7. Retention After Placement
**Pain:** High turnover in healthcare roles
**Current Solution:** Hope they stay
**Cost of Pain:** $100K+ per replacement cycle
**Your Solution:** Retention-focused matching
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K with retention guarantee

### 8. Telehealth Provider Recruitment
**Pain:** Rapid need for telehealth-capable providers
**Current Solution:** Adapt in-person recruitment methods
**Cost of Pain:** Missed telehealth expansion opportunities
**Your Solution:** Telehealth-specific sourcing
**Pricing:** $15K-$30K per telehealth placement

### 9. Compensation Benchmarking Gaps
**Pain:** Healthcare compensation varies widely by region
**Current Solution:** Use outdated MGMA data
**Cost of Pain:** Lost candidates to better offers
**Your Solution:** Real-time healthcare compensation data
**Pricing:** $15K-$35K per placement

### 10. Work-Life Balance Expectations
**Pain:** Younger physicians demand better work-life balance
**Current Solution:** Traditional schedules
**Cost of Pain:** Generational mismatch, turnover
**Your Solution:** Lifestyle-focused opportunity marketing
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K per placement

### 11. Subspecialty Sourcing Challenges
**Pain:** Hard to find pediatric cardiologists, neurosurgeons
**Current Solution:** National job boards (limited reach)
**Cost of Pain:** Months-long searches for subspecialists
**Your Solution:** Fellowship program networking
**Pricing:** $30K-$50K per subspecialist placement

### 12. Locum Tenens Dependency
**Pain:** Over-reliance on expensive locum providers
**Current Solution:** Accept high locum costs
**Cost of Pain:** 2-3x permanent hire costs
**Your Solution:** Permanent placement focus
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K per permanent placement

### 13. Cultural Fit in Healthcare Teams
**Pain:** Clinical skills do not predict team integration
**Current Solution:** Focus only on credentials
**Cost of Pain:** Team dysfunction, patient care issues
**Your Solution:** Team culture assessment
**Pricing:** $18K-$35K per placement

### 14. Emergency Department Staffing
**Pain:** Critical ED staffing shortages
**Current Solution:** Expensive locum ED physicians
**Cost of Pain:** Diversion status, patient safety concerns
**Your Solution:** ED specialist network
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K per ED placement

### 15. Academic Medical Center Recruitment
**Pain:** Need clinician-researchers, teaching faculty
**Current Solution:** Academic job boards only
**Cost of Pain:** Limited applicant pools
**Your Solution:** Academic network, research opportunity marketing
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K per academic placement
