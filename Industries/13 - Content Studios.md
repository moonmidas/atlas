# 13 - Content Studios

**Category:** Media  
**Project Range:** $6K-$45K+  
**Timeline:** 2-6 months to scale  
**Business Model:** Retainer + project fees

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Content Studios = Scaled content production with stronger margins through format specialization. You build systems, templates, and teams that produce content at scale for brands and creators.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $10K-$30K (equipment, initial team) |
| Monthly Revenue | $20K-$100K+ |
| Clients | 5-15 simultaneously |
| Margin | 40-60% |
| Team | 3-10 people |

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

**Hook:** "We turn your expertise into [content format] at scale."

**Differentiation:**
- Speed (24-48 hour turnaround)
- Specialization (one format done well)
- Systems (templates, processes)
- Performance (engagement, revenue metrics)

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Clients who want "just post for us" (no strategy)
- ❌ Price shoppers (content is investment, not cost)
- ❌ No clear approval process (endless revisions)
- ❌ Unrealistic expectations (viral guarantees)
- ❌ No access to subject matter experts
