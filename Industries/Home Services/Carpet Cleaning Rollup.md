# Carpet Cleaning Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Carpet Cleaning Business Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $1M-$5M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, service diversification

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The carpet cleaning industry is $5B+ with massive fragmentation. Low barriers to entry create commoditization. Rollup opportunity from brand building, service diversification (tile, upholstery, air ducts), and operational efficiency.

**Market Size:** $5B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $300K-$1.5M revenue  
**Examples:** Stanley Steemer franchises, Chem-Dry, local consolidators

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, multi-service platforms  
**Budget:** $1M-$5M  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Commoditization Pressure
**Pain:** $99 whole house specials devalue the industry.
**Current Solution:** Compete on price or quality
**Cost of Pain:** Margin compression
**Your Solution:** Premium brand positioning

### 2. Service Diversification
**Pain:** Carpet only = limited revenue. Tile, upholstery, ducts needed.
**Current Solution:** Basic add-on services
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$150K missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform service menu

### 3. Equipment Investment
**Pain:** Truck mounts cost $30K-$50K. Maintenance constant.
**Current Solution:** Finance, repair
**Cost of Pain:** Equipment downtime
**Your Solution:** Platform fleet management

### 4. Chemical Costs
**Pain:** Cleaning solutions, protectors, deodorizers add up.
**Current Solution:** Buy retail
**Cost of Pain:** 20-30% premium
**Your Solution:** Platform purchasing

### 5. Technician Consistency
**Pain:** Quality varies wildly between techs.
**Current Solution:** Training, supervision
**Cost of Pain:** Callbacks, bad reviews
**Your Solution:** Platform standards

### 6. IICRC Certification
**Pain:** Certification builds credibility but requires investment.
**Current Solution:** Owner certifies
**Cost of Pain:** Training costs
**Your Solution:** Platform certification

### 7. Up-selling Difficulty
**Pain:** Protector, deodorizer increase ticket but customers resist.
**Current Solution:** Tech attempts upsell
**Cost of Pain:** Low average ticket
**Your Solution:** Platform sales training

### 8. Rebooking Rates
**Pain:** Most customers clean once every 2-3 years.
**Current Solution:** Reminder cards
**Cost of Pain:** High CAC dependency
**Your Solution:** Maintenance programs

### 9. Real Estate Opportunities
**Pain:** Move-out cleaning is profitable but seasonal.
**Current Solution:** Agent relationships
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent flow
**Your Solution:** Platform realtor programs

### 10. Commercial Contracts
**Pain:** Commercial work is steady but requires scale.
**Current Solution:** Small commercial jobs
**Cost of Pain:** Missed contracts
**Your Solution:** Platform commercial sales

### 11. Air Duct Cleaning
**Pain:** NADCA certification required. Equipment expensive.
**Current Solution:** Refer out
**Cost of Pain:** $75K-$200K missed
**Your Solution:** Platform capabilities

### 12. Water Damage Crossover
**Pain:** Carpet cleaners see water damage first.
**Current Solution:** Refer to restoration
**Cost of Pain:** Missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform integration

### 13. Route Density
**Pain:** Drive time between scattered jobs.
**Current Solution:** Accept inefficiency
**Cost of Pain:** 25-30% labor waste
**Your Solution:** Route optimization

### 14. Seasonal Patterns
**Pain:** Spring cleaning rush, winter slowdown.
**Current Solution:** Accept seasonality
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow challenges
**Your Solution:** Service mix, geography

### 15. Exit Value
**Pain:** Carpet cleaning businesses sell for low multiples.
**Current Solution:** Accept low offers
**Cost of Pain:** 1.5-2.5x multiples
**Your Solution:** Platform exit premium
