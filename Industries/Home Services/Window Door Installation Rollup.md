# Window/Door Installation Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Window/Door Company Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $3M-$15M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, in-home sales focus

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The window and door replacement industry is $20B+ with moderate fragmentation. High ticket sales ($5K-$30K average), energy efficiency trends, and aging housing stock drive demand. Rollup opportunity from sales process optimization and installation scale.

**Market Size:** $20B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $1M-$4M revenue  
**Examples:** Renewal by Andersen, Pella, Window World

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, building products platforms  
**Budget:** $3M-$15M  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium-High

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. In-Home Sales Complexity
**Pain:** One-call close model requires skilled salespeople. Hard to find/train.
**Current Solution:** Owner sells or commission reps
**Cost of Pain:** 35-50% commission splits
**Your Solution:** Platform sales system, training

### 2. Lead Generation Costs
**Pain:** Home shows, canvassing, digital ads. CAC $500-$1,000.
**Current Solution:** Mix of channels
**Cost of Pain:** High marketing spend
**Your Solution:** Platform marketing efficiency

### 3. Installation Quality
**Pain:** Poor installation causes callbacks, warranty issues.
**Current Solution:** Train crews, hope for best
**Cost of Pain:** 8-12% callback rate
**Your Solution:** Platform installation standards

### 4. Manufacturer Relationships
**Pain:** Dealer programs require volume commitments.
**Current Solution:** Purchase through distributors
**Cost of Pain:** 10-20% product premium
**Your Solution:** Platform dealer status

### 5. Financing Programs
**Pain:** Customers need financing for $10K+ projects.
**Current Solution:** Third-party financing
**Cost of Pain:** 5-10% financing fees
**Your Solution:** Platform financing

### 6. Permit and Code Compliance
**Pain:** Windows require permits in many jurisdictions.
**Current Solution:** Handle individually
**Cost of Pain:** Administrative burden
**Your Solution:** Platform compliance

### 7. Subcontractor Management
**Pain:** Installation crews often subcontracted.
**Current Solution:** Work with available crews
**Cost of Pain:** Quality inconsistency
**Your Solution:** Platform crew standards

### 8. Seasonal Demand
**Pain:** Spring/fall peaks. Winter slow.
**Current Solution:** Accept seasonality
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow swings
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification

### 9. Warranty Liability
**Pain:** Lifetime warranties create long-term exposure.
**Current Solution:** Hope no major claims
**Cost of Pain:** Unpredictable liability
**Your Solution:** Platform warranty management

### 10. Showroom Investment
**Pain:** Customers want to see products. Showrooms expensive.
**Current Solution:** In-home samples
**Cost of Pain:** Limited customer experience
**Your Solution:** Platform showrooms

### 11. AIA/Commercial Work
**Pain:** Architects specify windows. Large projects available.
**Current Solution:** Focus residential
**Cost of Pain:** Missed commercial
**Your Solution:** Platform commercial capabilities

### 12. Patio Door Opportunities
**Pain:** Sliding doors, French doors. Related to window work.
**Current Solution:** Occasional sales
**Cost of Pain:** Underutilized
**Your Solution:** Platform product mix

### 13. Entry Door Cross-Sell
**Pain:** Front doors are natural add-on.
**Current Solution:** Minimal focus
**Cost of Pain:** Missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform cross-selling

### 14. Energy Efficiency Trends
**Pain:** Customers want energy savings. Education required.
**Current Solution:** Basic energy pitch
**Cost of Pain:** Lost sales
**Your Solution:** Platform sales training

### 15. Exit Challenges
**Pain:** Sales-dependent business hard to transfer.
**Current Solution:** Accept lower value
**Cost of Pain:** 2-3x multiples
**Your Solution:** Platform exit
