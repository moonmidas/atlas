# Landscaping/Lawn Care Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Landscaping Company Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $3M-$20M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, seasonal optimization

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The landscaping industry is $100B+ with 500,000+ operators. Highly fragmented with strong recurring maintenance revenue potential. Rollup opportunity driven by route density, equipment scale, and year-round service diversification.

**Market Size:** $100B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $1M-$5M revenue  
**Examples:** BrightView, TruGreen, Grounds Guys franchises

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, facilities management platforms  
**Budget:** $3M-$20M  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium-High

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Seasonal Revenue Swings
**Pain:** Northern climates: 6-8 month season. Cash flow nightmares.
**Current Solution:** Snow removal, holiday lights, layoffs
**Cost of Pain:** $100K-$300K financing costs
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification, winter services

### 2. Equipment Capital Intensity
**Pain:** Mowers, trucks, trailers cost $100K+ per crew. Constant maintenance.
**Current Solution:** Finance purchases, repair constantly
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$150K annual equipment costs
**Your Solution:** Platform fleet management, leasing

### 3. Route Efficiency
**Pain:** crews drive 2-3 hours daily. Low density kills margins.
**Current Solution:** Accept inefficiency
**Cost of Pain:** 25-35% labor waste
**Your Solution:** Route optimization, density-focused M&A

### 4. Labor Shortages
**Pain:** H2B visa issues, seasonal workers, high turnover.
**Current Solution:** Pay premium wages, struggle to staff
**Cost of Pain:** $75K-$250K recruitment and overtime
**Your Solution:** Platform recruiting, retention programs

### 5. Weather Dependency
**Pain:** Rain delays cascade schedules. Cannot make up lost days.
**Current Solution:** Work weekends, extend season
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$150K lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Geographic spread, scheduling systems

### 6. Commercial Contract Bidding
**Pain:** Commercial work requires bonds, insurance, experience.
**Current Solution:** Stay residential
**Cost of Pain:** Missed $200K-$500K opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform commercial capabilities

### 7. Chemical and Fertilizer Costs
**Pain:** Input costs rising. Small volume = no pricing power.
**Current Solution:** Retail purchasing
**Cost of Pain:** 15-20% premium
**Your Solution:** Platform purchasing

### 8. Customer Retention
**Pain:** 20-25% annual churn. Price shoppers common.
**Current Solution:** Hope they renew
**Cost of Pain:** $100K-$300K lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform retention programs

### 9. Design/Build vs Maintenance Mix
**Pain:** Design/build is profitable but lumpy. Maintenance is steady.
**Current Solution:** Take what comes
**Cost of Pain:** Cash flow volatility
**Your Solution:** Balanced portfolio strategy

### 10. Irrigation Expertise
**Pain:** Irrigation work commands premium but requires expertise.
**Current Solution:** Refer out, miss revenue
**Cost of Pain:** $75K-$200K missed opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform irrigation capabilities

### 11. Tree Work Liability
**Pain:** Tree work is dangerous and liability-heavy.
**Current Solution:** Avoid or subcontract
**Cost of Pain:** Missed high-margin work
**Your Solution:** Platform safety and insurance

### 12. Snow Removal Risk
**Pain:** Snow is profitable but weather-dependent. Equipment sits idle.
**Current Solution:** Hope for snow
**Cost of Pain:** $30K-$80K equipment carrying costs
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification

### 13. Owner Seasonality
**Pain:** Owner works 80 hours/week in season, nothing in winter.
**Current Solution:** Accept the cycle
**Cost of Pain:** Burnout, health issues
**Your Solution:** Platform management

### 14. Estimating Complexity
**Pain:** Every property is different. Estimates take time.
**Current Solution:** Experience-based guessing
**Cost of Pain:** 10-15% margin variance
**Your Solution:** Platform estimating tools

### 15. Succession Planning
**Pain:** Hard to sell seasonal business.
**Current Solution:** Hope for the best
**Cost of Pain:** 30-50% value discount
**Your Solution:** Platform exit
