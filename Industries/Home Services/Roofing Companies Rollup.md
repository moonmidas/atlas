# Roofing Companies Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Roofing Contractor Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $5M-$30M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, platform value creation

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The roofing industry is a $50B+ market with extreme fragmentation. Rollup opportunity driven by storm restoration, insurance relationships, and supply chain scale.

**Market Size:** $50B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated contractors with $2M-$8M revenue  
**Examples:** RoofClaim.com, Aspen Contracting

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, storm restoration specialists  
**Budget:** $5M-$30M  
**Pain Urgency:** High

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Storm Demand Volatility
**Pain:** 70% of revenue from insurance restoration. Revenue swings wildly year to year.
**Current Solution:** Chase storms, layoff/rehire
**Cost of Pain:** $300K-$1M in lost revenue during slow years
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification
**Pricing:** 2-4x EBITDA

### 2. Crew Management
**Pain:** 1099 subcontractors. Hard to control quality.
**Current Solution:** Work with whoever shows up
**Cost of Pain:** 10-15% callback rate, $200K-$600K warranty costs
**Your Solution:** Platform crew standards
**Pricing:** Reducing callbacks saves $150K-$400K annually

### 3. Insurance Adjuster Relationships
**Pain:** No relationships means no storm revenue.
**Current Solution:** Build personal relationships
**Cost of Pain:** $200K-$800K lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform insurance relationships
**Pricing:** Improved capture = $300K-$1M annually

### 4. Material Cost Volatility
**Pain:** No pricing power with suppliers.
**Current Solution:** Pay retail prices
**Cost of Pain:** 10-15% premium = $200K-$600K annually
**Your Solution:** Platform purchasing power
**Pricing:** $150K-$500K savings annually

### 5. Sales Commission Structure
**Pain:** 100% commission, high turnover.
**Current Solution:** Pay competitive splits
**Cost of Pain:** $150K-$400K recruitment costs
**Your Solution:** Platform sales infrastructure
**Pricing:** Improved productivity = $200K-$600K

### 6. Safety and Liability
**Pain:** Falls are leading cause of construction deaths.
**Current Solution:** Basic safety training
**Cost of Pain:** Workers comp 15-25% of payroll
**Your Solution:** Platform safety programs
**Pricing:** 20-30% cost reduction

### 7. Warranty Management
**Pain:** Warranties run 10-50 years. Callbacks are expensive.
**Current Solution:** Fix reactively
**Cost of Pain:** 3-5% of revenue = $100K-$400K annually
**Your Solution:** Platform quality standards
**Pricing:** 50% cost reduction

### 8. Reputation Management
**Pain:** Industry has terrible reputation.
**Current Solution:** Hope for good reviews
**Cost of Pain:** $150K-$500K lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform brand standards
**Pricing:** 15-25% close rate improvement

### 9. Financing Terms
**Pain:** Roofs cost $10K-$50K. Insurance pays in 30-90 days.
**Current Solution:** Refer to lenders
**Cost of Pain:** 2-4% financing costs
**Your Solution:** Platform financing programs

### 10. Project Management
**Pain:** Complex projects with permits, materials, crews.
**Current Solution:** Whiteboards and spreadsheets
**Cost of Pain:** 15-20% schedule overruns
**Your Solution:** Platform project management
**Pricing:** $100K-$400K efficiency gains

### 11. Geographic Expansion
**Pain:** Local reputation does not transfer.
**Current Solution:** Occasional storm chasing
**Cost of Pain:** Growth ceiling, $200K-$800K missed opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform brand systems

### 12. Seasonal Cash Flow
**Pain:** Northern climates lose 3-4 months.
**Current Solution:** Credit lines
**Cost of Pain:** $75K-$250K financing costs
**Your Solution:** Platform cash management

### 13. Quality Control
**Pain:** Quality varies by crew.
**Current Solution:** Fix what inspectors find
**Cost of Pain:** 5-10% jobs need rework
**Your Solution:** Platform quality standards
**Pricing:** 50% rework reduction

### 14. Succession Planning
**Pain:** Hard to sell roofing businesses.
**Current Solution:** Hope something works out
**Cost of Pain:** 40-60% discount on value
**Your Solution:** Premium exit with platform
**Pricing:** $500K-$2M preserved value

### 15. Technology Gap
**Pain:** Minimal technology usage.
**Current Solution:** Excel and paper
**Cost of Pain:** 15-20 hours/week waste
**Your Solution:** Platform technology stack
