# Gutter Services Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Gutter Company Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $1M-$4M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, seasonal smoothing

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The gutter services industry is $3B+ with high fragmentation. Essential maintenance prevents water damage. Highly seasonal (fall/spring peaks). Rollup opportunity from operational smoothing, guard installation, and cross-selling with roofing.

**Market Size:** $3B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $300K-$1M revenue  
**Examples:** LeafFilter, local operators

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, roofing platforms  
**Budget:** $1M-$4M  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Extreme Seasonality
**Pain:** Fall leaf season = 60% of revenue. Spring secondary. Rest is slow.
**Current Solution:** Accept seasonality
**Cost of Pain:** $30K-$80K winter shortfall
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification

### 2. Ladder Safety Risk
**Pain:** Falls from ladders serious. Liability exposure high.
**Current Solution:** Insurance, training
**Cost of Pain:** High workers comp rates
**Your Solution:** Platform safety programs

### 3. Gutter Guard Upsell
**Pain:** Gutter guards eliminate recurring cleaning. High ticket.
**Current Solution:** Occasional sales
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$150K missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform sales focus

### 4. Repair vs Replace
**Pain:** Repairs are quick but customers often need replacement.
**Current Solution:** Quote both
**Cost of Pain:** Missed upsells
**Your Solution:** Platform sales training

### 5. Material Options
**Pain:** Aluminum, copper, steel, vinyl. Options confuse customers.
**Current Solution:** Offer standard options
**Cost of Pain:** Analysis paralysis
**Your Solution:** Platform sales process

### 6. Downspout Routing
**Pain:** Water must drain properly. Grading issues common.
**Current Solution:** Basic routing
**Cost of Pain:** Callbacks for drainage
**Your Solution:** Platform standards

### 7. Fascia and Soffit Work
**Pain:** Water damage often extends to fascia/soffit.
**Current Solution:** Refer to carpenters
**Cost of Pain:** Missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform carpentry

### 8. Roof Integration
**Pain:** Gutters meet roofing. Natural cross-sell.
**Current Solution:** Occasional roofing referrals
**Cost of Pain:** Missed opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform roofing integration

### 9. Pressure Washing Add-on
**Pain:** Exterior cleaning natural complement.
**Current Solution:** Occasional upsell
**Cost of Pain:** $25K-$75K missed
**Your Solution:** Platform service menu

### 10. Holiday Light Installation
**Pain:** Use ladders/equipment in winter. Off-season revenue.
**Current Solution:** Some offer lights
**Cost of Pain:** Inconsistent offering
**Your Solution:** Platform winter services

### 11. Leaf Removal Bundling
**Pain:** Fall leaf removal pairs with gutter cleaning.
**Current Solution:** Separate services
**Cost of Pain:** Missed bundling
**Your Solution:** Platform bundling

### 12. Route Efficiency
**Pain:** Drive time between scattered jobs.
**Current Solution:** Accept inefficiency
**Cost of Pain:** 30-40% labor waste
**Your Solution:** Route optimization

### 13. Equipment Simplicity
**Pain:** Trucks, ladders, blowers. Basic but maintenance needed.
**Current Solution:** Maintain individually
**Cost of Pain:** Downtime costs
**Your Solution:** Platform fleet mgmt

### 14. Weather Dependency
**Pain:** Rain/snow delays work.
**Current Solution:** Compress schedule
**Cost of Pain:** Overtime, burnout
**Your Solution:** Scheduling flexibility

### 15. Exit Value
**Pain:** Small, seasonal business. Low multiples.
**Current Solution:** Accept low offers
**Cost of Pain:** 2-3x multiples
**Your Solution:** Platform exit premium
