# Water Damage Restoration Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Restoration Company Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $3M-$20M

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The restoration industry is $20B+ with high fragmentation. Insurance-driven revenue, emergency response, and recurring weather events create stable demand. Rollup opportunity from insurance relationships and operational scale.

**Market Size:** $20B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $1M-$5M revenue  
**Examples:** ServiceMaster Restore, BELFOR, SERVPRO franchises

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, insurance restoration specialists  
**Budget:** $3M-$20M  
**Pain Urgency:** High

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Insurance Program Access
**Pain:** Major carriers have preferred vendor programs. Hard to access without scale.
**Current Solution:** Work TPA programs, direct referrals
**Cost of Pain:** $200K-$800K lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform insurance relationships

### 2. 24/7 Emergency Response
**Pain:** Water damage requires immediate response. 2 AM calls common.
**Current Solution:** Owner on-call, rotating staff
**Cost of Pain:** Burnout, turnover
**Your Solution:** Platform call center, scheduled response

### 3. Equipment Capital Intensity
**Pain:** Dehumidifiers, air movers, generators. $200K+ per crew.
**Current Solution:** Finance purchases
**Cost of Pain:** Equipment carrying costs
**Your Solution:** Platform fleet management

### 4. Large Loss Capability
**Pain:** Commercial losses require scale. Small operators excluded.
**Current Solution:** Refer to nationals
**Cost of Pain:** Missed $100K-$500K opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform large loss team

### 5. Mold Remediation License
**Pain:** Mold requires separate certification. Complicated regulations.
**Current Solution:** Refer out, miss revenue
**Cost of Pain:** $75K-$200K lost
**Your Solution:** Platform capabilities

### 6. Contents Restoration
**Pain:** Pack-outs, cleaning, storage. Profitable but complex.
**Current Solution:** Refer to specialists
**Cost of Pain:** Missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform contents division

### 7. Reconstruction Services
**Pain:** Repair work after mitigation. $2-$4 per $1 of mitigation.
**Current Solution:** Refer to contractors
**Cost of Pain:** Massive missed revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform reconstruction

### 8. Weather Event Scalability
**Pain:** Hurricanes/floods overwhelm capacity.
**Current Solution:** Decline work, refer out
**Cost of Pain:** Missed catastrophe revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform resource sharing

### 9. Moisture Mapping Expertise
**Pain:** Proper documentation required for insurance.
**Current Solution:** Basic training
**Cost of Pain:** Claim denials
**Your Solution:** Platform training, standards

### 10. IICRC Certification
**Pain:** Industry certification is expected.
**Current Solution:** Owner maintains certs
**Cost of Pain:** Training burden
**Your Solution:** Platform certification

### 11. TPA Relationships
**Pain:** Third-party administrators control work.
**Current Solution:** Apply individually
**Cost of Pain:** Slow approval
**Your Solution:** Platform TPA access

### 12. Deductible Collection
**Pain:** Collecting deductibles is awkward.
**Current Solution:** Bill customer
**Cost of Pain:** Collection issues
**Your Solution:** Platform billing

### 13. Storage Facility Needs
**Pain:** Contents require warehouse space.
**Current Solution:** Lease space
**Cost of Pain:** Fixed overhead
**Your Solution:** Platform facilities

### 14. Response Time Pressure
**Pain:** Insurance requires 1-4 hour response.
**Current Solution:** Rush, stress
**Cost of Pain:** Quality issues
**Your Solution:** Platform coverage

### 15. Industry Competition
**Pain:** SERVPRO, ServiceMaster dominate marketing.
**Current Solution:** Local reputation
**Cost of Pain:** Lead cost inflation
**Your Solution:** Platform marketing
