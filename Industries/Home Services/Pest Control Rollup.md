# Pest Control Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Pest Control Business Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $2M-$15M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, recurring revenue platform

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The pest control industry generates $20B+ annually with 25,000+ operators. It is uniquely positioned for rollups with high recurring revenue (80%+ from monthly/quarterly service contracts), essential services status, and strong unit economics (20-30% EBITDA margins).

**Market Size:** $20B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $500K-$3M revenue  
**Examples:** Rollins (Orkin), ServiceMaster (Terminix), Anticimex rollups

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, pest control specialists  
**Budget:** $2M-$15M  
**Pain Urgency:** High

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Customer Acquisition Costs
**Pain:** CAC rising 15% annually. Door-to-door declining. Digital marketing expensive.
**Current Solution:** Door knocking, Google Ads, referrals
**Cost of Pain:** $300-$600 per new customer
**Your Solution:** Platform marketing, referral programs, cross-selling
**Pricing:** 3-5x EBITDA

### 2. Technician Retention
**Pain:** 40% annual turnover. Training costs $5K-$10K per tech.
**Current Solution:** Pay above market, hope they stay
**Cost of Pain:** $75K-$200K annually
**Your Solution:** Platform career paths, benefits, training
**Pricing:** 50% turnover reduction

### 3. Route Density
**Pain:** Technicians drive 100+ miles daily. Low route density kills margins.
**Current Solution:** Accept long drives, inefficient routing
**Cost of Pain:** 20-30% labor waste
**Your Solution:** Platform route optimization, acquisition targeting
**Pricing:** 15-20% efficiency gain

### 4. Seasonal Demand Swings
**Pain:** Spring/summer peaks. Winter valleys. Cash flow challenges.
**Current Solution:** Year-round contracts, winter services
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$150K financing costs
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification, service mix

### 5. Regulatory Complexity
**Pain:** EPA, state licenses, chemical handling. Violations are serious.
**Current Solution:** Training, compliance tracking
**Cost of Pain:** $25K-$75K compliance costs
**Your Solution:** Platform compliance management

### 6. Chemical Procurement
**Pain:** Small volume = retail pricing. Supply chain issues.
**Current Solution:** Local distributors
**Cost of Pain:** 15-20% material premium
**Your Solution:** Platform purchasing power
**Pricing:** 10-15% cost reduction

### 7. Contract Churn
**Pain:** 25-30% annual churn. Hard to grow recurring base.
**Current Solution:** Hope customers renew
**Cost of Pain:** $100K-$400K lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform retention programs

### 8. Service Consistency
**Pain:** Quality varies by technician. Callbacks expensive.
**Current Solution:** Training, hope for best
**Cost of Pain:** 8-12% callback rate
**Your Solution:** Platform standards, QA

### 9. Termite Warranty Risk
**Pain:** Termite bonds create long-term liability. Claims unpredictable.
**Current Solution:** Insurance, reserves
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$200K warranty exposure
**Your Solution:** Platform risk pooling

### 10. Technology Gap
**Pain:** Paper routes, manual billing. No CRM.
**Current Solution:** Basic software
**Cost of Pain:** 10-15 hours/week waste
**Your Solution:** Platform field service software

### 11. Commercial vs Residential Mix
**Pain:** Residential is steady but competitive. Commercial is complex.
**Current Solution:** Focus on easier segment
**Cost of Pain:** Missed diversification
**Your Solution:** Platform commercial capabilities

### 12. M&A Competition
**Pain:** Orkin/Terminix buying everyone. Hard to compete.
**Current Solution:** Hope they do not target your market
**Cost of Pain:** Margin pressure
**Your Solution:** Regional platform defense

### 13. Owner Dependence
**Pain:** Business relies on owner relationships.
**Current Solution:** Keep working
**Cost of Pain:** No exit path
**Your Solution:** Platform transition

### 14. Training Burden
**Pain:** NPMA certification, state requirements, ongoing education.
**Current Solution:** Handle individually
**Cost of Pain:** $20K-$50K annually
**Your Solution:** Platform training infrastructure

### 15. Capital Constraints
**Pain:** Cannot fund growth, equipment, marketing.
**Current Solution:** Slow growth, credit lines
**Cost of Pain:** Missed opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform capital
