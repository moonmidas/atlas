# Garage Door Services Rollup

**Category:** Home Services  
**Sub-Category:** Garage Door Company Rollups  
**Entry Cost:** $1.5M-$8M  
**Timeline:** 12-24 months  
**Revenue Model:** Buy-and-build, emergency service focus

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The garage door industry is $3B+ with high fragmentation. Emergency repair focus creates premium pricing. Strong recurring maintenance potential. Rollup opportunity from operational scale and inventory leverage.

**Market Size:** $3B+ US market  
**Target:** Owner-operated with $500K-$2M revenue  
**Examples:** Precision Door, Overhead Door dealers

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Private equity, multi-trade platforms  
**Budget:** $1.5M-$8M  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Emergency Call Intensity
**Pain:** 60% of calls are emergencies. 24/7 availability required. Burnout high.
**Current Solution:** Owner takes calls, rotating on-call
**Cost of Pain:** 60-80 hour weeks, family strain
**Your Solution:** Platform call center, scheduled on-call

### 2. Parts Inventory Complexity
**Pain:** 50+ door manufacturers, 100+ opener models. Impossible to stock everything.
**Current Solution:** Common parts on truck, order rest
**Cost of Pain:** 20-30% callbacks for parts
**Your Solution:** Platform inventory management

### 3. Spring Break Seasonality
**Pain:** Spring = high demand. Rest of year is slow.
**Current Solution:** Accept seasonality
**Cost of Pain:** $50K-$100K financing needs
**Your Solution:** Geographic diversification

### 4. Technician Training
**Pain:** Garage doors are dangerous (springs). Training is critical.
**Current Solution:** Shadow experienced techs
**Cost of Pain:** Safety liability, callbacks
**Your Solution:** Platform training programs

### 5. Manufacturer Relationships
**Pain:** Dealer programs require volume. Small operators pay retail.
**Current Solution:** Retail pricing
**Cost of Pain:** 15-25% parts premium
**Your Solution:** Platform dealer status

### 6. Sales Commission Structure
**Pain:** New door sales are commission-heavy. Inconsistent salespeople.
**Current Solution:** Owner sells or commission reps
**Cost of Pain:** 30-40% commission splits
**Your Solution:** Platform sales system

### 7. Safety Liability
**Pain:** Springs under tension. Doors are heavy. Injury risk high.
**Current Solution:** Insurance, training
**Cost of Pain:** High workers comp rates
**Your Solution:** Platform safety programs

### 8. Call Center Needs
**Pain:** Customers expect immediate answer. Owner cannot scale.
**Current Solution:** Owner answers, voicemail
**Cost of Pain:** Lost calls = lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Platform call center

### 9. Installation Quality
**Pain:** Poor installation causes callbacks. Warranty issues.
**Current Solution:** Hope techs do good work
**Cost of Pain:** 10-15% callback rate
**Your Solution:** Platform quality standards

### 10. Opener Technology
**Pain:** Smart home integration, WiFi openers, battery backup. Constant change.
**Current Solution:** Learn as you go
**Cost of Pain:** Lost sales to competitors
**Your Solution:** Platform training, certification

### 11. Commercial Door Work
**Pain:** Commercial doors are different. Dock equipment, rolling steel.
**Current Solution:** Refer out
**Cost of Pain:** Missed $100K-$300K opportunities
**Your Solution:** Platform commercial capabilities

### 12. Marketing Efficiency
**Pain:** Hard to differentiate. Price shoppers common.
**Current Solution:** Google Ads, Yellow Pages
**Cost of Pain:** $400-$800 CAC
**Your Solution:** Platform marketing

### 13. Warranty Management
**Pain:** Manufacturer + labor warranties. Tracking is hard.
**Current Solution:** Paper records, hope for best
**Cost of Pain:** Unpaid warranty work
**Your Solution:** Platform warranty tracking

### 14. Seasonal Cash Flow
**Pain:** Spring rush, winter slow. Working capital challenges.
**Current Solution:** Credit lines
**Cost of Pain:** Interest costs
**Your Solution:** Platform working capital

### 15. Exit Challenges
**Pain:** Small business, hard to sell.
**Current Solution:** Hope someone buys
**Cost of Pain:** 40-60% value discount
**Your Solution:** Platform exit
