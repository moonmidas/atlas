# 11 - Medical Practices

**Category:** Local  
**Project Range:** $12K-$70K+  
**Timeline:** 6-18 months to profitability  
**Business Model:** Fee-for-service + value-based contracts

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Medical Practices = High-value local operators with admin drag, no-show costs, and revenue leakage. Recession-resistant, aging population tailwinds, but operational complexity creates opportunity for improvement.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $250K-$1.5M (equipment, buildout, working capital) |
| Revenue per Provider | $300K-$800K/year |
| EBITDA Margin | 15-35% |
| Patient Volume | 1,500-4,000 patients per provider |
| Insurance Mix | 60-80% insurance, 20-40% cash |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Revenue Optimization:**
- Insurance contract negotiation
- Procedure mix optimization
- Cash pay service tiers

**Operational Excellence:**
- Scheduling efficiency
- No-show reduction
- Coding optimization

**Patient Experience:**
- Online scheduling
- Text reminders
- Portal access

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Heavy Medicare dependence (reimbursement risk)
- ❌ Unfavorable payer mix
- ❌ Provider burnout/high turnover
- ❌ Poor online reputation
- ❌ Regulatory compliance gaps
