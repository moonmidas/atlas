## The Opportunity

ESG has shifted from nice-to-have to business imperative. Investors, customers, and regulators demand sustainability reporting and action. Most companies lack expertise to navigate ESG frameworks, measure impact, and communicate progress. This creates high-value consulting opportunities.

**Market Size:** $8B+ ESG consulting market, growing 20% annually  
**Target:** Public companies, PE portfolio companies, B2B enterprises  
**Target Pricing:** $25K-$60K per engagement

**Can our legal team handle this?**
Legal ensures compliance. We provide strategic sustainability expertise and implementation support.
