# Exit Readiness Consulting

**Category:** Specialty Consulting  
**Sub-Category:** Transaction Advisory  
**Entry Cost:** $8K-$20K  
**Timeline:** 6-12 months  
**Revenue Model:** Fixed fee + success fee tied to exit value

---

## The Opportunity

Founders spend years building companies but often unprepared when exit opportunities arise. Poor preparation means lower valuations, failed deals, or distressed sales. Exit readiness consulting helps companies prepare 12-24 months before exit to maximize value and ensure smooth transactions.

**Market Size:** $5B+ transaction advisory market  
**Target:** $10M+ revenue companies considering sale in 1-3 years  
**Target Pricing:** $30K-$75K + 1-3% of exit value
