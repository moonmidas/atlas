## The Opportunity

Startups often operate without clear product-market fit (PMF) signals. They burn cash on growth before confirming fit, or pivot endlessly without data. A systematic PMF diagnostic helps companies understand where they stand and what to do next—whether double down, pivot, or shut down. This is high-value for early-stage companies with limited runway.

**Market Size:** $3B+ startup advisory market  
**Target:** Seed to Series A startups, product teams  
**Target Pricing:** $15K-$40K per engagement
