# Regulatory and Compliance Strategy

**Category:** Specialty Consulting  
**Sub-Category:** Risk Management  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 8-16 weeks  
**Revenue Model:** Fixed project fee with ongoing advisory

## The Opportunity

Regulatory requirements are expanding across industries—GDPR, CCPA, SOX, industry-specific regulations. Non-compliance carries severe penalties and reputational damage. Most companies lack specialized regulatory expertise internally and need external guidance to navigate complex requirements efficiently.

**Market Size:** $12B+ regulatory consulting market  
**Target:** Regulated industries, growth companies entering compliance phases  
**Target Pricing:** $25K-$60K per engagement

**Current Solution:** Ad-hoc responses to issues
**Cost:** Regulatory findings, enforcement risk
**Your Solution:** Compliance program design
