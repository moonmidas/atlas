# Market Entry Strategy

**Category:** Specialty Consulting  
**Sub-Category:** Growth Strategy  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 8-16 weeks  
**Revenue Model:** Fixed project fee with optional implementation support

---

## The Opportunity

Companies seeking growth must enter new markets—whether geographic expansion, vertical moves, or new customer segments. Market entry is high-risk and expensive if done wrong. Most companies underestimate the complexity and fail to develop systematic entry strategies. This consulting de-risks expansion decisions.

**Market Size:** $7B+ market entry and expansion consulting  
**Target:** Growth-stage companies, PE portfolio companies  
**Target Pricing:** $30K-$70K per engagement

---

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Market Selection Analysis Missing
**Pain:** Entering markets based on gut feel or opportunistic inbound
**Current Solution:** Basic market sizing, anecdotal research
**Cost:** Wrong market choice, wasted investment
**Your Solution:** Market attractiveness framework, scoring model
**Pricing:** $35K-$65K

### 2. Competitive Landscape Blindness
**Pain:** Underestimating local competition
**Current Solution:** Google searches, industry reports
**Cost:** Price wars, inability to differentiate
**Your Solution:** Competitive intelligence, positioning strategy
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 3. Regulatory and Compliance Complexity
**Pain:** Unknown regulatory requirements in new market
**Current Solution:** Learn as you go, hire local lawyer
**Cost:** Fines, delays, market exit
**Your Solution:** Regulatory roadmap, compliance checklist
**Pricing:** $35K-$60K

### 4. Localization Requirements
**Pain:** Product-market fit in home market does not translate
**Current Solution:** Minimal localization, hope it works
**Cost:** Low adoption, reputation damage
**Your Solution:** Localization strategy, adaptation roadmap
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 5. Go-to-Market Model Unclear
**Pain:** Direct sales, partners, or digital unclear for new market
**Current Solution:** Copy home market approach
**Cost:** Wrong channel, slow growth
**Your Solution:** Channel strategy, partner assessment
**Pricing:** $35K-$65K

### 6. Pricing Strategy Misalignment
**Pain:** Wrong price points for new market economics
**Current Solution:** Currency conversion, competitor matching
**Cost:** Margin erosion or volume shortfall
**Your Solution:** Market-based pricing analysis
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 7. Talent and Organization Gaps
**Pain:** No local team, hiring from afar difficult
**Current Solution:** Expat deployment, delayed hiring
**Cost:** Slow start, cultural missteps
**Your Solution:** Market entry org design, hiring plan
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 8. Partnership Evaluation
**Pain:** Need local partners but evaluation ad-hoc
**Current Solution:** Reactive to inbound partner interest
**Cost:** Bad partnerships, lost time
**Your Solution:** Partner assessment framework
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 9. Market Entry Sequencing
**Pain:** Trying to enter everywhere at once
**Current Solution:** Big bang launch
**Cost:** Resource strain, execution failure
**Your Solution:** Phased entry roadmap
**Pricing:** $35K-$60K

### 10. Risk Assessment Incomplete
**Pain:** Underestimating market entry risks
**Current Solution:** Generic business case
**Cost:** Unanticipated challenges, write-offs
**Your Solution:** Risk assessment matrix, mitigation plans
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 11. Investment Requirements Unclear
**Pain:** Underestimating capital needs for entry
**Current Solution:** Rough estimates, hope for efficiency
**Cost:** Cash crunches, underfunded launches
**Your Solution:** Investment model, funding requirements
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 12. Success Metrics Missing
**Pain:** No clear KPIs to judge entry success
**Current Solution:** Revenue growth only
**Cost:** Continue losing investments too long
**Your Solution:** Entry metrics dashboard
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K

### 13. Exit Strategy Absent
**Pain:** No plan if market entry fails
**Current Solution:** Hope it does not happen
**Cost:** Trapped in bad markets, high exit costs
**Your Solution:** Entry exit planning
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 14. Cultural and Language Barriers
**Pain:** Home market playbook fails abroad
**Current Solution:** Hire bilingual staff
**Cost:** Miscommunication, cultural offense
**Your Solution:** Cultural adaptation strategy
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 15. Supply Chain and Operations
**Pain:** Serving new markets from existing infrastructure
**Current Solution:** Export model, long lead times
**Cost:** Poor service, high costs
**Your Solution:** Supply chain localization plan
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K
