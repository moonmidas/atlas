## 🎯 The Opportunity

Pricing optimization for B2B companies leaving 20-40% on table. Most companies set prices based on costs or competitors, not value.

**Market Size:** Every B2B company  
**Target:** $2M-$50M B2B, underoptimized pricing  
**Examples:** Pricing consultancy firms, independents

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Price Too Low ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Underpricing by 20-50% vs. value delivered
**Current Solution:** Annual increases (2-3%), cost-plus
**Cost of Pain:** Millions in lost revenue
**Your Solution:** Value-based pricing analysis
**Pricing:** $30K-$60K + 10-20% of first-year increase

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How did you set your current prices?"
- "What would happen if you raised prices 20%?"
- "What's your gross margin?"

- ❌ Companies with <50% gross margin (bigger problems)

- ❌ Price-sensitive buyers only
- ❌ Pre-product/market fit

**Best Prospects:**
- B2B SaaS
- Professional services
- Differentiated products
- Companies that haven't raised prices in 2+ years
