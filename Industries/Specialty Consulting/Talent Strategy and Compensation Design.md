# Talent Strategy and Compensation Design

**Category:** Specialty Consulting  
**Sub-Category:** Human Resources  
**Entry Cost:** $3K-$10K  
**Timeline:** 6-12 weeks  
**Revenue Model:** Fixed project fee

---

## The Opportunity

The war for talent has intensified. Companies struggle to attract, retain, and motivate employees while controlling costs. Compensation is often the largest expense but managed with outdated approaches.

**Market Size:** $10B+ HR consulting market  
**Target:** Growth-stage companies, professional services firms  
**Target Pricing:** $20K-$50K per engagement

---

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Compensation Misaligned with Market
**Pain:** Paying below market and losing talent, or overpaying
**Current Solution:** Annual salary surveys, cost-of-living adjustments
**Cost:** High turnover, inability to hire, inflated payroll
**Your Solution:** Market pricing analysis, compensation philosophy
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K

### 2. High-Performer Retention Crisis
**Pain:** Best people leaving for competitors
**Current Solution:** Counteroffers, exit interviews too late
**Cost:** $50K-$200K replacement cost per key hire
**Your Solution:** Retention risk analysis, stay interviews
**Pricing:** $25K-$40K

### 3. No Clear Career Progression
**Pain:** Employees see no growth path
**Current Solution:** Generic job descriptions, tenure-based promotions
**Cost:** Stagnation, turnover of ambitious talent
**Your Solution:** Career ladder design, competency frameworks
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K

### 4. Performance Management Broken
**Pain:** Annual reviews dreaded by all, no differentiation
**Current Solution:** Generic rating scales, forced rankings
**Cost:** Demotivation, unclear expectations, bias
**Your Solution:** Continuous performance framework, calibration
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 5. Incentive Plans Drive Wrong Behavior
**Pain:** Sales comp creates gaming, metrics misaligned
**Current Solution:** Excel spreadsheets, gut-feel adjustments
**Cost:** Unprofitable deals, team conflicts, quota misses
**Your Solution:** Incentive design, quota-setting methodology
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 6. Equity Compensation Confusion
**Pain:** Stock options misunderstood, not motivating
**Current Solution:** Standard 4-year vest, unclear communication
**Cost:** Dilution without retention benefit
**Your Solution:** Equity strategy, refresher grant policy
**Pricing:** $20K-$35K

### 7. Hiring Process Inefficient
**Pain:** 90+ day time-to-fill, poor candidate experience
**Current Solution:** Ad-hoc interviews, no scorecards
**Cost:** Lost candidates, bad hires, team frustration
**Your Solution:** Structured hiring process, interview training
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K

### 8. Pay Equity Issues
**Pain:** Unexplained pay disparities, legal risk
**Current Solution:** Ignore until someone complains
**Cost:** Litigation risk, morale issues, turnover
**Your Solution:** Pay equity audit, remediation roadmap
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 9. Remote Work Policy Chaos
**Pain:** Inconsistent policies, location-based pay confusion
**Current Solution:** Make it up as we go
**Cost:** Talent loss, geographic arbitrage problems
**Your Solution:** Remote work compensation strategy
**Pricing:** $15K-$30K

### 10. Manager Capability Gaps
**Pain:** People leave managers not companies
**Current Solution:** Promote best ICs, hope they figure it out
**Cost:** Team dysfunction, high turnover costs
**Your Solution:** Manager effectiveness program
**Pricing:** $30K-$50K

### 11. No Talent Pipeline
**Pain:** Key roles open with no internal candidates
**Current Solution:** External search for every opening
**Cost:** Expensive searches, cultural mismatches
**Your Solution:** Succession planning, development programs
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 12. Culture and Engagement Declining
**Pain:** Quiet quitting, disengagement, Glassdoor problems
**Current Solution:** Annual engagement survey
**Cost:** Productivity loss, reputation damage
**Your Solution:** Culture diagnostic, action planning
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K

### 13. Total Rewards Communication
**Pain:** Employees do not understand their full value
**Current Solution:** Pay stubs only, no total comp statements
**Cost:** Perception of underpayment, unappreciated benefits
**Your Solution:** Total rewards communication strategy
**Pricing:** $15K-$30K

### 14. Executive Compensation Scrutiny
**Pain:** Founders/executives over or underpaid vs market
**Current Solution:** Board compensation committee discussions
**Cost:** Retention risk, governance issues
**Your Solution:** Executive comp benchmarking, design
**Pricing:** $30K-$50K

### 15. HR Technology Chaos
**Pain:** Multiple systems, poor integration, bad data
**Current Solution:** Add point solutions as needed
**Cost:** Admin burden, reporting nightmares
**Your Solution:** HR tech stack assessment, roadmap
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K

### Authority Builders

### Differentiators

- Implementation support, not just analysis

## Objection Handling

**We have an HR team.**
We complement internal HR with specialized expertise and external benchmarks they lack access to.

**This is confidential information.**
We sign NDAs and work with your legal team. Discretion is our core competency.

**Can we just use PayScale or Glassdoor?**
Those are inputs, not strategy. We translate data into decisions and implementation.
