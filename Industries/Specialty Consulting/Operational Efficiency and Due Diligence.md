## The Opportunity

Private equity and venture capital firms need rapid, accurate operational assessments before closing deals. Most due diligence focuses on financials while operational risks go unexamined, creating high-value consulting opportunities.

**Market Size:** $15B+ due diligence and operational consulting market  
**Target:** PE/VC firms, strategic acquirers  
**Target Pricing:** $30K-$75K per deal

## Objection Handling

**We have McKinsey/Bain for DD.**
Strategy firms are excellent but often lack granular operational expertise. We complement them with deep operational analysis.

**Can our internal ops team do this?**
Internal teams bring bias and often lack cross-portfolio pattern recognition. External validation is valuable.

**This is expensive for a DD budget.**
Our fee is 0.1-0.2% of typical deal size. Finding one operational issue pays for the engagement 10x.
