# Customer Experience Transformation

**Category:** Specialty Consulting  
**Sub-Category:** Experience Design  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 12-24 weeks  
**Revenue Model:** Fixed project fee with implementation support

---

## The Opportunity

Customer experience has become the primary competitive differentiator. Companies with superior CX see 4-8% higher revenue than competitors. Yet most organizations manage CX reactively through support tickets rather than systematically designing experiences. This creates high-value consulting opportunities to transform customer journeys.

**Market Size:** $10B+ customer experience consulting market  
**Target:** B2B and B2C companies with $10M+ revenue  
**Target Pricing:** $25K-$60K per engagement
