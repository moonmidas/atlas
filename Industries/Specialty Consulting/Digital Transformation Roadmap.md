**Category:** Specialty Consulting  
**Sub-Category:** Technology Strategy  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 8-16 weeks

## The Opportunity

Legacy systems trap companies in inefficiency while nimble competitors leverage modern technology. Digital transformation is no longer optional but most organizations lack the roadmap to get from current state to future state without disrupting operations. This consulting guides companies through the journey.

**Market Size:** $25B+ digital transformation consulting market  
**Target:** Established companies with $20M+ revenue, legacy technology  
**Target Pricing:** $35K-$80K per engagement
