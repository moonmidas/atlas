## The Opportunity

Supply chain disruptions have made optimization a C-suite priority. Companies face inventory imbalances, supplier reliability issues, and logistics cost pressures. Most lack systematic supply chain expertise internally.

**Market Size:** $20B+ supply chain consulting market  
**Target:** Manufacturing, retail, distribution companies with $10M+ revenue  
**Target Pricing:** $25K-$60K + 10-20% of savings

## Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Inventory Imbalances
**Pain:** Stockouts on key items, excess on others
**Current Solution:** Gut-feel ordering, safety stock rules
**Cost:** 15-25% of inventory value suboptimal
**Your Solution:** Inventory optimization model, ABC analysis
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K + 15% of savings

### 2. Supplier Reliability Issues
**Pain:** Unpredictable lead times, quality inconsistencies
**Current Solution:** Dual sourcing, expediting when problems arise
**Cost:** Production delays, excess safety stock, expedite fees
**Your Solution:** Supplier scorecards, risk mapping, diversification
**Pricing:** $25K-$50K

### 3. Logistics Cost Escalation
**Pain:** Freight costs increasing 15-30% annually
**Current Solution:** Accepting carrier increases, RFPs every few years
**Cost:** Millions in excess freight spend
**Your Solution:** Transportation optimization, mode shift analysis
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K + 10% of freight savings

### 4. No Supply Chain Visibility
**Pain:** Blind spots from supplier to customer
**Current Solution:** Spreadsheets, periodic check-ins
**Cost:** Surprises, reactive firefighting, poor planning
**Your Solution:** SCOR framework, KPI dashboard, control towers
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 5. Demand Forecasting Failure
**Pain:** Forecast accuracy below 60%, bullwhip effect
**Current Solution:** Last year plus growth, sales forecasts
**Cost:** Excess inventory, stockouts, obsolescence
**Your Solution:** Statistical forecasting, S&OP process
**Pricing:** $35K-$60K

### 6. Warehouse Inefficiency
**Pain:** Picking errors, poor space utilization, slow throughput
**Current Solution:** Add headcount when volume increases
**Cost:** 20-40% labor cost premium vs best-in-class
**Your Solution:** Warehouse layout optimization, slotting
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 7. Single-Source Dependencies
**Pain:** Critical components from sole suppliers
**Current Solution:** Keep fingers crossed, emergency stockpiles
**Cost:** Production shutdown risk, negotiation weakness
**Your Solution:** Supplier diversification roadmap
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K

### 8. Quality Control Gaps
**Pain:** Incoming quality issues discovered late
**Current Solution:** Reject at receiving, supplier complaints
**Cost:** Rework, warranty claims, customer dissatisfaction
**Your Solution:** Supplier quality management system
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 9. Supply-Demand Mismatch
**Pain:** Capacity constraints or excess, poor planning
**Current Solution:** Overtime when busy, layoffs when slow
**Cost:** Premium labor costs or underutilized assets
**Your Solution:** Sales and Operations Planning (S&OP)
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 10. International Logistics Complexity
**Pain:** Customs delays, tariff confusion, compliance issues
**Current Solution:** Freight forwarders, customs brokers
**Cost:** Delays, penalties, landed cost uncertainty
**Your Solution:** Trade compliance optimization
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K

### 11. Last-Mile Delivery Inefficiency
**Pain:** High cost per delivery, poor customer experience
**Current Solution:** Traditional carriers, no optimization
**Cost:** 30-50% of total logistics cost in last mile
**Your Solution:** Route optimization, delivery consolidation
**Pricing:** $20K-$40K

### 12. Supplier Negotiation Weakness
**Pain:** Paying above-market prices, poor contract terms
**Current Solution:** Annual negotiations, incumbent preference
**Cost:** 5-15% excess procurement costs
**Your Solution:** Strategic sourcing, should-cost models
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K + 20% of savings

### 13. Sustainability and ESG Pressure
**Pain:** Scope 3 emissions tracking, sustainable sourcing demands
**Current Solution:** Ignore or basic carbon calculators
**Cost:** Customer churn, regulatory risk, reputation damage
**Your Solution:** Green supply chain strategy
**Pricing:** $30K-$55K

### 14. Digital Supply Chain Gaps
**Pain:** Manual processes, siloed systems, no real-time data
**Current Solution:** ERP reports, email coordination
**Cost:** Slow response, poor decisions, excess inventory
**Your Solution:** Supply chain digitization roadmap
**Pricing:** $35K-$60K

### 15. Risk Management Missing
**Pain:** No systematic risk identification or mitigation
**Current Solution:** Deal with disruptions as they happen
**Cost:** Catastrophic failures, business continuity gaps
**Your Solution:** Supply chain risk management framework
**Pricing:** $25K-$45K
