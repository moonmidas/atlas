# Cannabis Retail OS

**Category:** Vertical SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Cannabis/Retail  
**Entry Cost:** $150K-$400K  
**Timeline:** 6-12 months to market  
**Revenue Model:** Per dispensary monthly subscription

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

The legal cannabis industry has exploded to $30B+ annually in the US, with over 7,000 licensed dispensaries operating under intense regulatory scrutiny. Cannabis retailers face unique challenges: strict state-by-state compliance requirements (Metrc, BioTrack), cash-heavy operations, banking limitations, seed-to-sale tracking, and rapid market evolution. Most dispensaries rely on generic retail POS systems adapted for cannabis, or legacy cannabis software that is expensive, clunky, and poorly supported.

An all-in-one operating system built specifically for cannabis retail—with deep Metrc integration, compliance automation, inventory management, and wholesale purchasing—creates massive value by reducing compliance risk, improving operational efficiency, and enabling dispensaries to focus on customer experience rather than regulatory paperwork. The market is growing rapidly, has high willingness to pay, and is underserved by quality software.

**Market Size:** 7,000+ dispensaries in US, $500M+ TAM at target pricing  
**Target:** Independent dispensaries, small chains (2-10 locations), vertically integrated operators  
**Examples:** Dutchie ($1.7B valuation), Leaf Logix, Flowhub, MJ Freeway, Cova

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Metrc Compliance and Reporting ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Metrc (state tracking system) errors, reconciliation nightmares, audit risk, manual data entry, discrepancies
**Current Solution:** Standalone Metrc portal, manual manifest entry, end-of-day reconciliation, hoping for accuracy
**Cost of Pain:** Metrc discrepancy = $1,000-$10,000+ fine; pattern of errors = license suspension; manual reconciliation = 20-30 hours/week
**Your Solution:** Direct Metrc integration, automatic manifest creation, real-time reconciliation, discrepancy alerts, audit trail
**Pricing:** $199/month compliance module

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How many hours per week do you spend on Metrc reconciliation?"
- "Have you had any compliance violations or discrepancies?"
- "How confident are you in your current Metrc accuracy?"

### 2. Cash Handling and Banking

**Current Solution:** Cash vaults, manual counting, armed transport, workaround banking solutions
**Cost of Pain:** Cash handling labor 10-15 hours/week = $800-$1,200; security risk; audit trail gaps; lost productivity
**Your Solution:** Integrated cash drawer management, cash reconciliation, secure transport integration, cashless ATM support
**Pricing:** Included in base platform

**Discovery Questions:**

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Dispensaries with recent compliance violations or license suspensions (high risk)
- ❌ Operators without previous retail experience (learning curve too steep)
- ❌ Single-location with <$1M annual revenue (price sensitive)
- ❌ Dispensaries not using any compliance software (change-resistant)
- ❌ Operators prioritizing price over compliance (dangerous)

**Best Prospects:**
- Multi-location operators planning expansion
- Dispensaries with recent compliance issues seeking change
- Vertically integrated operators (cultivation + retail)
- Established dispensaries (2+ years) with stable revenue
- Operators frustrated with current software limitations

**"We are happy with Dutchie/Flowhub/our current system."**

How many hours do you spend on Metrc reconciliation?"

Plus, one Metrc violation fine pays for our system for a year."

**"We have been burned by cannabis software vendors before."**

We do not lock you into annual contracts. And we have a dedicated compliance team, not general support. Can I show you our compliance dashboard and how it prevents the issues you have had before?"

**"We do not have time to train staff on new software."**
