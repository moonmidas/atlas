# Salon & Spa Operating System

**Category:** Vertical SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Beauty/Wellness  
**Entry Cost:** $75K-$200K  
**Timeline:** 4-6 months to market  
**Revenue Model:** Per location monthly subscription

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Salons and spas operate on a unique business model that combines service delivery, retail sales, membership programs, and appointment-based scheduling. Most businesses rely on a mix of paper books, generic scheduling software, and basic POS systems that don't understand the complexities of the beauty industry: commission structures, service add-ons, product recommendations, and the critical importance of client relationships. An all-in-one platform built specifically for salons and spas can capture significant market share by solving industry-specific problems that generic software ignores.

The opportunity is particularly strong because salon/spa software is dominated by legacy players (Square, Acuity, MINDBODY) that lack modern features and charge premium prices. A fresh approach with better UX, integrated marketing, and modern client communication tools can win significant market share.

**Market Size:** 80K+ salons and spas in US, $1B+ TAM at target pricing  
**Target:** Independent salons/spas, small chains (2-5 locations), suite renters managing their own books  
**Examples:** Square Appointments, Acuity Scheduling, MINDBODY, Schedulicity, Vagaro

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Appointment Scheduling Chaos ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Double bookings, no-shows, last-minute cancellations, phone tag for appointments, walk-in management
**Current Solution:** Paper book, Google Calendar, basic scheduling software, phone-only booking
**Cost of Pain:** 15-20% no-show rate = $3K-$8K monthly lost revenue; double bookings hurt client relationships; front desk overwhelmed
**Your Solution:** Online booking 24/7, automated confirmations, waitlist backfill, cancellation policies with deposits
**Pricing:** $99/month scheduling module

**Discovery Questions:**
- "What is your current no-show rate?"
- "How do clients currently book appointments?"
- "How often do you have double bookings or scheduling conflicts?"

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Booth renters only (no retail, simpler needs)
- ❌ Start-up salons (<1 year, high failure rate)
- ❌ Solo operators (price sensitive, simple needs)
- ❌ Salons not using any software (change-resistant)
- ❌ Commission-only salons with no retail

**Best Prospects:**
- Established salons with 3+ stylists
- Currently using 3+ separate systems
- Focus on color/chemical services (high retail potential)
- Medspas with membership models
- Growing salons planning expansion
