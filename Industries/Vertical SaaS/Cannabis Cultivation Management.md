**Category:** Vertical SaaS

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Cannabis cultivation is a high-stakes agricultural business with strict regulatory requirements, significant capital investment, and complex operational workflows. With over 15,000 licensed cultivation facilities in the US, operators face unique challenges: Metrc seed-to-sale tracking, environmental control optimization, batch management, compliance documentation, and wholesale distribution. Most cultivators rely on a mix of spreadsheets, generic agriculture software, and manual processes that don't address the specific needs of cannabis compliance and workflow.

An all-in-one cultivation management platform—with Metrc integration, environmental monitoring, batch tracking, and harvest forecasting—creates immediate value by reducing compliance risk, optimizing yields, and providing real-time visibility into operations. The market has high willingness to pay due to facility values ($1M-$50M+) and strict compliance requirements.

**Market Size:** 15,000+ cultivation facilities in US, $400M+ TAM at target pricing  
**Target:** Indoor cultivators, greenhouse operations, craft cultivators, multi-state operators  
**Examples:** Distru, Leaf Logix, GrowFlow, Agrify, Trym

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Metrc Compliance and Batch Tracking ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Metrc tags must follow every plant, batch reconciliation errors, destruction tracking, manifest creation
**Current Solution:** Manual Metrc entry, spreadsheet tracking, end-of-day reconciliation, hoping for accuracy
**Cost of Pain:** Metrc discrepancy = $10K-$100K+ fine; pattern of errors = license suspension; manual work = 30-40 hours/week
**Your Solution:** Direct Metrc API integration, automatic tag assignment, batch lifecycle tracking, one-click manifest creation
**Pricing:** $299/month compliance module

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How many hours per week does your team spend on Metrc data entry?"
- "Have you had any Metrc discrepancies or compliance violations?"
- "How do you currently track plants from clone to harvest to sale?"

**Current Solution:** Thermometers/hygrometers, manual checks, Excel logs, walking the rooms
**Cost of Pain:** One environmental failure = $100K-$500K crop loss; suboptimal conditions = 10-20% yield loss
**Your Solution:** IoT sensor integration, real-time monitoring, automated alerts, historical trending, equipment control
**Pricing:** $199/month environmental module + hardware costs

**Discovery Questions:**
