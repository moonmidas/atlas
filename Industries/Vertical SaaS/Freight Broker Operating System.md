# Freight Broker Operating System

**Category:** Vertical SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Logistics/Transportation  
**Entry Cost:** $150K-$350K  
**Timeline:** 6-9 months to market  
**Revenue Model:** Per broker monthly subscription

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Freight brokers (17,000+ in the US) serve as critical intermediaries connecting shippers with carriers, managing $200B+ in annual freight spend. These brokers face intense operational complexity: finding and vetting carriers, managing shipment visibility, processing paperwork, handling claims, and maintaining cash flow while paying carriers quickly and collecting from shippers slowly. Most brokers rely on a mix of generic CRM, spreadsheets, load boards, and manual processes that create inefficiencies and limit scalability.

An all-in-one operating system built specifically for freight brokers—with carrier CRM, TMS functionality, quick pay, and business intelligence—can capture significant market share by providing purpose-built tools that generic software cannot match. The market is fragmented, brokers have high willingness to pay for efficiency gains, and successful brokers have predictable budgets.

**Market Size:** 17,000+ freight brokers in US, $800M+ TAM at target pricing  
**Target:** Small-to-mid brokers ($5M-$100M revenue), asset-light 3PLs, freight agents  
**Examples:** Tai Software, McLeod Software, Descartes, Echo Global Logistics (internal), Coyote (internal)

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Carrier Sourcing and Vetting ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Finding reliable carriers takes hours, vetting is manual, insurance verification spotty, fraud risk
**Current Solution:** Load boards (DAT, Truckstop), phone calls, manual insurance checks, hope for reliability
**Cost of Pain:** 2-4 hours per load sourcing = $100-$200 labor cost; bad carriers = claims, service failures; fraud losses
**Your Solution:** Integrated carrier database, automated vetting, insurance verification, performance ratings, fraud detection
**Pricing:** $199/month carrier module

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How long does it take to find and vet a carrier for a typical load?"
- "How do you currently verify carrier insurance and authority?"
- "What percentage of loads have carrier-related issues?"

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Brokers with <$1M revenue (price sensitive, simple needs)
- ❌ Brokers with recent FMCSA enforcement actions (compliance risk)
- ❌ Brokers with high claim rates or fraud issues (operational risk)
- ❌ Brokers not using any TMS (change-resistant)
- ❌ Asset-based carriers pretending to be brokers (different model)

**Best Prospects:**
- Growing brokers ($5M-$50M revenue)
- Currently using 3+ separate systems
- Cash flow constrained (quick pay opportunity)
- Planning to add sales reps or expand lanes
- Frustrated with current TMS limitations

We offer everything integrated at $699 with below-market quick pay. How much are you spending total on technology and factoring?"

**"Switching TMS is too disruptive during peak season."**

Data migration includes all your carrier and shipper history. Most brokers see efficiency gains within the first month. What concerns you most about timing?"

**"Our team is not tech-savvy."**
> "We designed our interface based on how brokers actually work, not how engineers think. Training takes 4 hours for the whole team. Plus, we provide unlimited support during onboarding. Most brokers tell us our system is simpler than what they were using. What specific training concerns do you have?"

**"Your pricing seems high compared to basic TMS."**
> "Let us compare total cost: TMS ($300) + vetting ($100) + tracking ($150) + factoring at 3% ($300) = $850 for disconnected tools. We are $699 for everything integrated, plus our 1.5% quick pay saves you another $150 monthly on factoring. That is $300 monthly savings plus better functionality."

**"We need specific features for [flatbed/reefer/hazmat]."**
> "That is exactly why we built mode-specific workflows. For flatbed, we have securement tracking and OD permits. For reefer, we have temp monitoring and washout documentation. For hazmat, we have compliance checklists and routing. Can I show you the specific features for your mode?"
