# HVAC Service Platform

**Category:** Vertical SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Home Services/HVAC  
**Entry Cost:** $100K-$250K  
**Timeline:** 4-6 months to market  
**Revenue Model:** Per truck/per month subscription

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Mid-market HVAC contractors (20-100 trucks) face unique operational challenges: they are too large for simple scheduling software but too small for expensive enterprise solutions. These contractors manage seasonal demand swings, complex maintenance agreements, inventory across trucks and warehouses, and a mix of residential service, replacement, and light commercial work. Most rely on a patchwork of systems that don't integrate, creating inefficiencies and customer experience gaps.

An all-in-one platform built specifically for HVAC—with dispatch optimization, maintenance agreement management, inventory tracking, and sales enablement—can capture significant market share by providing purpose-built functionality for HVAC workflows at a mid-market price point.

**Market Size:** 100K+ HVAC companies in US, $1.5B+ TAM at target pricing  
**Target:** Mid-market residential HVAC (20-100 trucks), multi-location operators  
**Examples:** ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, FieldPulse, Sera Systems, NexTraq

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Dispatch and Route Optimization ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Dispatchers overwhelmed, inefficient routing, emergency calls disrupt schedule, technician idle time
**Current Solution:** Whiteboard, phone calls, Google Maps, experience-based dispatching
**Cost of Pain:** 20-30% technician downtime = $1K-$2K per truck monthly; customer wait times; fuel waste
**Your Solution:** Intelligent dispatch, route optimization, real-time GPS, emergency slot management
**Pricing:** $99/month dispatch module per truck

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How do you currently dispatch technicians and route them?"
- "How much time do technicians spend driving vs. working on jobs?"
- "What happens when an emergency no-heat call comes in?"

---

### 2. Maintenance Agreement Management

**Current Solution:** Spreadsheet tracking, calendar reminders, manual scheduling, hope for renewals
**Cost of Pain:** 30-40% annual agreement churn; acquiring new agreement costs $200-$500; recurring revenue unstable
**Your Solution:** Automated agreement tracking, scheduled maintenance, renewal campaigns, membership portal
**Pricing:** $79/month maintenance module per truck

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Companies with <10 trucks (price sensitive, simple needs)
- ❌ Companies with >200 trucks (enterprise sales cycle)
- ❌ New construction only (different model)
- ❌ Companies not using any software (change-resistant)
- ❌ Solo operators (price sensitive)

**Best Prospects:**
- Growing mid-market (20-75 trucks)
- Currently using 3+ separate systems
- Maintenance agreement focus
- Multi-location or planning expansion
