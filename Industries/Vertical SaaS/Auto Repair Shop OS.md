# Auto Repair Shop OS

**Category:** Vertical SaaS  
**Sub-Category:** Automotive/Repair  
**Entry Cost:** $100K-$250K  
**Timeline:** 4-6 months to market  
**Revenue Model:** Per shop monthly subscription

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Independent auto repair shops face intense competition from dealerships, national chains (Jiffy Lube, Midas), and DIY culture. Success depends on maximizing car count, average repair order (ARO), and customer retention while managing complex workflows: diagnostics, parts ordering, customer communication, and warranty tracking. Most shops rely on legacy shop management software that hasn't evolved in 20 years, or a mix of paper, QuickBooks, and phone calls that creates inefficiencies and customer experience gaps.

An all-in-one platform built specifically for repair shops—with modern scheduling, integrated parts ordering, digital vehicle inspections, and customer communication tools—can help independent shops compete by delivering the technology and experience of a dealership at an independent price point.

**Market Size:** 230K+ repair shops in US, $1.8B+ TAM at target pricing  
**Target:** Independent repair shops (1-5 bays), specialty shops (European, diesel), fleet service providers  
**Examples:** Shop-Ware, Mitchell 1, ALLDATA, ShopBoss, AutoFluent

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

**Current Solution:** Paper calendar, whiteboard, phone-only booking, gut feel
**Cost of Pain:** 15-20% capacity underutilization = $5K-$15K monthly lost revenue; overbooking = customer frustration
**Your Solution:** Online booking, capacity management, no-show protection, waitlist, workflow optimization
**Pricing:** $99/month scheduling module

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How do you currently schedule appointments?"

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Start-up shops (<2 years, high failure rate)
- ❌ Shops with <2 bays (price sensitive, simple needs)
- ❌ Dealership service departments (different needs)
- ❌ Shops not currently using any software (change-resistant)
- ❌ Quick lube only (different model)

**Best Prospects:**
- Established shops (3+ years) with 3+ bays
- Currently using 2+ separate systems
- Focus on diagnostic and repair (not just maintenance)
- European or specialty shops
- Growing shops planning expansion
