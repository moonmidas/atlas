# 18 - Digital Products

**Category:** Education  
**Project Range:** $4K-$25K+  
**Timeline:** 2-6 months to launch  
**Business Model:** One-time purchase + upsells

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Digital Products = Templates, playbooks, and tools sold around immediate transformation. High margin (95%+), no inventory, scalable to infinity. The key is solving a specific, urgent problem.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $2K-$10K (creation, tools, initial ads) |
| Price Point | $29-$297 |
| Gross Margin | 95%+ |
| Conversion Rate | 2-5% of email list |
| Refund Rate | 5-15% |
| Annual Revenue | $50K-$500K+ |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Product:**
- Solves urgent, specific problem
- Immediate value delivery
- Professional quality

**Marketing:**
- Build audience first (email list)
- Content marketing (show, don't tell)
- Platform optimization (Gumroad, Product Hunt)

**Pricing:**
- Price for value, not hours
- Bundle for higher AOV
- Upsell to higher tiers

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Broad topic (no specific buyer)
- ❌ Low urgency (nice-to-have)
- ❌ Poor quality (bad reviews kill sales)
- ❌ No distribution plan
- ❌ Commoditized product (100+ competitors)
