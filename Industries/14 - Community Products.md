# 14 - Community Products

**Category:** Media  
**Project Range:** $4K-$30K+  
**Timeline:** 3-12 months to launch  
**Business Model:** Membership subscriptions + events

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Community Products = Paid communities organized around career leverage, identity, or insider access. Members pay for answers, accountability, and advantage. Recurring revenue, high retention, network effects.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $2K-$8K (platform, initial members) |
| Members | 100-$2,000+ |
| Price | $20-$200/month |
| Retention | 70-85% annually |
| Gross Margin | 85-95% |
| Annual Revenue | $50K-$500K+ |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Engagement:**
- Regular events (weekly/monthly)
- Accountability structures
- Member recognition

**Value:**
- Exclusive content/resources
- Direct access to experts
- Networking opportunities

**Retention:**
- Annual billing (improves retention)
- Progress tracking
- Community rituals

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Broad/general topics (no identity)
- ❌ Free communities competing
- ❌ No clear value proposition
- ❌ Creator not engaged
- ❌ High churn (>30% monthly)
