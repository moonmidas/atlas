# 02 - Vertical SaaS

**Category:** Software  
**Project Range:** $20K-$120K+  
**Timeline:** 6-12 months to MVP, 18-36 months to $1M ARR  
**Business Model:** Subscription ($200-$2,000/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Vertical SaaS = Industry-specific operating systems that replace stitched-together software stacks. Think Toast for restaurants, Shopify for e-commerce, Shopify for an entire industry. Higher ACV than Micro SaaS, more complex sales, but 10x larger outcomes.

### Why It Works
- Industries run on 5-10 disconnected tools
- Generic horizontal software misses industry nuances
- Winner-take-most dynamics (first to scale dominates)
- Network effects possible (marketplace, payments, data)

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"[Industry] deserves better software. Not horizontal tools retrofitted—software built for how you actually work."

### Validation Path
1. **Month 1-2:** Interview 100 industry professionals
2. **Month 3-4:** Build MVP with 3 design partners (free)
3. **Month 5-8:** Iterate to first paying customers ($500+/month)
4. **Month 9-12:** Scale to 20 customers, raise seed
5. **Year 2:** Scale to $1M ARR

### Go-to-Market
- **Initial:** Founder-led sales, industry conferences
- **Scale:** Inside sales team, partner channel
- **Expansion:** Land and expand (add modules, locations)

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Industries with <10,000 potential customers
- ❌ Markets where incumbent has 70%+ share
- ❌ Industries with shrinking headcount
- ❌ Buyers who view software as cost center only
- ❌ Opportunities requiring 12+ month sales cycles

---

## 💡 Success Patterns

**The Formula:**
1. Domain expertise in target industry (or partner who has it)
2. Identify 5+ tools stitched together
3. Build MVP with 3 design partners
4. Price at 50% of current tool stack cost
5. Expand through word-of-mouth in tight industry

**Examples to Study:**
- Toast (restaurants) - $3B+ valuation
- Shopify (e-commerce) - $100B+ market cap
- ServiceTitan (home services) - $9B+ valuation
- Procore (construction) - $8B+ valuation
