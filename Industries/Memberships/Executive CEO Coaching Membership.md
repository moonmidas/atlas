# Executive CEO Coaching Membership

**Category:** Expert Memberships  
**Sub-Category:** Executive CEO Coaching  
**Entry Cost:** $4K-$12K  
**Timeline:** 1-2 months  
**Revenue Model:** $800-$2,000/month membership

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Build a premium membership for CEOs and founders who need an ongoing thinking partner, structured accountability, and a confidential peer-quality support system without committing to a full bespoke coaching retainer. The offer combines 1:1 advisory calls, office hours, private resources, and practical leadership frameworks.

The strongest wedge is serving owner-operators and growth-stage CEOs in the $3M-$50M revenue range who feel isolated, overloaded, and under-supported. They already know leadership quality affects company value, team stability, and their personal health, so the willingness to pay is high when the offer feels specific and trusted.

**Market Size:** $15B+ executive coaching and founder support market  
**Target:** Founder-CEOs, presidents, operators, and second-stage business owners  
**Examples:** Vistage, CEO Coaching International, founder advisory circles

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Leadership Isolation ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** They have nobody safe to process fear, doubt, and sensitive decisions with.  
**Current Solution:** Bottle it up, vent to spouse, or use occasional therapy.  
**Cost of Pain:** Slower decisions, burnout, and avoidable mistakes worth $50K-$250K+.  
**Your Solution:** Confidential monthly coaching, voice-note support, and decision debriefs.  
**Pricing:** $1,500-$2,000/month

**Discovery Questions:**
- "Who do you talk to when you cannot be fully honest with your team or board?"
- "What major decision are you carrying alone right now?"

---

### 2. Strategic Whiplash

**Current Solution:** Reactive planning in Slack, email, and ad hoc meetings.  
**Cost of Pain:** Teams lose focus and key initiatives slip by 1-2 quarters.

**Pricing:** Included

**Discovery Questions:**

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"The CEO's real problem is rarely knowledge. It is carrying too much alone for too long."

### Authority Builders
1. Founder operating system diagnostic
2. Anonymous CEO case studies with before-and-after metrics
3. Private podcast or briefing for members
4. Board prep checklist and decision memo template

### Positioning Angles
- **Confidential strategic partner:** More practical than therapy and more personal than a mastermind.
- **Founder maturity upgrade:** Designed for leaders outgrowing founder-mode habits.
- **High-leverage prevention:** One avoided executive mistake can pay for years of membership.

### Differentiators
- Combines coaching, advisory structure, and accountability
- Built for SMB and lower mid-market CEOs, not generic corporate managers
- Focus on practical business decisions, not only mindset work

**Best Prospects:**
- $3M-$50M revenue companies
- Recently promoted or stretched founders

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"I already have a board for accountability."**
> "Boards are useful, but they are not built for weekly honesty. This membership gives you a confidential place to think clearly before the board ever sees the issue."

**"I do not have time for coaching."**
> "That usually means your current operating system is too expensive. If two focused hours per month help you avoid one bad executive decision, the time ROI is obvious."

**"This feels expensive."**
> "Compared with courses, yes. Compared with one executive mis-hire, one stalled initiative, or one quarter of poor prioritization, it is cheap."

**"I have done coaching before and it was too fluffy."**
> "That is exactly why this is structured around decisions, priorities, and leadership execution. We work on live business issues, not abstract inspiration."

**"I can figure this out myself."**
> "You probably can. The question is how much delay, stress, and collateral damage that path costs you and the company while you do."

**"My company should pay for this, not me."**
> "Many members expense it as leadership development. If helpful, I can give you a business-case memo framed around retention, execution, and decision quality."
