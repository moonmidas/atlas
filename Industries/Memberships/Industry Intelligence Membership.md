# Industry Intelligence Membership

**Category:** Expert Memberships  
**Sub-Category:** Industry Intelligence  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K  
**Timeline:** 2-3 months  
**Revenue Model:** $200-$500/month membership

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Sell a recurring intelligence product to operators, investors, consultants, and founders who need signal faster than their market peers. The offer packages research, summaries, trend analysis, and member-only briefings into an easier buy than enterprise analyst subscriptions.

This works best in niches where information quality directly affects budget decisions, product strategy, or investment timing. Buyers will pay for curation, interpretation, and context, not just raw links.

**Market Size:** $40B+ analyst, intelligence, and research market  
**Target:** Strategy leaders, product leaders, investors, consultants, and vertical operators  
**Examples:** Stratechery, CB Insights, niche policy and vertical intelligence services

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Strategy lead, product leader, independent consultant, niche investor  
**Secondary:** Founder, chief of staff, analyst team lead  
**Decision Maker:** Individual buyer or department head

**Budget:** $2.4K-$6K annually  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium-high because decisions get weaker when information is late or shallow

### The Hook
"Get the signal your market insiders act on without paying enterprise analyst prices."

1. State of the niche report
2. Weekly public teaser brief
3. Competitor tracker sample
4. Trend scorecard or dashboard

### Positioning Angles

- **Mid-market alternative:** Better fit than giant analyst firms.
- **Decision support:** Research tied directly to action, not just reading.

### Differentiators
- Narrow niche focus
- Human curation and interpretation

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Buyers wanting real-time terminal-grade data
- ❌ Buyers seeking insider or non-public information
- ❌ Prospects who never read what they already pay for
- ❌ Teams expecting unlimited custom consulting
- ❌ Niches where no decision value exists

**Best Prospects:**
- Make recurring market-sensitive decisions
- Already pay for multiple fragmented sources
- Need evidence for internal influence
- Work in fast-changing verticals

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"We can find this online for free."**
> "The raw ingredients are often public. The expensive part is the time to filter, compare, and interpret them correctly before the moment passes."

**"This is just another newsletter."**
> "A newsletter summarizes. An intelligence membership helps you make better decisions with curated evidence, structured analysis, and a usable archive."

**"We already have analyst subscriptions."**
> "That often means you have broad coverage, not niche focus. This complements large subscriptions by getting more specific and more actionable."

**"I do not know if my team will use it."**
> "That is why the strongest rollout starts with one workflow: board prep, competitor tracking, or market scans. Once one recurring use case sticks, adoption follows."

**"The price feels high."**
> "Compared with one-off content, yes. Compared with one delayed product bet or one poorly timed strategy call, it is small."

**"Can you customize research for us?"**
> "Yes within clear boundaries. The membership is built around recurring patterns, and premium tiers can include a defined monthly research queue."
