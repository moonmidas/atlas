# CEO Peer Advisory Groups

**Category:** Expert Memberships
**Sub-Category:** Executive Leadership
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$15K
**Timeline:** 1-3 months to launch
**Revenue Model:** $500-$1,500/month membership

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Facilitated peer groups for CEOs to share challenges, get feedback, and hold each other accountable. High-value, high-retention model.

**Market Size:** 100K+ SMB CEOs
**Target:** $2M-$50M revenue company CEOs
**Examples:** Vistage, EO (Entrepreneurs Organization), YPO, CEO Roundtable

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** CEO/Founder
**Secondary:** COO, President
**Demographics:** 35-55 years old, $200K+ income, decision-maker

**Budget:** $500-$1,500/month
**Pain Urgency:** High (isolation, need perspective)

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. CEO Isolation ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Can't share struggles with team/board/spouse
**Current Solution:** Bottle it up, therapy (limited)
**Cost of Pain:** Bad decisions, burnout, mental health
**Your Solution:** Confidential peer group
**Pricing:** $500-$1,500/month

**Discovery Questions:**
- "Who do you talk to when facing a major decision?"
- "Do you ever feel lonely at the top?"
- "What's your biggest challenge you can't discuss with your team?"

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"You're not alone. Join 8-12 CEOs who understand exactly what you're going through."

### Authority Builders
1. Member testimonials
2. Success stories (business growth)
3. Facilitator credentials
4. Group tenure/retention rates

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Pre-revenue founders
- ❌ Want to stay anonymous
- ❌ Not willing to share vulnerably
- ❌ Just looking for networking (not depth)

**Best Prospects:**
- $2M-$50M revenue
- 5+ years experience
- Feeling isolated
- Want accountability
