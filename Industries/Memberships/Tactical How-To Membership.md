# Tactical How-To Membership

**Category:** Expert Memberships  
**Sub-Category:** Tactical How-To  
**Entry Cost:** $1.5K-$4K  
**Timeline:** 1 month  
**Revenue Model:** $50-$150/month membership

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Create a membership that helps members execute a specific outcome with step-by-step playbooks, checklists, templates, troubleshooting help, and examples. This category performs best when buyers want speed and certainty, not more theory.

The sweet spot is a repeatable problem with a clear before-and-after result: launch a YouTube channel, run ops cleanly, ship a client workflow, or implement a specific tactic without wasting weeks on trial and error.

**Market Size:** Broad execution, operations, and implementation education market  
**Target:** Operators, freelancers, creators, small business owners, implementation-heavy teams  
**Examples:** SOP memberships, implementation clubs, specialist template libraries

---

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

**Current Solution:** Search online, ask friends, or improvise.

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"Stop googling how to do it and install the exact process that already works."

### Authority Builders
1. SOP starter pack
2. Troubleshooting cheat sheet
3. Before-and-after workflow case study
4. Implementation webinar or teardown

### Positioning Angles
- **Execution speed:** Get results faster with less trial and error.
- **Practical certainty:** Clear steps beat generic education.
- **Operational leverage:** A better process outlasts one smart person.

### Differentiators
- Built for action, not passive learning
- Immediate templates and examples
- Focus on common failure points and recovery

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Buyers wanting done-for-you services hidden inside a membership
- ❌ Prospects who refuse any standardization
- ❌ Teams without a clear use case for the templates
- ❌ Buyers seeking high strategy when they really need basic execution
- ❌ Industries where templates must be heavily customized each time

**Best Prospects:**
- Have recurring workflows
- Feel current execution pain weekly
- Value speed over theory
- Have enough team or task volume to benefit from standardization

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"I can just search for templates online."**
> "You can find fragments online. The value here is proven structure, consistency across assets, and guidance on how to actually install them."

**"This seems too tactical."**
> "That is the point. Many buyers do not need more strategy. They need fewer execution errors and faster implementation."

**"My process is too unique."**
> "Most teams are more unique in detail than in structure. Good tactical systems give you an 80 percent base you can adapt quickly."

**"We do not have time to document everything."**
> "Then start with the process that breaks most often or steals the most senior time. Documentation pays back fastest there."

**"This should be a one-time purchase, not a membership."**
> "If your workflows, tools, and people never changed, that would be true. Ongoing support matters because execution environments keep moving."

**"Will my team actually use it?"**
> "Usage rises when the first asset solves a current headache immediately. Start with one painful workflow instead of rolling out everything at once."
