# 08 - Recruiting Firms

**Category:** Service  
**Project Range:** $9K-$50K+  
**Timeline:** 30-90 day placements  
**Business Model:** Contingency (20-30% of salary) or Retainer ($5K-$15K/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Recruiting Firms = Talent matching, employer branding, and specialized hiring pipelines. High-margin when focused on hard-to-fill roles. The leverage comes from candidate databases, hiring process optimization, and retained relationships.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $5K-$15K (ATS, job boards, branding) |
| Placement Fee | 20-30% of first-year salary |
| Average Fee | $20K-$50K per placement |
| Placements/Year | 10-30 (solo) |
| Gross Margin | 60-75% |
| Annual Target | $200K-$800K |
