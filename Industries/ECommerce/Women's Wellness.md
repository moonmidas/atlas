# Women's Wellness

**Category:** Niche E-Commerce  
**Sub-Category:** Health & Wellness  
**Entry Cost:** $40K-$120K  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months to launch  
**Revenue Model:** Product sales + subscription (50-70% recurring), $40-$100/order

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Women's wellness encompasses supplements, feminine care, hormonal health, and self-care products. The market is driven by women seeking natural alternatives, personalized solutions, and discretion for intimate health concerns. High subscription rates due to recurring product needs and the desire for ongoing wellness support.

**Market Size:** $50B+ women's wellness market, growing 8-12% annually  
**Target:** Women 25-45, health-conscious, seeking natural/alternative solutions  
**Examples:** Ritual, Care/of, Lola, Hum Nutrition, The Honey Pot Company

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** Women 25-45, middle to upper-middle class  
**Secondary:** Healthcare practitioners, wellness coaches  
**Decision Maker:** Self-directed, influenced by peer recommendations

**Budget:** $40-$100/month on wellness products  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium-High (hormonal issues, energy, intimate health)

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"I've tried supplements before and didn't notice a difference."**
> "That's frustrating and common. Most supplements use generic doses and low-quality ingredients that your body can't absorb. We use bioavailable forms at therapeutic doses, and our customers typically notice changes within 2-4 weeks. Plus, we have a 30-day satisfaction guarantee because we stand behind the quality."

**"Your products are more expensive than what I buy at the store."**
> "I understand. When you add up the separate vitamins, feminine care, and beauty supplements most women buy, it often exceeds our all-in-one subscription. Plus, our ingredients are third-party tested for purity—you'd be surprised what's in some store brands. The real value is in results, not just pills."

**"I'm worried about taking too many supplements."**
> "That's a smart concern. Our formulations are based on what women commonly need, and we provide clear guidance on timing and combinations. Everything is at safe, effective doses—not the mega-doses that cause issues. Many customers actually reduce their total pill count by switching to our targeted formulas."

**"How do I know which products I actually need?"**
> "Great question. We start with a comprehensive health assessment that takes 5 minutes. Based on your symptoms, goals, and lifestyle, we recommend a personalized protocol. No guesswork, no waste—just what your body needs. You can always adjust as your needs change."

**"Is this safe to take with my medications/birth control?"**
> "Safety is our priority. Our products are designed to complement, not interfere with, common medications. However, we always recommend checking with your healthcare provider, especially if you're on prescription medications. We provide full ingredient lists and can answer specific interaction questions."

**"Do I really need a subscription, or can I just buy once?"**
> "Supplements work cumulatively—you need consistent use for 2-3 months to see full benefits. The subscription ensures you never run out and lose momentum. That said, you can pause, skip, or cancel anytime. Most women choose to subscribe because they feel the difference and don't want to go back."
