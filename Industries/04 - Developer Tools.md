# 04 - Developer Tools

**Category:** Software  
**Project Range:** $15K-$90K+  
**Timeline:** 4-12 months to MVP  
**Business Model:** Subscription + usage-based

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Developer Tools = Products for engineering friction, observability, and deployment pain. Developers have budget authority, technical sophistication, and viral growth potential.

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $30K-$100K (MVP, 6 months runway) |
| Target Pricing | $10-$500/month per seat |
| Gross Margin | 80-90% |
| Sales Cycle | Self-serve to 3 months |
| Payback Period | 12-24 months |

## 🎯 Success Factors

**Product:**
- CLI-first or API-first
- Great DX (developer experience)
- Fast time-to-value
- Freemium for adoption

**Go-to-Market:**
- Bottom-up (developers choose)
- PLG (product-led growth)
- Community building
- Documentation excellence

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Requires behavior change
- ❌ Competing with free alternatives
- ❌ Low switching costs
- ❌ Enterprise sales required (no self-serve)
- ❌ No clear pricing metric
