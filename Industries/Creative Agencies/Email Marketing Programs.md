# Email Marketing Programs

**Category:** Creative Agencies  
**Sub-Category:** Email Marketing  
**Entry Cost:** $4K-$10K  
**Timeline:** Ongoing monthly  
**Revenue Model:** Monthly retainer ($8K-$18K/month) + Revenue share option

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Email marketing delivers $36 for every $1 spent—but most B2B companies treat it as an afterthought. Newsletters are sporadic, sequences are basic, and personalization is nonexistent. This full-service email program provides strategy, copy, design, automation, and optimization—turning email lists into predictable revenue engines.

Unlike email tools that just provide software, this is done-for-you execution with strategic guidance, creative production, and performance accountability.

**Market Size:** $12B email marketing industry (B2B service segment growing 15% annually)  
**Target:** B2B SaaS and services companies with 10K+ email subscribers  
**Examples:** Demand Curve, Mutiny, Litmus, Really Good Emails

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"We can do email internally."**
> "You can—and many do. But email is complex: strategy, copy, design, automation, deliverability, testing. It takes a full team to do well. Most internal teams are stretched thin. We bring specialized expertise and guaranteed output. How much is your team currently spending on email?"

**"Email is dead."**
> "Actually, email has the highest ROI of any channel—$36 for every $1 spent. What's dead is bad email. If your email program isn't working, it's an execution problem, not a channel problem. What's your current email ROI?"

**"How is this different from our ESP?"**
> "Your ESP (Mailchimp, HubSpot, etc.) is a tool. We're the execution. It's like the difference between having a kitchen and having a chef. We use your tools to create, send, and optimize. What gaps do you have that your ESP isn't filling?"

**"What if we don't like the emails?"**
> "You're involved throughout. We start with strategy alignment and editorial calendar. You approve content themes, review drafts, and provide feedback. We iterate until you're satisfied. How involved do you want to be in email creation?"

**"How do we measure ROI?"**
> "We track everything: engagement metrics, lead generation, pipeline influence, and revenue attribution. We set goals together in month 1 and report monthly. Most clients see measurable improvement in 60-90 days. What metrics matter most to you?"

**"What about deliverability?"**
> "Deliverability is foundational. We monitor sender reputation, manage list hygiene, optimize for inbox placement, and maintain best practices. We also handle authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC). What's your current deliverability rate?"
