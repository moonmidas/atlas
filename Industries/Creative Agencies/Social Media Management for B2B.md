# Social Media Management for B2B

**Category:** Creative Agencies  
**Sub-Category:** Social Media  
**Entry Cost:** $3K-$8K  
**Timeline:** Ongoing monthly  
**Revenue Model:** Monthly retainer ($5K-$12K/month per executive)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

B2B executives know they need social presence but lack time, strategy, and content. This service provides executive social media management: strategy, ghostwriting, content creation, and engagement—turning LinkedIn from a resume repository into a business development and thought leadership engine.

Unlike general social media agencies that focus on brand accounts, this specializes in executive presence—authentic personal brands that build relationships and drive pipeline.

**Market Size:** $25B social media management market (B2B executive segment growing 35% annually)  
**Target:** B2B executives (Founders, C-suite, VPs) at companies with $10M+ ARR  
**Examples:** Everyone Hates Marketers, Foundation, Gallup, Gong's Udi Ledergor model

---

**Primary Buyer:** Executive (CEO, CMO, VP Sales)

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. No Time for Social Media ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Executives know social presence matters but can't find 30 minutes daily. Strategy exists only in their head.
**Current Solution:** Occasional posts, sporadic activity, feeling guilty about neglect
**Cost of Pain:** Missed opportunities, competitors owning the conversation, network stagnation
**Your Solution:** Complete management—strategy, content, scheduling, engagement
**Pricing:** $5K-$12K/month per executive

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How much time do you currently spend on LinkedIn per week?"
- "What stops you from being more active?"
- "What opportunities do you think you've missed from not being active?"

---

### 2. Don't Know What to Post
**Pain:** Unsure what content works. Afraid of posting "wrong" thing. Analysis paralysis.
**Current Solution:** Lurking, occasional shares, safe corporate content
**Cost of Pain:** Boring presence, no engagement, fear-based inaction
**Your Solution:** Content strategy with proven formats and editorial calendar
**Pricing:** Included in service

---

### 3. Inauthentic or Corporate Voice
**Pain:** Posts sound like marketing wrote them. No personality. Engagement is minimal.
**Current Solution:** Marketing team drafts, legal review, sanitized corporate speak
**Cost of Pain:** Low engagement, inauthentic perception, wasted effort
**Your Solution:** Ghostwriting that captures authentic voice and expertise
**Pricing:** Included in service

---

### 4. No Engagement Strategy
**Pain:** Posts go into void. No comments, no DMs, no relationships built. One-way broadcasting.
**Current Solution:** Posting and hoping, occasional comment replies
**Cost of Pain:** Network doesn't grow, relationships don't deepen, opportunities missed
**Your Solution:** Active engagement strategy with targeted relationship building
**Pricing:** Included in service

### 5. No Connection to Business Goals
**Pain:** Social feels like vanity metric exercise. No clear ROI. Can't justify time.
**Current Solution:** "Brand awareness," hoping for the best, no measurement
**Cost of Pain:** Investment without business impact, deprioritization, abandonment
**Your Solution:** Strategy tied to pipeline, recruiting, or thought leadership goals
**Pricing:** Included in service

**Discovery Questions:**
- "What do you want to achieve with your social presence?"
- "How do you measure success on LinkedIn currently?"
- "What business impact would an active presence have?"

**Pricing:** Included in service

## 🚨 Red Flags

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Executives who want to approve every word (defeats purpose)
- ❌ No clear business goal for social presence
- ❌ Companies with compliance restrictions prohibiting personal posts
- ❌ Expecting overnight viral success
- ❌ Executives uncomfortable sharing opinions

**Best Prospects:**
- $10M+ ARR company
- Active in B2B sales or partnerships
- Strong opinions and expertise to share
- Willing to delegate content creation
- 12+ month commitment to building presence

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"I should manage my own LinkedIn."**
> "You absolutely should—and many do. But let's be honest: you're not. You have a company to run. This service lets you show up consistently without the time investment. You provide the expertise and approval; we handle the execution. How much is your time worth per hour?"

**"What if the content doesn't sound like me?"**
> "That's our #1 priority. We start with voice capture—interviews, content review, tone analysis. You approve sample posts before we go live. We iterate until it sounds like you. Most executives say our posts sound more like them than their own attempts. How particular are you about your voice?"

**"How do we measure success?"**
> "We track leading indicators (followers, engagement, reach) and business outcomes (inbound opportunities, speaking invites, recruiting pipeline). We set goals together in month 1 and report monthly. What success metrics matter most to you?"

**"What about controversial topics?"**
> "We establish guardrails upfront—topics to avoid, tone boundaries, approval requirements. Most executives have more freedom than they think. We help you find the line between safe and boring. What are your no-go zones?"

**"This is expensive for social media."**
> "It is—compared to managing it yourself (but you're not). Let's look at the value. One quality inbound opportunity from LinkedIn could be worth $50K-$500K. One great hire saves $30K in recruiting fees. Thought leadership opens speaking and partnership doors. What's the value of one strong relationship?"

**"What if I want to post something myself?"**
> "Please do! This is a partnership, not a takeover. Post when inspired, we'll handle the consistency. The best results come from collaboration—you bring spontaneous insights, we bring strategic consistency. How hands-on do you want to be?"
