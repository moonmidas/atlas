# Content Studio for B2B

**Category:** Creative Agencies  
**Sub-Category:** Content Marketing  
**Entry Cost:** $5K-$10K  
**Timeline:** Ongoing monthly  
**Revenue Model:** Monthly retainer ($8K-$20K/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

B2B companies know content marketing works but can't execute consistently. They lack the strategy, production capacity, and creative talent to produce high-quality content at scale. This subscription service provides end-to-end content production: strategy, writing, design, and distribution—turning content from a sporadic effort into a reliable growth engine.

Unlike hiring freelancers or building an internal team, this offers predictable costs, consistent quality, and strategic oversight without the management overhead.

**Market Size:** $50B content marketing industry (B2B segment growing 16% annually)  
**Target:** B2B SaaS, professional services, and tech companies with $10M+ ARR  
**Examples:** Animalz, Foundation, Revenue Hero, Beam Content

---

## 👤 Buyer Profile

**Primary Buyer:** VP of Marketing / Content Director  
**Secondary:** CMO, Head of Demand Gen  
**Decision Maker:** VP Marketing or CMO

**Budget:** $8K-$25K/month for content  
**Pain Urgency:** Medium-High - impacts long-term pipeline but shows results in 3-6 months

## 🔴 Top 15 Pain Points

### 1. Inconsistent Content Production ⭐ #1 PAIN
**Pain:** Blog posts published sporadically, social media goes dark for weeks, content calendar always behind. Momentum never builds.
**Current Solution:** Freelance writers, occasional agency projects, internal team scrambling
**Cost of Pain:** Lost SEO momentum, audience forgets about brand, competitors dominate search, 40-60% of content investment wasted on stop-start efforts
**Your Solution:** Predictable content production machine with guaranteed monthly deliverables
**Pricing:** $8K-$20K/month based on volume

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How often are you currently publishing content?"
- "When was the last time you stuck to a content calendar for more than 2 months?"
- "How much content did you plan to produce last quarter vs. what actually got published?"

---

### 2. Content Strategy Vacuum
**Pain:** Content created without strategy—random topics, no buyer journey alignment, no keyword research. Content for content's sake.
**Current Solution:** Quarterly planning sessions, content calendars that get abandoned, copying competitors
**Cost of Pain:** 60-70% of content gets no traffic or engagement, $100K-$300K annually on ineffective content
**Your Solution:** Research-backed content strategy aligned with business goals and buyer journey
**Pricing:** Included in retainer

**Discovery Questions:**
- "What's driving your current content topics?"
- "How do you decide what content to create next?"
- "What percentage of your content drives actual business results?"

---

### 3. Can't Find Quality Writers
**Pain:** Good B2B writers are unicorns. Generalist writers don't understand the industry. Subject matter experts can't write.
**Current Solution:** Content mills, Upwork roulette, expensive specialized freelancers
**Cost of Pain:** 20-40 hours/month managing writers, content quality inconsistent, editorial burden on internal team
**Your Solution:** Vetted B2B specialist writers with industry expertise + editorial oversight
**Pricing:** $8K-$20K/month

**Discovery Questions:**
- "How many writers have you tried in the last year?"
- "How much time do you spend editing and rewriting content?"
- "What makes a writer 'good' for your industry specifically?"

**Your Solution:** Integrated design production with 48-hour turnaround on creative assets
**Pricing:** Included in retainer

## 💬 Objection Handling

**"We can hire freelancers for cheaper."**
> "You absolutely can. Most of our clients tried that first. The challenge is management—finding good writers, editing their work, managing the calendar, handling design. You're effectively becoming a content agency. Our model gives you the output without the overhead. How many hours per week do you currently spend managing content?"

**"How do we know the content will be good?"**
> "Three things: First, you approve our strategy before we write a word. Second, you get to review and request revisions on everything. Third, we measure quality by results—traffic, engagement, and leads. If content isn't performing, we adjust. Can I show you samples from companies similar to yours?"

**"What if we need to pause or cancel?"**
> "Our agreements are month-to-month after an initial 3-month commitment. We need 3 months because content marketing takes time to show results. After that, you're free to pause or cancel with 30 days notice. We don't believe in locking clients into long contracts if we're not delivering value. What concerns do you have about commitment?"

**"This is more than we budgeted for content."**
> "Let's look at what you're spending now. [Calculate their current costs] Most companies underestimate their true content costs when you include management time, tools, and inefficiency. Plus, we guarantee deliverables. When was the last time you calculated the full cost of your current approach?"

**"How is this different from [Agency X]?"**
> "Most agencies are either strategy-only (no execution) or production shops (no strategy). We do both—and we specialize in B2B. We also guarantee output. Many agencies bill by the hour and projects expand. We deliver specific assets every month regardless of hours. What matters most to you in a content partner?"

**"We want to see ROI before committing."**
> "Completely reasonable. That's why we start with a Content Audit—free, no commitment. We'll analyze your current content, identify gaps, and show you specific opportunities. You'll get value even if we don't work together. Then, if we move forward, we track metrics that matter to your business. What metrics would prove ROI to your leadership?"

## 🚀 Validation Path

**Month 1:** Interview 15-20 VP Marketing about content challenges. Document patterns. Create Content Audit framework.

**Month 3:** Launch with 2 beta clients at $5K-$6K/month. Deliver for 2 months. Gather testimonials and case studies.

**Month 4-6:** Full launch at $8K-$15K. Target 5-8 clients. Refine processes and writer management.

**Month 6-12:** Scale to 15-25 clients. Add service tiers (basic, multimedia, executive). Build client success team.
