# 05 - Creative Agencies

**Category:** Service  
**Project Range:** $8K-$60K+  
**Timeline:** 2-8 weeks per engagement  
**Business Model:** Project-based retainers ($5K-$25K/month)

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Creative Agencies = High-margin advisory and execution wrapped around positioning and delivery. Unlike traditional agencies that sell hours, modern creative agencies sell outcomes: brand repositioning, conversion optimization, content systems. The leverage comes from productized services and retainer relationships.

### Why It Works
- Execution bottlenecks at growing companies
- In-house teams are expensive and slow to hire
- Creative talent wants flexibility (agency model attracts top talent)
- Outcome-based pricing captures more value than hourly

---

## 💰 Economics

| Metric | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Entry Investment | $5K-$15K (brand, portfolio, basic tools) |
| Monthly Overhead | $3K-$12K (co-working, contractor payments) |
| Target Project | $15K-$75K |
| Target Retainer | $8K-$30K/month |
| Gross Margin | 50-70% |
| Utilization Target | 60-70% (not 100%—that's burnout) |

**Valuation:** 1-2x annual revenue (service multiple)

## 🎯 Positioning Strategy

### The Hook
"We don't sell hours. We sell [outcome] in [timeframe] for [price]."

### Validation Path
1. **Month 1:** Build portfolio with 3 case studies (can be free/discounted)
2. **Month 2-3:** Launch with productized service offering
3. **Month 4-6:** Land 3-5 retainer clients
4. **Month 6-12:** Scale to $30K-$50K/month

### Pricing Strategy
- **Productized:** Fixed scope, fixed price, fixed timeline
- **Retainer:** Monthly fee for ongoing work
- **Value-based:** Price based on outcome, not hours
- **Revenue share:** For performance-driven work (CRO, email)

---

## 🚨 Red Flags

- ❌ Clients who want "just a logo" (commodity work)
- ❌ Clients who shop on price alone
- ❌ Clients without decision-making authority
- ❌ Industries with shrinking marketing budgets
- ❌ Projects with unclear success metrics

---

## 💡 Success Patterns

**The Formula:**
1. Pick one specific outcome (not "creative services")
2. Productize the delivery (fixed scope, fixed timeline)
3. Build case studies in one niche
4. Price for value, not hours
5. Layer on retainers for recurring revenue

**Examples to Study:**
