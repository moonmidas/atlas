# Business Opportunity Atlas

An Obsidian-native research vault for people who like chasing high-upside business ideas.

This repository is built to help you explore markets, business models, and sub-niches that are worth studying more deeply. Instead of a flat list of ideas, it gives you a linked map: top-level opportunity overviews, detailed niche notes, and deeper rabbit holes across software, services, media, commerce, education, local businesses, and asset-light plays.

## What You'll Find Inside

- 25 top-level overview notes covering major business model categories
- Hundreds of linked markdown notes across industries and sub-opportunities
- Research angles on pricing, margins, positioning, validation, and business model fit
- An Obsidian-friendly structure designed for browsing, backlinking, and idea compounding

## Why This Vault Is Worth Exploring

Open almost any overview and you'll find more than a niche name.

You'll run into concrete opportunity framing, linked sub-markets, directional economics, positioning angles, and validation ideas that make it easier to spot where a business could get interesting. Some notes point toward buildable software. Others lean toward service businesses, local rollups, media plays, or niche commerce. The point is range with structure, not random inspiration.

If you're looking for a place to spark a new product, acquisition thesis, research sprint, or market rabbit hole, start here.

## Best Way To Explore

1. Open [Start Here](Start%20Here.md).
2. Pick the category that matches your interests or unfair advantage.
3. Open an overview note and follow the linked sub-opportunities.
4. Use `Industries/` to browse the full library directly.

1. Open Obsidian.

## GitHub And Obsidian Notes

- The repo is structured as an Obsidian-style knowledge base.
- GitHub does not make Obsidian double-bracket links clickable in normal repository markdown.
- If you're browsing on GitHub, start from `README.md`, then move into `Start Here.md`, then into the overview notes.
- If you're browsing in Obsidian, backlinks and note-to-note traversal make the vault much more powerful.

## Repo Layout

- `Start Here.md`: top-level launchpad for the main industry overviews
- `README.md`: repo overview, setup, and browsing guidance
- `Industries/`: overview notes plus detailed industry folders

## Working With The Research

- Treat pricing, margins, and timelines as directional research, not guarantees.
- Validate demand with real customers before building, acquiring, or investing.
- Update notes in place as markets shift and your thinking gets sharper.
